Professor Winsome Landy (nicknamed 'Winnie' by [[Seid]]) is a halfling scholar who the party met in [[The Horror at Havel's Cross]]. She is an archaeologist, who was studying a cult with a history of necromancy. She has the stats of a commoner.
She is incredibly timid, and not battle ready at all. During the session she performs several acts of necromancy to save the party, but is incredibly ashamed of this and will not do that again, nor speak of it.
