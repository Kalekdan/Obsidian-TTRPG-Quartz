###  Summary 

The players received a letter from [[Professor Winsome Landy]] inviting them to assist her and her colleagues in escorting an artefact from a dig site, back to civilisation to be studied. They meet Landy at [[The Spring Shoot]] (an inn in [[Ardsey]]) where she gives them more details. The artefact has been found at a dig site whilst investigating an ancient cult, who have potential ties to necromanctic practices. Her colleagues (including elves [[Calador]] and Skein, a human Tanlik, a gnome called Quinn, and a dwarven bodyguard, Graz Shatterbone) are at the dig site currently and the quest is to head to the site and retrieve the artefact.

Upon arriving at [[Havel's Cross]] where the archaeologists were staying, they find a dead horse and lots of dead humanoid bodies. 4 goblins and a hobgoblin ([[Krax]]) are looting the bodies when the player arrives. After a short fight, they admit that they did not cause this and do not know what did. The goblins all run away. After an investigation, the players conclude that this was the result of ghouls attacking. They follow a blood trail to the dig site.

At the dig site there is a temple, with all the lights off (highly unusual). The party investigates and finds 3 ghouls inside and a fight ensues. [[Stuart the Little | Stuart]] and [[Bralvian Silverstone | Bralvian]] are killed during the fight, but [[Professor Winsome Landy | Landy]] shamefully uses the knowledge she has picked up from studying the necromancers to resurrect them. They are now undead. Landy confirms the ghouls were once her colleagues, Quinn, Skein and Tanlik.

After further investigations, a secret entrance is discovered to a chamber beneath the church. The party descends and finds an altar with a humanoid torn apart on. There are lots more bodies down here, including the bodyguard Shatterbone, with his head caved in. [[Calador]] is also hunched behind the altar, and claims he was attacked and hid here to protect himself, and killed Shatterbone when he went mad and tried to attack him. The party soon realise this was a lie and fight [[Calador]]. He is an undead spellcaster who has been using necromancy. He resurrects a ghoul to join the fight.

The party manage to kill [[Calador]] but the bone in his hand ([[The Bone of Doresain]]) glows and he comes back to life. The party attempt to kill him again and the same occurs. [[Bralvian Silverstone | Bralvian]] grapples the bone from [[Calador]] and kills him again, but he comes back once more. [[Bralvian Silverstone | Bralvian]] then destroys the bone by jumping on it. The whole party then attack [[Calador]], including his colleague, [[Professor Winsome Landy]], and manage to kill him, not before he manages to kill [[Seid]]. 

Despite [[Seid]]'s incessant teasing of [[Professor Winsome Landy | Landy]] throughout the adventure, she agrees to use the remains of the bone to bring him back. She is ashamed and swears never to perform necromancy again, nor ever talk of it. [[Seid]] is now also undead.

The whole party increase from Lvl 1, to Lvl 2.
