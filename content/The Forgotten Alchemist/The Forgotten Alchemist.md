####  Additional mechanics 
* Potion ingredients only deliver effect when mixed in a drink (See Obojima for decent potion brewing rules)
* Can get what an ingredient does by studying it for an hour (does not consume)
* Can get what an ingredient does by consuming it (does consume, but does not deliver effect)
* If they mix a 'known' potion (i.e. a cocktail I've defined), it refreshes Dosius memory, and they will know what each ingredient does

####  Introduction 
The Forgotten Alchemist is a potion focus D&D campaign. It relies on real ingredients which the players will have to taste and mix their own cocktail/potions in order to succeed.

The players are informed of a potion of immortality which Dosius is brewing. When they get to him he asks for them to pass him something, drinks it and his memory is wiped (mislabelled bottle). Turns out there is a rival (Kyrath) potion maker who didn't want to be bested.
