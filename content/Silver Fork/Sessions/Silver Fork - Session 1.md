The party start off in The Rusty Spur, a bar in a mining town called Silver Fork. They each have a 'Help Wanted' poster in their pockets, directing them to seek out employment with Langstone and Son Industry, in a few hours time, where they can expect a handsome payment.

#### In The Rusty Spur
**At the bar**:
- Marty Shwartz (the bartender)
- ***Cormac O'Rielly***
**Poker Table**:
- Young handsome man - waistcoat, well put together (Calvin Klien)
- Older silver fox, looking pensive (Henry Buchannan) - the dealer
- ***Cleetus McGee***
- ***Bartholemew 'Barty' Winthrop***
- Young boy - milky bar kid
**In the corner:**
- ***"Wire"/"Howling Wolf"***
**At a second table:**
- Sean Striklin
- ***Vincent Kane***

The poker table has a game of cards, in which **Barty** successfully spies **Cleetus'** good hand and successfully folds. **Cleetus** goes all in.

Meanwhile, at the second table, Sean Striklin and **Vincent Kane** are challenging each other to an arm wrestle which Vincent wins. Sean skulks off to the bar, where **Cormac** riles him up to the point that he returns and tries to start a fight with Vincent. The bar kicks off into a large fight.

Before Cleetus is able to claim his winnings, the fight kicks off. Barty declares his bet on Vincent to win the fight, and throws him an unmarked potion which he quickly drinks. Cleetus disappears in a puff of smoke. The fight is brief, but Vincent wins, and leaves Sean unconscious on the floor.

Shortly after, 3 men walk into the bar, commanding respect and fear in equal measure. They are established to be 'the Murphys', a group of dangerous criminals:
- Blake Murphy
- Michael McGreggor
- Connor McGreggor
Murphy also has a help wanted poster from Langstone, who he explains lives at the end of the street. He asks that the party come see him tomorrow evening after having spoken with Langston. His camp is a couple of miles north of town.


#### Out in town
Once out of the bar fight, the group wonder town for a bit, briefly encountering a shaken man, by the name of 'Jack Johnson' on the northern side. He had been mugged by the Murphys.
The party head toward Langston's property, and come across a group of miners, 

A man (Noel Edmunds) is preaching to the group:

> 'Wheels of progress are lubricated, by the blood of innocents. The Langston Family don't give a shit about us - if the mine changes hands then a new family comes in, eat our food, have sex with our women'
> 
> 'And everyone knows the reason we aren't getting paid is half the money is going out the back door to the Murphys'

Apparently Langston owns the silver mines in the town, where everyone is employed. Noel tells the party of a union meeting he is holding tomorrow evening and invites the party to join. "Miners Workers Union - 'Giving the mine back to the miners'"

The party carry on to speak with Langston about the work. They are greeted by Delia at the reception who offers them all a drink and makes them wait for an hour or so for Langston. They head through and he tells them that the silver mine is nearly tapped, and he is looking to sell. He is organising an auction for 3 days time, and wants the party to help make sure it all goes smoothly. They will receive 500GP each if they complete this successfully.

The National Prospecting Committee (NPCs) are coming in a weeks time to officiate the deed to the mine - so the correct person needs to be holding it at that time.

The party asked about viewing the mine, and were dismissed (somewhat suspiciously). The deed will stay locked in the safe until the auction which is to be held in the town hall, under heavy security (no weapons etc.). The winner will be escorted back to their transport from which point the job is complete. There are a few prospective bidders, who the party are free to vet on their arrival.

Langston gives the party a map with directions to their hotel. (Half way up the hill is an abandoned part of the mine, and the hotel nearby. At the top of the hill is the active mine entrance).

As the party head towards their hotel, a group of thugs ambush them. Vincent attempts to close the distance, but the thugs pull weapons and shout 'Give us all your money!'.
