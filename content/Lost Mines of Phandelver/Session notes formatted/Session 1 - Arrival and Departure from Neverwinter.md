### **Grommar**

Grommar is currently working at the Stone Masons’ Guild in Neverwinter, where his friend **Gundren Rockseeker** is also employed. One day, **Gilbert Groundworm**, the guild clerk, gives Grommar a new assignment: helping to escort a wagon of supplies to the town of **Phandalin**. The job was arranged by Grommar’s father, **Grom Pebblejaw**, on Gundren’s behalf.

The delivery is scheduled to depart from the **south gate tomorrow morning**, and while escorts are still being arranged, the destination is clear: **Barthen’s Provisions** in Phandalin.

---
### **Solanar**

Meanwhile, in a poorer district of Neverwinter, **Solanar** is working or volunteering at a local shelter or soup kitchen. Unexpectedly, an older dwarf enters—**Grom Pebblejaw**, who turns out to be Grommar’s father. He quietly asks Solanar to **accompany Grommar to Phandalin** and keep him out of trouble along the way.

---
### **Zephira**

That same night, **Zephira** is seen breaking into the upper windows of the Masons’ Guild. She moves through the building with precision and confidence, navigating its corridors as if she knows it intimately. She’s after something—and she finds it in the guild archives.
While scanning documents, one particular page catches her attention. It references the **supply wagon to Phandalin**, much like the others, listing materials and timelines. But unlike the others, this one includes an unusual detail: **“Also a large stone structure.”** This raises Zephira’s suspicion, and she begins to worry that the guild might be building something dangerous—something that could threaten the Underdark.

---
### **Bimblee Tumblepot**

A few days prior, **Bimblee** began to notice strange patterns in nature—beetles walking in circles, moss reacting sluggishly, and animals behaving nervously. Her bird companion, **Peep**, confirms that the local wildlife around Neverwinter is restless.
Recognising these as signs of imbalance, Bimblee decides to act. Hearing of a group called **The Harpers**, an organisation dedicated to maintaining balance between civilisation and nature, she packs up and heads toward Phandalin to investigate further.

---
## **Meeting at the Wagon**

The group gathers at the south gate. Zephira notices Grommar ticking off an item on his inventory list with a smirk—**“large stone structure.”** Suspicion grows.
They learn that **Gundren Rockseeker** has already gone ahead with a warrior escort named **Sildar Hallwinter**. The party’s job is to deliver the wagon to **Barthen’s Provisions**, where someone will be waiting to pay them upon arrival.

---
## **On the Road to Phandalin**

Roughly half a day into their journey along the **Tribal Trail**, the party arrives at a crossroads and comes upon the aftermath of a **recent battle**. Two horses by the roadside with overturned carriages and signs of a struggle.
Before they can investigate further, they are **ambushed by goblins**. The attack feels organized and deliberate, suggesting these goblins are part of a larger group or force.

---
## **Arrival in Phandalin**

Eventually, they arrive safely in **Phandalin**, a small frontier town with **40–50 log buildings** and the ruins of what was once a much larger settlement.
They park the wagon near an inn. While the others stay with the wagon, **Solanar** heads inside. The barkeep at the inn suggests that **Elmina** may have mentioned **Gundren Rockseeker**. She also runs the **provisions shop next door**—**Barthen’s Provisions**.

---
### **Inside Barthen’s Provisions**

Elmina confirms she knows Gundren but hasn’t seen him arrive yet, which worries her. She suggests they may need to **go back and find him,** perhaps where they encountered the goblins. Solanar buys a dagger to replace the one Zephyra lost in the fight with the goblins..
While Sol and Grommar are in the shop, Zephira inspects the delivery and notes that the **stone shipment** is particularly fine. Meanwhile, Bimblee chats with a dog on the green, **Pfuk**.
Grommar asks Elmina if he can **store something at the shop** while they're away. Zephira, curious, sneaks in and spies on him. Grommar claims it’s **“just some stoneware for a personal project,”** and bribes Zephira with one gold to keep quiet.

---
## **Next Steps**

The party decides to **spend the night at the inn**, and in the morning, **return to the scene of the goblin ambush** to search for Gundren and Sildar, who may have been captured or worse.
