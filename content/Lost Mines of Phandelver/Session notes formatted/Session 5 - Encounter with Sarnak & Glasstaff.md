
The party explores deeper into the hideout, uncovering hidden passages and encountering Sarnak, a mind-reading **Nothic** guarding the cavern.

---
#### Key Events
- **Exploring the Skeleton Room**
    - Zephira notices a suspicious stone, which opens a **secret door** to a storage room with crates and barrels.
    - One crate contains **40 beaver pelts (2 GP each)** – to collect later.
- **Sarnak’s Offer**
    - Sarnak speaks telepathically, warning about “the sinister drow” and offering help in exchange for food.
    - The party feeds him Redbrand heads.
    - Reveals he has a stash with **two skeletons**, one wearing a **wooden amulet** (goblin-like with elongated noses).
    - Indicates a second hidden escape route for Glasstaff.
- **Wizard’s Workshop**
    - Sol finds **Urmon’s journal**, mentioning Wave Echo Cave.
    - Collects alchemical ingredients and poisons.
    - Letter with a **spider-seal** discovered.
- **Glasstaff Confrontation**
    - Glasstaff claims his actions are “for the good of Phandalin” against threats like dragons and zombies.
    - Party traps him, but he escapes via **Misty Step**, leading to combat.
    - Admits:
        - The Spider is just a paid associate.
        - Bugbears know more about the Spider’s location and Cragmaw Castle.
---
#### Leads & Hooks
- Speak to **bugbears** to learn more about the Spider and Cragmaw Castle.
- Investigate the **wooden amulet** from Sarnak’s stash.
