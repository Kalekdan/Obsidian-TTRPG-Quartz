### At the Stonehill Inn
- The Redbrands were reportedly looking for the party again.
- Toblen mentions they usually hang around _The Sleeping Giant_, a tavern down the road.
- The party asks Toblen if he knows where Cragmaw Castle is—he suggests **Qaleen Alderleaf**, who might know a druid familiar with the forests where the castle is thought to be.
### Encounter at _The Sleeping Giant_ (Ramshackle Tavern, East End of Town)
- The party enters and spots about four humans inside—Redbrands.
- A fight breaks out. "Tangle" appears to be the one in charge.
- After the fight, Tangle is restrained and interrogated.
#### Interrogation Details:
- **Leader**: Glasstaff, a wizard who runs the Redbrand hideout up the hill.
- Glasstaff gives orders to bully townsfolk and intimidate adventurers.
- Hideout contains:
    - Skeleton guardians
    - Prisoners
- Tangle suggests:
    - Disguising themselves in red cloaks might help.
    - There are likely 6–7 more Redbrands in the hideout.
- Glasstaff receives orders from **someone known only as "The Spider"**.
- Bimblee asks about the Red Crow, but Tangle doesn't know anything.
- They take his 'whip', silk snare
### Strange Sighting
- Bimblee and Sol feel a moment of warmth—some kind of positive presence.
- Bimblee spots a **red-and-gold pseudodragon** perched on a rooftop. It flies off quickly.
### Meeting with the Townmaster – Harbin Wester
- A well-dressed man appears, alarmed: “What have you done—what is this?”
- Concerned that the Redbrands will retaliate.
- After some back-and-forth with Zephira, Harbin agrees to:
    - Pay **175 gold** for eliminating the Redbrands entirely.
    - **Bonus objective**: Rescue a missing mother and her children.
    - Potential extra **5 gold** for helping with another matter.
    - He believes the Redbrands’ hideout is in the **abandoned manor**.
- To Grommar (privately): “I believe we have business. Come by the town hall and we can discuss.”

### Infiltrating the Abandoned Manor
- The group finds **footprints** and a **cellar door** hidden behind rubble.
- **Bimblee gives Zephira a whistle**—if she runs into trouble, she’s to blow it for backup.
- **Zephira enters the hatch alone.**
- Once confirmed to be empty, the rest of the party follow
#### Inside the Hideout:
- **First Room**: Empty.
    - 5 ft wide landing, stone steps descending to a cellar.        
- **Straight Ahead**: Redbrands in the next room.
- **Room Above**:
    - Appears empty but contains a **pit trap**.
    - They avoid the trap and reach a locked door.
    - Zephira unlocks it.
- **Beyond the Door**:
    - Room with **two skeletons** and a **sarcophagus**.
    - The confidently walk in, and the skeletons seem to ignore them, in part due to the red cloaks the party are all wearing
