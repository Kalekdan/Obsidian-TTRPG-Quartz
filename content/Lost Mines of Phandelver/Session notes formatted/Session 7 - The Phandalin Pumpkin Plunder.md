### **A Visitor in the Night**

While resting at the inn, **Zephira** is awakened by a knock at her door.
Standing outside is **Syndrar Talour**, a drow noble of **House Zolarin**. Without waiting for an invitation, he enters the room.
Syndrar is clearly displeased.
> "Zephira Viseran, you had better have a good reason for your silence."
He questions her loyalties and demands to know what she has been doing since leaving the Underdark.
Zephira explains that she has been travelling with a group from Neverwinter under the guise of helping them. She tells him about:
- The mysterious **monolith** being transported by Grommar.
- Her belief that it contains powerful magic.
- The Redbrands and their defeat.
- Glasstaff and his connection to the Spider.
- The search for **Wave Echo Cave**.
- The legendary **Forge of Spells** hidden somewhere within it.

Syndrar seems particularly interested whenever the Forge of Spells is mentioned.
> "What do you know of the Forge of Spells?"

The questioning becomes increasingly intense.
Eventually, Syndrar asks directly:
> "Where is Grommar? Where are the stones?"

Zephira reveals that Grommar is staying further down the hall and that the stone shipment is currently stored in a workshop in town.
Syndrar is unimpressed by the lack of concrete information.
> "You had better provide us with something more substantial regarding the Forge of Spells, or this will become much worse for you."

---
## **A Meeting with the Townmaster**
The following morning, the party discusses their plans over breakfast.
**Toblen Stonehill** approaches their table.
> "You lot did a great job dealing with the Redbrands. Harbin wants to see you at the Town Hall."

The group heads over immediately.

---
### **Harbin's Questions**
At the Town Hall, **Harbin Wester** explains that he intends to make a public speech celebrating the defeat of the Redbrands and wants the details.
However, his questions feel unusually specific.
The party becomes suspicious and decides to be less than truthful:
- How did they get into the hideout?
    - **"We knocked five times and rang a bell."**
- Who was the leader?
    - **"A man named Jon."**
- What happened to the bodies?
    - **"A wolf ate them."**
- What were the Redbrands looking for?
    - **"No idea."**
Harbin accepts these answers without question.
> "I'll be making the speech shortly. Make sure you get a good seat for the festivities afterwards."

---
## **Harbin's Speech**

Not long later, Harbin addresses the town.
To the party's amusement, he takes credit for much of the operation and repeats several of their fabricated details as fact, including references to "Jon" and the mysterious wolf.
The townsfolk seem impressed regardless.
As the speech concludes, villagers begin encouraging everyone to head to **Alderleaf Farm**, where the annual autumn festivities are about to begin.

---
# **The Phandalin Pumpkin Plunder**

The campaign temporarily takes a festive turn as the town celebrates its annual harvest festival.
The farm is filled with:
- Pumpkin carving contests.
- Carnival games.
- Food stalls.
- Friendly competitions.
The villagers explain that every year a number of pumpkins grow to unusual sizes, and the town celebrates the harvest with games and contests.

---
### **Festival Games**

The party participates in a variety of carnival events, including **Pumpkin Bowling**.
The celebrations continue peacefully until disaster strikes.

---
### **Attack of the Giant Pumpkin**

Without warning, a **massive pumpkin creature** appears and begins rampaging through the festival.
The party leaps into action and defeats the monster.
As it dies, the enormous creature rapidly shrivels, collapsing into nothing more than a **small pumpkin**.

---
### **Festival Victors**

Among the day's competitions, **Solanar wins the pumpkin contest**.
As a reward, he receives the miniature pumpkin left behind by the defeated monster.
Sol gives the prize to **Bimblee**, who accepts it.

---

### **Session End**
The Redbrands are gone. The town celebrates its heroes. A mysterious drow presses Zephira for answers. And somewhere in the wilderness, the Spider continues to pull the strings.

Meanwhile, Phandalin settles down after another successful **Pumpkin Plunder Festival**... though whether the giant pumpkin was truly the strangest thing the party will face remains to be seen.
