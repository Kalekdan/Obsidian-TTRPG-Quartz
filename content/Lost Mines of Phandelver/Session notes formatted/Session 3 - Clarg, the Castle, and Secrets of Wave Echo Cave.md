### **Bridge Battle and Illusions**

As the party prepares to confront **Clarg**, **Yeemick** and **Urk** ask **Zephira** and **Tini** to scout ahead. When they reach the rope bridge, they find **Gurp**, the goblin from earlier, back on watch. However, Yeemick quickly tells him to stand down, claiming they’re just heading to meet Clarg.

Before long, **a fight breaks out**.

In the chaos, the party **destroys the rope bridge** to cut off enemy movement—but **Bimblee cleverly conjures an illusionary bridge**, allowing them to move freely while confusing their enemies.

As the dust settles, Yeemick shouts a final threat:

> **“You will perish! You’re lucky King Grol won’t take care of this himself!”**

---

### **Clues from Yeemick and Sildar**

With Yeemick defeated, the group interrogates him further. While he doesn’t know **exactly where Gundren is**, he reveals more about their mission:

> **“Clarg was told to capture them. He wants the map.”**

When questioned further, **Sildar Hallwinter**—the rescued warrior—speaks up:

> **“They want the map to Wave Echo Cave.”**

Yeemick elaborates:

- **King Grol** is the true leader of the **Cragmaw tribe**.
- Grol resides in a **castle approximately 25 miles northeast** of their current location.
- Yeemick believes **Gundren Rockseeker is being held there**.
    

With this information, Sildar makes a request:

> **“Please—get me back to Phandalin. I’ll pay you 50 gold if you get me there safely and quickly.”**

---

### **Return to Phandalin**

On the journey back, **Sildar shares what he knows** of the situation:

- Centuries ago, dwarves and gnomes formed the **Phandelver Pact**, agreeing to share a mine rich in magical energy and resources.
- Within it was the legendary **Forge of Spells**, a place where magical items could be crafted.
- However, a group of **bandits and an evil mercenary wizard** attacked, devastating the area. A great battle followed, and the location of the mine—**Wave Echo Cave**—was lost.
- Sildar is a member of the **Lords’ Alliance**, a group dedicated to upholding law and order.
- He was originally sent to Phandalin to locate his contact, **Yarno Albreck**, another Lords’ Alliance member—but **Yarno has gone silent**.
- While imprisoned, Sildar overheard the goblins talking about a figure known only as **“The Spider”**, to whom **Gundren** was ultimately taken.

---

### **Strange Glimmer in the Forest**

While making their way through the woods, the group hears a rustle in the trees. **Grommar** and **Bimblee** both catch a fleeting glimpse of **gold and red shimmer** between the branches—then it vanishes. The source remains unknown.

---

### **Back in Phandalin**

Once back in town, the party resupplies:

- **Bimblee** buys a **mirror** and several **candles**—perhaps in preparation for something arcane or symbolic.    
- **Grommar** purchases a new **warhammer**, ready to be better prepared for what’s to come.

At the **Stonehill Inn**, **Toblin the innkeeper** pulls them aside and warns them:
> **“The Redbrands were looking for you today. Keep your eyes open.”**

The threat in Phandalin is growing, and time may be running out.
