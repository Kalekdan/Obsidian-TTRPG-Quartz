### **Glasstaff’s Secrets**

- Sol discovers a **letter sealed with a spider’s mark** on Glasstaff’s desk, addressed to _Lord Iarno Albrek (Glasstaff)_.
    - The letter warns of **strangers in Phandalin** possibly working for the dwarves.
    - Orders: _“Stop them at any cost. Retrieve any dwarven maps in their possession.”_
- Sol also reviews notes in Glasstaff’s **journal**, which detail **Wave Echo Cave** and the **Forge of Spells** — a magical forge for crafting enchanted items.
- Glasstaff admits he’s **never met “the Spider”** directly.

The party strikes a **temporary truce** with Glasstaff — he’ll guide them to the bugbears to learn more about the Spider in exchange for his freedom once it’s done.

---
### **Meeting the Bugbears**
- Glasstaff warns: _“Use the north bridge, not the south — it’s trapped.”_
- They follow him to a chamber with **four bugbears**, led by **Nosk**, berating a **goblin prisoner** into joining the Cragmaw tribe.
- Bimblee understands the goblin threats and intervenes.

**Conversation Highlights:**
- Glasstaff: _“My friends need directions to Cragmaw Castle — the Spider commands you to cooperate.”_
- Nosk: _“We answer to the Spider, not you. Why do you need to know where he is?”_
- Bimblee bluffs: _“We have a message for the Spider. If you don’t tell us where he is, we’ll kill you.”_
- Sol presents the **Spider-sealed letter** as proof.

**Nosk’s Directions (dubious):**
> “It’s in the treeline. Keep going till a big brown tree with leaves — near a yellow river that moves.”
The group suspects Nosk’s **directions are nonsense** and he’s **never been there**.

---
### **Clearing Out the Hideout**
- Passing another room, they find **four Redbrands playing cards**. Glasstaff calms them down before things escalate. 
- On their way out, Glasstaff admits he **fed Fell Dendrar (the missing husband)** to Sarnak the Nothic — so reuniting the family could be... awkward.

---
### **The Blue Cape Plan**
To confuse the remaining Redbrands and their skeleton guardians:
- The party **buys blue capes** and claims it’s the _Spider’s new uniform_.
- Plan: give blue capes to Redbrands so the **skeleton traps attack them instead**.

---
### **Rescuing the Prisoners**
- Zephira bluffs the guards: _“The Spider wants me to interrogate the prisoners — and by the way, we’re rebranding to blue.”_
- The Redbrands leave wearing **blue capes** and promptly die to the skeleton trap.
- The party frees:
    - **Miene Dendrar** (mother)
    - Her daughters: **13F and 18F**
    - Their husband/father **Fell Dendrar** is missing (fed to Sarnak).

Miene offers a **future reward**:
> “We had a family heirloom in Thundertree — an emerald necklace in an old alchemy shop.”

_(Note: Remember this for when the party reaches **Thundertree**.)_

---
### **Aftermath**
- The party brings **Redbrand bodies** to **Sarnak**, who devours them and gifts them **5 malachite gems** in return.
- They send Miene and her children back safely to Phandalin.
- Zephira executes **Glasstaff** and kills his **pet quasit**.
- The party leaves **blue capes and a note** instructing any remaining Redbrands to wear them.

Upon returning to Phandalin, they’re greeted with **cheers and gratitude** — the heroes of the town.
- **Free bed and board** at the inn for several nights.
