The party finds themselves at the Rusty Anvil, a well-known tavern in town. Upon entering, they encounter Eric Thaumand, a former tattooed mercenary who offers valuable information about the Iron Company for 150gp. Generous Jack decides to buy drinks for everyone present, while the group welcomes Aevorilius Eclipsus, a highborn character with a questionable background.

As the night unfolds, Jasmal mourns the loss of her executed son, who was involved with the dangerous Sacrosanct. Theodore captivates the group with a thrilling tale of Kale the Scorned. Meanwhile, Jack pickpockets Echo, stealing 15gp from his coin dispenser, and makes a failed attempt to charm Aevorilius. Instead, he pranks Aevor by using a rusty bag of tricks to spawn a goat in his bed.

The tavernkeep, Otto Brownlock, mentions that hangings take place at the crossroads just outside of town. This information hits Jasmal hard, as she learns about the tragic fate of Dorn and his green leather boots from Adrik. The party sets off on a journey through the farmland, reaching the edge of the high forest after a couple of hours.

Upon arriving at the crossroads, they discover a massive, thousand-year-old oak adorned with nooses and surrounded by footprints. Aevorilius spots an overturned cart nearby, its contents—rags and bodies—strewn across the ground. Investigating further, Felran senses something amiss with the oak, which suddenly comes to life and attacks!

As the battle commences, Echo reveals his newly-acquired thunder gauntlets. The tree, enraged by the party's onslaught, consumes Echo, who finds himself in a race against time as the tree's acid erodes his health points. Bramble, driven to protect his friend, enters a furious rage, while Felran rains eldritch blasts upon the tree.

Despite Theodore's inspiring tales of Kale the Scorned, Echo succumbs to the acid, his consciousness fading as he experiences a vision of a large humanoid figure working on his robotic body. Jack's inflict wounds spell finally weakens the tree enough for it to expel Echo's lifeless form. Theodore finally slays the tree with one swift slash.

Rushing to Echo's side, Bramble and Theodore manage to revive him with cure wounds, and Echo shares his vision with Bramble. Felran notices guards approaching, and the group quickly hides. Bramble feigns death and is loaded onto a cart with the other bodies, where he stages a terrifying resurrection, scaring the guards into revealing information about the hangings and a recent ambush on an envoy carrying Dorn to his execution.

Searching through the tree's digested remains, the party uncovers a pair of green leather boots, confirming their fears that the body they found belonged to Dorn. Grieving for the young man, Bramble tenderly wraps Dorn's remains and places them in the cart, which the intimidated guards have graciously "donated" to the party.

With heavy hearts, the group leads the cart back to the village, arriving in the evening to share the news with Jasmal and bring closure to Dorn's tragic tale.
