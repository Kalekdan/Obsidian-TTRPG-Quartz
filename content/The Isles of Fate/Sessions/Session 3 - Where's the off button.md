##  The Crypt 
As the party descends down the stairs, Echo spreads grease around behind the party, in an attempt to stop anyone following them down. They descend into the basement and find themselves in an expansive chamber, with 3 plinths in front of them. On the left is a shield, the right is a chalice, and in the middle a dark granite pyramid. By the plinth in the center are 3 individuals, two tattooed members of the iron company, and an old man.

##  The Fight 

Theo immediately tries to control one of the individuals but fails to grasp his mind. Felran steps forward and casts a massive shatter, damaging all 3, Bramble steps forward and swings his great axe, finishing the first thug off. From plinths on the walls, two gargoyles start to move and fly towards the fight, one swinging for the other thug, and one critting Theo, leaving him deeply wounded. The old man who was with the members of the iron company grabs the Pyramid and tells the party they don't know what they're messing with. He then casts a spell, that collapses in on him and removes him from the room.

As he disappears the middle plinth, now empty, starts to rise, and a portcullis falls, keeping Jack and Felran outside the chamber and trapping the others within. Jack throws out a trick from his rusty bag, summoning a loyal rat that attacks the nearest gargoyle, he also pushes his incorporeal form through the portcullis to go and help. He then goes to run upstairs, navigating the grease on the stairs. Felran casts shatter again on the stone gargoyle, but with much less effect than the last cast had, so Echo runs over and smashes the gargoyle with his gauntlets trying to save Theo. Theo heals himself and then steels himself with a legend of a Traveller. Bramble seeing how the fight seems to be going also runs over and hits the gargoyle near Theo, raging at the situation. 

Jack, finding no good way out of the situation without the party, runs back down again, and uses his doppelganger to cast inflict wounds, causing massive damage to the same gargoyle the rest of the party has been hitting. The rat attacks again, and somehow manages to remove one of the gargoyles toes. Theo casts Bane, causing all 3 gargoyles to falter on their attacks. Echo and Bramble swing for this same gargoyle again, which despite the beating is still standing. The dwarf from upstairs stumbles down the stairs over the grease, and tells the nearest gargoyle to slumber, and it stops moving, but the others don't hear it. The other gargoyle distracted by Jacks doppelganger continues to swipe for it to no effect. Jack in confusion swings for the dwarf, whilst his rat manages to bite off another toe, and topples the gargoyle forward, finally destroying it. Theo calls out for the last gargoyle to slumber, and it does so, ending the fight.

##  Looting the temple 

As the party goes to raise the portcullis, Theo and Echo suddenly realise that they have time alone in this room, and drop the portcullis before rushing off to grab the other two relics. Theo goes to the right, to an artifact titled the chalice of temptation. It contains a clear liquid and seems to be a healing substance, so Theo steps up and grabs it, taking a swig. The liquid heals him completely, but as he's drinking, a net falls restraining him. Meanwhile Echo heads to the plinth on the left, here he finds the Shield of Calor. Upon casting identify item, he recognises it to be a shield that gives resistance to ranged attacks. But when Echo goes to pick it up, another net falls on him, restraining him as well.

As Jack is keeping the Dward distracted throughout this, Bramble is able to try and free Theo and Echo, he goes to free Theo, but only manages to tangle himself in the net. In rage he flexes and manages to tear the net, freeing himself and Theo. He goes to free Echo, and does the exact same thing, Theo and Echo, apparently not having learnt their lesson then go and loot the bodies of the Iron Company members. Theo finds a large key, and a note detailing their purpose.

The party then go and together lift the portcullis, and talk to the dwarf, claiming that the Iron Company had stolen all 3 artifacts. The dwarf explained that the middle artifact could definitely be dangerous in the wrong hands, the other two were more locked up to prevent harm coming to people who came in to contact with them. Whilst Echo and Theo might have looked sheepish, they don't offer anything up and are allowed to leave the temple with the artifacts
