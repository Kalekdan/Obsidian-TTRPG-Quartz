####  Premise 
A pair of owlbears have been attacking adventurers and hoarding their items. They can be fought to free their stuff.
###  Starting the quest 
If the party pick up one of the sending stones from [[Starnley Higgs]] in [[Pinapota]], they may hear a noise on the other end, which can be recognised to be an owlbear.

Alternatively they may just encounter the nest.

> As you come into a clearing in the trees, there is a small cluster of rocks surrounding a large mass of twigs and branches. A large figure begins to loom out from between some of the rocks on all-fours. A creature, larger than a bear, covered in feathers and with large eyes and a beak looks in your direction. It cocks it's head and makes a sinister clicking noise with its beak. A second creature approaches from behind.
> You also notice half of the head appears to be torn off, and what appear to be worms are crawling out of it.

There is a sensitive piece of arcane equipment, any magic cast in the cave will cause it to detonate, dealing 4d10 damage if not stopped. It will leave behind a tear in the boundaries to random plane.
