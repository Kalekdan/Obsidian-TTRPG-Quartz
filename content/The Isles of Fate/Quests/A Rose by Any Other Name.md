####  Premise 
[[Poppo Threadborne]] is in love with [[Saffron Brownlock]] (the barmans wife), and wants a magic flower for her, to make her fall in love with him.

###  Starting the quest 
The quest is given by [[Poppo Threadborne]], the bard from [[The Rusty Anvil]] in the old town of [[Pinapota]]. He is playing very sad music of heartbreak, and unrequited love. The party can approach him and speak to him, or the barman [[ Otto Brownlock]] can ask the party to speak to him and ask him to play something more lively.

###  Poppo Threadborne 
* He is in love with [[Saffron Brownlock]] (the dwarf who works the forge opposite)
* He wants her to love him back
* He's heard of a magic flower ([[Lover's Rose]]), which would be the perfect gift.
* It grows in a grove nearby, but the druids that tend it won't give it to him
* He will pay 50g (75g) to the party to get it for him
###  The Grove 
As they approach the grove, there are some [[Flower Druids]] looking uneasy. They inform the party that the flower grove has been invaded by some astral beasts.
