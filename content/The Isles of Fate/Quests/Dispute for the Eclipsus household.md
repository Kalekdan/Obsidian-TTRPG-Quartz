When speaking to people in Bres, they will establish everyone thought the duke had no sons, so his bastard [[Ser Gideon Stormborn]] has showed up to try and claim his inheritance of the position.

[[Helvetria]], the botanist, who gave [[Aevorilius Eclipsus|Aevor]] the sword, is the only person who knows he is still alive, and is being threatened by Gideon not to tell anyone.

**The party must find a way to prove to the rest of the council that [[Aevorilius Eclipsus|Aevor]] is the rightful heir, or they will be tried for treason**

Members of the council:
* [[Ser Gideon Stormborn]] (newest member since showing up out of the blue)
    * **Motivation**: Gideon seeks recognition and legitimacy. He believes that by claiming to be the bastard son of the late duke, he can secure a position of power and influence within the city.
    * **Useful Information**: Gideon may have some knowledge of the late duke's affairs or secrets that could be valuable to the party. However, he is also a potential rival to [[Aevorilius Eclipsus|Aevor]]'s claim to the throne, and his motives should be carefully scrutinized.
    * Evidence *for* him being duke:
    * Owns the dukes signet ring (high DC it is fake - family in jewelry trade)
    * Has the dukes will (text is scrawled as though drugged/manipulated)

* [[Mercoth Vask]] - The Groundskeeper
    * **Motivation**: As the Groundskeeper, Mercoth is deeply loyal to the city and its people. He values stability and order above all else and wants to see a leader who can ensure the city's safety and prosperity.
    * **Useful Information**: Mercoth has intimate knowledge of the city's layout and secrets, including hidden passages or forgotten chambers within the palace. He may also have insights into the public sentiment regarding the various candidates for the throne.
    * Reasons for/against Ser Gideon as Duke:
    * **Just wants the rightful heir**

* [[Master Thorne Goldhand]] - The Merchant
    * **Motivation**: Thorne is primarily motivated by profit and self-interest. As a shrewd merchant, he seeks to align himself with the candidate who can offer him the greatest financial opportunities and advantages.
    * **Useful Information**: Thorne has extensive connections throughout the city and beyond, including other merchants, traders, and information brokers. He may possess valuable intelligence on the economic state of the kingdom and any potential trade agreements or conflicts that could impact the council's decision.
    * Reasons for/against Ser Gideon as Duke:
    * Ser Gideon has promised him primary **shipping contracts** with his families jewellery business.

* [[Lady Isadora]] - The Spymaster
    * **Motivation**: Isadora operates in the shadows, gathering intelligence and manipulating events to serve her own agenda. She seeks to maintain her position as the Spymaster and ensure that the council's decision aligns with her interests.
    * **Useful Information**: Isadora has a network of spies and informants at her disposal, allowing her to uncover secrets and manipulate events behind the scenes. She may possess incriminating evidence against certain council members or candidates that could be used to sway the vote in [[Aevorilius Eclipsus|Aevor]]'s favor.
    * Reasons for/against Ser Gideon as Duke:
    * Can **manipulate** Ser Gideon easily
    * May be unstable

* [[Lord Aric Ironhand]] - The Military Commander
    * **Motivation**: Aric is a staunch traditionalist who values strength and military prowess. He believes that the next ruler must be a capable leader who can defend the city from external threats and maintain law and order within.
    * **Useful Information**: Aric commands the city's military forces and has firsthand knowledge of its defenses and strategic vulnerabilities. He may have insights into potential threats or conflicts looming on the horizon, as well as any alliances or rivalries between neighboring kingdoms.

* [[Archmage Valeria Nightshade]] - The Archmage/Alchemist
    * **Motivation**: Valeria is driven by a thirst for arcane knowledge and power. She seeks to ensure the city's safety from magical threats and may be willing to support the candidate who can demonstrate a strong connection to the mystical arts.
    * **Useful Information**: Valeria possesses extensive knowledge of ancient magic and artifacts, as well as access to potent potions and spells. She may have insights into any magical disturbances or anomalies occurring within the city that could influence the council's decision.
