####  Premise 
A clothes store in [[Pinapota]] has been cursed by a fiend. He threatens the owner [[Risha Highvale]] to keep selling or he will kill her. He says if she sells enough of the cursed items they will be lifted and he will leave.

* [Fiend Statblock](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#hydroloth_mpmm)

###  Starting the quest 
They may visit [[Heirloom Apparel]] and notice something off.
They will see a man walking around town with a swarm of rats chasing after him - His new shoes are from the shop and he can't take them off.

###  Cursed Clothes 
* Shoes which attract rats
* Shoes which always untie themselves
* A cloak which attracts insects
* A belt which tightens itself slowly
* A shirt that makes the wearer stink, regardless of how many times they wash it or themselves
* A pair of pants that always seem to have a tear in them, exposing the wearer's undergarments
* A dress that causes the wearer to itch uncontrollably, making it difficult to focus or concentrate

###  The Fiend 
The fiend comes to the shop every night and curses another item of clothing. He then leaves through a tunnel under the floorboards. His abode is through an underground cave under the city.

If the party are not careful, he will take Risha hostage and threaten to kill her if they engage him.

###  The Sewers 
In the sewers, there are several points of interest:
* Dancing skeletons - the won't attack, unless the music player next to them is destroyed
* Carnivorous plant - only bites if they try and pick it up. A few flowers next to it, including a green chromatic rose.
* 'Curse Workshops' - various items he's working on cursing
    * Chain armor, which chains you to anything you touch
    * Fur coat, which has fleas which you can't get rid of

###  Outcomes 
* Kill the fiend - all items remain cursed (severe loss of stock)
* Convince the fiend to remove the curses (very difficult)
* Lift the curses
