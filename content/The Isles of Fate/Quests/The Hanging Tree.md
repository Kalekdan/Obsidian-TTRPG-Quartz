**Map: 9649**
####  Premise 
Woman's son is hanged on the tree. The body is supposed to be returned to her, but is refused. This is due to the body being consumed by the tree.

* Woman is [[Jasmal]]
* Son was called [[Dorn]] (was involved in the Sacrosanct)
* [[Adrik]] was a friend of [[Dorn]].
###  Starting the quest 
The quest may be given by the woman in the Rusty Anvil in Pinapota, who is crying. She will tell without much prompting, about her son who was executed on the tree. If they push further, they can find out why he was executed. He was a member of the Sacrosanct.

She will tell the party that she was refused the body being returned to her. She is too old and weak to go herself, and does not wish to see her son hanging anyway.

#### = The Hanging Tree 
The tree is situated right at the edge of the forest to the W of Pinapota (about a 4 hour walk).
