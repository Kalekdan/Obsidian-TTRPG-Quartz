The Isles of Fate is a high fantasy campaign set in the world of [[Hircia]]. It consists of 4 large continents, surrounded by a large number of small, mostly independant islands.

The 4 primary continents are below, as well as the primary factions which reside on them:
* The Northern Wilds
    * Dominion of Savaria
    * Principality of Sal
* The Gem of the West
    * Duchy of Bunelia
    * Empire of Kelpis
    * Free Cities of Nakur
* Eastern Isle
    * City State of Momalia
    * Republic of Teshi
    * Lanian Republic
* The Kingdom of Errun
    * The Kingdom of Errun

The surrounding smaller islands are mostly considered part of the independant Singualian Territories.

The Kingdom of Errun technically holds power over all of the above, but their influence is lessened the further from their central position you find yourself. Whilst there is no major war ongoing at the moment, tensions are high as rumours of rebellion against Errun circulate.

The only currently active conflicts of any scale are between the Duchy of Bunelia and the Empire of Kelpis on the 'Gem of the West'. They fight over the ownership of the dense and gem rich jungles of the south west coast, as well as laying claim to much of the land of the Free Cities of Nakur.

The campaign begins in the centre of the known world, in the capital of Errun, in a large central city called Pinapota.

##  Rules 
The game will mostly follow rules a written with a couple of exceptions:
* We will follow the official death save rules (hit when down is an automatic death save failure). The only exception is I won't give enemies an automatic crit on a downed person (they will continue to have advantage though)
* Death saves will be whispered to the DM (I think we generally just found that more exciting)
* **Flashing Before Your Eyes:** Any time you are incapacitated for your turn, the DM will ask you a question about your character or their history. If you answer the question, you get inspiration.
