*The lich turns slowly, his once-regal robes now tattered and stained with the passage of time. His hollow eyes, glowing with a cold blue light, fix upon the party as a twisted smile spreads across his gaunt face.*

"So, you’ve come at last… to stop me, I presume? To brand me as some villain in your little tale. But you know nothing of the pain that drives me. You see only the monster I’ve become, not the man I once was—the father, the husband who lost everything.

*He raises a skeletal hand, gesturing toward the towering arcane machine at the center of the room, its gem pulsing ominously.*

"This device, this ‘abomination’ as you no doubt see it, is my last hope. Through it, I will tear down the veil between worlds and bring them back—my beloved wife, my innocent child—ripped from me by the cruelty of fate. Would you not do the same, if you had the power? Would you not defy death itself to reclaim those you love?"

*His voice grows cold, the light in his eyes intensifying as his tone hardens.*

"But you cannot understand. You only see what is in front of you—heroes, blind to the greater truth. Stand in my way if you must, but know this: I will not be stopped. Not by you, not by anyone. And if the world must burn for me to hold them once more… so be it."
