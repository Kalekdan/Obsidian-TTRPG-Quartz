The Duchy of Bunelia is a very large state situated on [[The Gem of the West]]. It has a very powerful military, and is very close with [[The Kingdom of Errun]].

It is currently in possession of the majority of the highly sought after jungle on the south western coast of the content, known for it's high concentration of precious stones, for which the continent gets it's name.

The Duke of Bunelia ([[Duke Alosrin Eclipsus]]) has long been a popular figure with much of the aristocracy, and draws a lot of favour from the [[The King of Errun|King of Errun]] in particular. He also shares the King's love of extravagance and often hosts parties and lavish events. Many of the Dukes closest advisors recognise this behaviour as severely damaging to the fief, and foresee economic collapse, but the Duke himself appears largely ignorant of this.

The Dukes son, [[Aevorilius Eclipsus]], attempted to stage a coup to overthrow the Duke for the sake of maintaining a semblance of the power that his father was whittling away. When uncovered, this caused a rift in the already strained relationship between the father and son, and Aevorilius (Aevor) was disowned.

[[Mercoth Vask]] was one of the hosuekeepers in the house.

There are several powerful houses in the Duchy of Bunelia, as well as some smaller houses, such as the one [[Surzak | Surzak Valrel's]] family belongs to.

The state capital of Bunelia, and where the Eclipsus dukedom reside, is [[Bres]].
