The salt dwellers are an indigenous group who live in the caves along the coasts of some of the more remote southern islands of the [[The Singualian Territories|Singualian Territories]]. They are thought to be a mostly fishing community, though interactions with them are rare. In addition to how remote they live, they are also most active during the night and are therefore an uncommon sight. In appearance, they are very similar to humans although they have larger eyes and webbed digits. They are also recorded as being able to stay underwater for significantly longer than a human, up to 10 minutes on a single dive. It is thought therefore that they are a hybrid races between humans and [[Mer-people]] many generations back.
