The Empire of Kelpis is one of the two largest states on [[The Gem of the West | the westernmost continent]]. There are often conflicts along the eastern border of Kelpis, with neighbouring states of [[The Duchy of Bunelia|Bunelia]] and [[The Free City of Nakur |Nakur]], mostly over ownership of the dense and gem rich jungles of the south west coast, from which [[The Gem of the West]] gets it's name. The majority of these territories are currently owned by the [[The Duchy of Bunelia|Duchy of Bunelia]].
