The Iron Company are an infamous group of mercenaries who primarily reside on [[ The Northern Wilds]], near [[Southelme]], in a large outpost in the forest.

They are famed for their 'highest bidder' approach, and will work for the most evil of individuals, provided they are paid handsomely. They are generally very traditional in their combat styles, preferring hand to hand combat, over assassinations or the use of magic.

They are easily recognisable due to their distinctive blue face tattoos, and thick fur cloaks, attached across one shoulder with a large iron medallion.

One of their ex-members, [[Erick Thormund]], frequents [[The Rusty Anvil]] where he offers his services privately.
