The Sacrosanct are an anonymous rebel group based in [[Pinapota]]. They also have a significant following in [[The Duchy of Bunelia| Bunelia]]. They oppose the leadership of the [[The King of Errun|King of Errun]] and [[The Duchy of Bunelia]]. They wish to liberate the people. They started as a group of rebels from [[The Free City of Nakur| Nakur]], but left in the midst of the conflict. Seeing the powerful states as a cause of this conflict, they aim to bring them down by any means neccesary.

There are some more extreme members who plot assassinations and other attacks as an aim to destabilise the state. The more common members provide asylum to those fleeing the war, and vandalise government buildings.
