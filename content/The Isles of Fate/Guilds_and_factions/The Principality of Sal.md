The Principality of Sal is one of the major political powers on the Isles of Fate, despite holding the smallest amount of land on the central islands. This is mostly due to the vast wealth provided by the mines just south of the capital of [[Castinfort]], which are the primary source of gold and steel for the other territories.

The Principality also holds a few of the smaller northern isles.

There are also a number of smaller settlements in the principality, most notably the small town of [[Virellis]], which hosts its annual [[The Festival of the Dead|Festival of the Dead]].
