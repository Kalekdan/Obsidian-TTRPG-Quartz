The Taxmen are a criminal gang based in [[Pinapota]]. They are experts at blackmailing and extorting individuals for money and influence. They also offer 'protection' to some of the villages in the area, including the one [[Bramble]] was raised in. When the village refused to pay, they raided and pillaged to send a message, and [[Bramble]]'s family were killed.

In some cases, the individuals they extort end up joining their ranks if they could be considered useful. One such individual is [[Bron Kaskin]]. He borrowed some money, and when he couldn't pay back the huge interest, they decided to employ him as muscle due to his large stature - he has been working for them since, and is no closer to paying of the debt.

Their leader is [[Blath]].
