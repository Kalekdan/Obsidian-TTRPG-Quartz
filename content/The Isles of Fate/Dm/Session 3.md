###  The Iron Company Encampment 
> As you approach the clearing in the dense forest, you spot a small encampment beside a swiftly flowing river. A cliff on the far side of the river overlooks the encampment. The encampment is surrounded by trees on all sides, their leaves casting dappled shadows across the clearing. A few small paths lead from the edge of the clearing to the huts, which are made from rough-hewn timber and thatched with straw. ++ Perception DC 10. |It seems underpopulated, perhaps 2 or 3 people.++
>
>There are 5 or 6 huts in total, arranged in a rough circle in the centre of the clearing. Smoke rises from a small fire pit in the centre, and you can hear the faint murmur of voices as the mercenaries inside chat amongst themselves. The huts themselves appear to be fairly new, and relatively temporary, but still sturdy enough to provide shelter from the elements.

[[Iron Company Encampment]]

Within the camp - there is a lockbox containing 30 gold, and a plan of the [[Temple of Resplendence]], and Pinapota Library.

In the centre of the encampment is a large cage, with a [chimera](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#chimera_mm) in it. It is trapped to release the creature based on magical glyphs on the ground (investigation required to spot them).

There is also a post in the ground with open shackles attached.
