The party comes to on a stage in [[Pinapota Bardic Academy]]. [[Bron Kaskin]] is tied up on a stool next to them. In the middle of the stage is a large cage with a [Xorn](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#xorn_mm) inside.

There are members of [[The Taxmen]] in the audience, with [[Blath]] - [statblock](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#axe%20of%20mirabar%20soldier_skt) seated in the middle. He pulls a lever, and 3 [dire wolves](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#dire%20wolf_mm) are released from the wings on stage.

* There are several levers amongst the crowd, each has a different effect - from left to right:
    * A [swarm of snakes](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#swarm%20of%20poisonous%20snakes_mm) are dropped from the ceiling]], onto the stage
    * A fire trap is triggered, burning the right side of the stage (3d6 fire)
    * Set of crossbows, aimed at the centre of the stage fire 3 shots (+8 to hit - 5 (1d10) piercing dmg)
    * A fire trap is triggered, burning the left side of the stage (3d6 fire)
* The cage on the stage is magical, so that the creature can't burrow through it.
    * If he escapes, he will attack at random - but will target any precious metals

If the 'beat' the encounter, Blath will be impressed and will tell them all they need to know. They can also bargain to free Bron.
