As your party explores the Whispering Oaks Graveyard, you come across a heart-wrenching scene. Nestled amidst the headstones, you spot an ailing unicorn and a frail, elderly figure dressed in tattered robes [[Oake]] (if cured he is a much younger dryad). The unicorn's once-pure white coat is marred by a deep gash on its flank, and its horn flickers faintly. The elderly figure, the last surviving member of the Sylvaris Guardians, lies nearby, succumbing to a terrible corruption that has taken hold.

Approaching cautiously, you hear the feeble whispers of the elderly guardian. Their voice is barely audible, but filled with urgency and sorrow:

"Please, kind travelers, you must help us. I am Elowen, the last of the Sylvaris Guardians, and this is Sylvaris, our beloved protector of these ancient woods and this sacred graveyard. We have long defended this place from darkness, but a terrible corruption has taken root, and we are fading away. You are our only hope."

Elowen explains that the Sylvaris Guardians were a secret society dedicated to protecting the unicorn and the mystical energy of the graveyard from dark forces that sought to corrupt it. However, their numbers dwindled, and their magical defenses weakened over time.

The party can choose to help in several ways:

Treat Their Wounds: The party can use their healing abilities or knowledge of medicine to tend to Sylvaris and Elowen, providing immediate relief and easing their suffering.

Investigate the Corruption: The party can explore the graveyard to identify the source of corruption. This might involve dispelling dark enchantments, confronting corrupted creatures, or cleansing defiled altars.

Preserve the Legacy: Elowen implores the party to preserve the legacy of the Sylvaris Guardians. They can do this by recovering and safeguarding the society's hidden records, artifacts, and relics.

Defend Against Dark Forces: The party may need to defend Sylvaris and Elowen against waves of corrupted creatures drawn to the mystical energy of the graveyard.

As the party assists Sylvaris and Elowen, they uncover the tragic history of the Sylvaris Guardians and the significance of their role as protectors of this sacred place. Depending on their choices, they may earn the gratitude of the dying guardian and the unicorn, potentially receiving blessings or assistance that could aid them in future quests.
