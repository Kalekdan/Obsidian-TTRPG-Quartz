When the party arrive at the college, it is completely missing, and find it replaced by a mysterious and otherworldly landscape. The air shimmers with magical residue, and the familiar structures have been replaced by surreal and arcane formations.

If they enter, they encounter [Hounds](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#yeth%20hound_mpmm,scaled:2) walking around.

###  How did it happen 
Varian (a student) was playing around with transmutation, trying to turn a rat into an apple, magic was a bit fucky, and the whole college got replaced.

They can find him, half merged with an apple in the basement, as the only remnants of the college. He will attack but can be calmed down. [Statblock](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#treant_mm)`

###  The location 
**Corridor**, there are 3 [Hounds](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#yeth%20hound_mpmm,scaled:2) walking around. Lots of rats scurrying around as well.

On one of the **rooms on the near right** (the kitchen), there is lots of mushrooms growing, and spores in the air (DC 13 CON save or (1 - poisoned, 2 - hideous laughter for 1 minute, 3- 1d6 damage)). This was where the kitchen was, there are some apples that can be found in here.

**On the right** (equivalent of where the students quarters were) most rooms are destroyed or barely standing. There is one room that seems mostly intact - inside is Varian's room, lots of notes everywhere, fire damage - a small cage on the right (had a rat in), a rotten tree (was an apple tree), and notes about how to do transmutation, as well as a diagram of the experiment.

**On the left** (Danus' office and others) - they can open the door, and there is nothing behind it, a huge chasm collapsed into the ground.

**Lecture hall**, at the far side of the college, they will find a apple/treant creature (Varian) and a rat king who will attack them on sight. They will also see scorch marks in various seats around the lecture hall.

**In the basement**, where the labs are they will find remnants of this experiment: If they look into the fire damage, they could find a melted pile of gold (needed to be ignited for the experiment to work). The experiments they can see are:
* Alchemists bottles, cracked but one contains some potion still (potion of liquid smoke)
* 2 cloches, connected by a coiled wire
* A room full of levitating objects (quill, inkwell, silver coins, paper, cutlery). If they touch any of it, it all falls
* A plant with crystals growing out of it.
