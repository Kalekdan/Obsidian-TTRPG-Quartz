When travelling, the party meet a strange individual ([[Sliber]]) who recognises stormforged, and promises a gift to the great soldier who can wield it.

He told them there is another artefact of the tempest in a dungeon nearby. The Lyre of the Tempest. He will wait in the [[Goliaths Alehouse]] for them to return so he can look at it. He marked it for them on their map.

The gift is the Lyre of the Tempest, which is just an ordinary lyre.

####  The Dungeon 
- Room 1 Entrance (Lightning), 2 sparking knobs on the door, with wires leading away, need to short it or otherwise power them - otherwise door has 150hp to break
- Room 2 Puzzle (Rain) - There are rain clouds that hover over their heads. Glass looking floor, with channels running under it leading to a small pool under the end door. x grates in the room that when the rain falls down all of them, opens the door, but when the rain stops, the door closes. From other side can be opened with same shorting method as initial entrance.
- Room 3 (Cold) - Freezing in this room, and floor is very icy - There are 2 frost giants seemingly asleep, if they make too much noise they'll wake up.
- Room 4 (Wind) - There's a lyre on a pedestal embeddedin crystal - sign under it 'Only the worthy'
