###  Danus 
They may receive a message on the sending stone. "I have some important information about the planar fluctuations. Meet me at my study. It's urgent".

He will tell them that he has plotted the spikes in activity, and they seem to align with the moon phases. Each full moon causes a spike, with Infuscus causing the biggest spikes. In 11 days, all 4 moons will be full.

####  Stolen experiments

Danus will give them access to the library if they get the experiments back for him.
