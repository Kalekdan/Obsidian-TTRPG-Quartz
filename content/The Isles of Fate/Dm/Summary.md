###  Surzak Arc 
Surzak's wife and child died, he wants to resurrect them. They don't want to be resurrected, so he's breaking down the barriers between the planes to do so. (Visil was the one that infected his son with the curse that caused him to die). Visil is pulling all the strings, and wants the boundaries broken down so he can take over the material plane.

###  Jottunheimr/Bramble/Echo Arc 
Giants were super advanced with magic. **Bramble** was sent away when he was born - he was born of magic, which people believed caused Visil to first come about. [[Orlanthus Gemheart]] managed to cast him away to protect him. Scared for his other child (Bron) who no one knew about, he created **Echo** to protect them both. When Visil attacked 10 years later, and virtually destroyed Jottunheimr, **Echo** and Bron were also cast away as the tidal wave hit, and sought out **Bramble**.

**Echo** gets flashbacks to this, and can have his memory restored if they attempt to.

* Echo see's hammer being struck against him **DONE**
* Echo see's remembers his first moments of consciousness - see's Bron as a young boy
* Echo see's a tidal wave hundreds of metres tall. Also notices Bron next to him.

On Jottunheimr, the remaining giants will also be nervous about **Theodore** as he was born of magic, and they believe this is dangerous.
###  Visil Arc 
Visil is a creature from another plane, and grants power to **Felran**. He does this to use **Felran** as another pawn to help him come back. His rival is Bahamut, (**Father Traumaticus'** deity).
###  Taxmen Arc 
Taxmen are a criminal gang in Pinapota, run by Blath. He has had his hands chopped off by the party when interrogating him, and told to stop his work. In response, he gets a pair of metal hands (inspired by **Echo**), and steps up his terrorizing of the locals.
