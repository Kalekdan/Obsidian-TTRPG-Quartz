The party start in the court case, having taken the place of 'Jessica', and her lawyer 'Baron'. The case is for tax evasion and fraud. Jessica had been embezeling funds from a charity that Jasmal had put in place in memory for her son Dorn.

Whole case is a setup - Jessica was a friend of Jasmal's son Dorn, who was part of a underground rebellion called the Sacrosanct. They are hoping if they can send her down, they can interrogate her further.

The charges are as follows:
* 2 counts of fraud
    * Pretending to have lost a son to claim money from 'WhistleWhileYouWeep', a charity encouraging the elderly and grieving into music to take their minds off their loss
    * Charging imports an 'import tax' when they drop off goods at the skyport
* 1 counts of tax evasion

At one point in the case, the judge will ask the jury to leave to discuss, leaving just the judge (busy with paperwork, the prosecutor, and the remaining party in the room). The hidden shrine is on the back wall of the courtroom, which when a specific stone is hit by the gavel, will open a door.

Whenever the judge strikes with the gavel, observent players will notice parts of the wall shimmering.

###  Opening Statements 
####  Judge Scalin 
> Ladies and gentlemen of the court, we gather here today to seek justice in a matter of great importance. The allegations before us involve financial mismanagement, deceit, and the exploitation of trust. Our duty is to impartially evaluate the evidence presented and to ensure a fair and just resolution. May our proceedings be guided by the principles of truth, integrity, and a commitment to the pursuit of justice. You will hear from both sides of the case, and it is up to you to decide the fate of this individual.
> 
> The charges are as follows:
> **2 counts of fraudulent activity**, embezzling funds from the charitable organisation 'WhistleWhileYouWeep', up to a value of 3000gp, claiming a 'import tax' during your work on the skyport docks up to a value of 600gp, **1 counts of tax evasion**, refusing to claim income from your additional work as a cleaner in the palace, owing the state of Errun 250gp.

####  Prosecutor 
> Your Honor, esteemed members of the jury, today we present a case that exposes a pattern of deceit and manipulation. The accused, Jessica, stands before you charged with crimes that strike at the heart of our financial system and the trust we place in one another. We will unveil a carefully crafted scheme that exploits not only the mechanisms of commerce but the emotional vulnerabilities of a grieving mother. As we unfold the evidence, the truth will emerge, and justice will prevail.

###  Witnesses 
* [[Jasmal]] - The mother of [[Dorn]] and owner of the charity, 'Whistle While You Weep'. She is oblivious to the money that has been stolen, and lots of leading questions are asked by the prosecutor
    * Do you know the defendent? Yes, but...
    * How long has the charity been running? A couple of weeks
    * Have you noticed the income has been particularly low? Yes, but...
    * Why do you think the defendent stole from you? I don't know
    * So you admit they stole from you? I'm not sure
    * Are you protecting them? No
    * So you are happy for them to go to prison for their crimes? No
    * You want them to get away with this? No
* [[Dean Larkin]] - Half orc and manager at the skyport
    * Wearing very nice clothes, despite earning very low salary (was bribed to set jessica up)
* [[Captain Sally Smithers]] - Gnome, and captain of one of the skyships who were charged
    * Looks nervous - career has been threatened if they don't set up Jessica
* [[Lord Kally]] - Senior lord in the palace of Pinapota, and employer of Jessica
    * Jessica had been working in the palace cleaning, for the past 6 months (presents evidence of employment)
