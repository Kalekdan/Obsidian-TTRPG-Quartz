####  Journey to Roseport 
* Along river/road
    * Longer journey, more likely to encounter people on the road, less likely to get lost
    * [[The Misty Grove Ambush|misty grove]]
* Through the mountain pass
    * Direct route, but more challenging, if they get lost could take a long time
    * [[Mountain spectres]]
Depending on which way they gothey can take a much longer route which is safer, or travel off the road through the misty grove.

If they go to the skyport, it will cost 1300GP to Bunelia, 600GP to Roseport. They can get it for half price, if they agree to escort some valuables on a slower journey. The valueables would be wines and spirits.
