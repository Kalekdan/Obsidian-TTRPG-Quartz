###  [[The Rusty Anvil ]]
#####  [[Erick Thormund ]]
Once bought he will tell about the iron company:
* They are mercenaries, work for the highest bidder (not cheap)
* Very traditional, no nonsense swordsmen (not a fan of magic or sneaky assassinations)
* They originate on the northern continent (the northern wilds)
* They'd set up camp nearby
He will also tell what he knows about the camp:
* About a day's travel away, in the woods, upstream to the west
* 'Last I heard, there were only a few of them, maybe 5 or 6, but that was 6 months ago, when the encampment was first being setup'

###  The Iron Company Encampment 
> As you approach the clearing in the dense forest, you spot a small encampment beside a swiftly flowing river. A cliff on the far side of the river overlooks the encampment. The encampment is surrounded by trees on all sides, their leaves casting dappled shadows across the clearing. A few small paths lead from the edge of the clearing to the huts, which are made from rough-hewn timber and thatched with straw. ++ Perception DC 10. |It seems underpopulated, perhaps 2 or 3 people.++
>
>There are 5 or 6 huts in total, arranged in a rough circle in the centre of the clearing. Smoke rises from a small fire pit in the centre, and you can hear the faint murmur of voices as the mercenaries inside chat amongst themselves. The huts themselves appear to be fairly new, and relatively temporary, but still sturdy enough to provide shelter from the elements.

[[Iron Company Encampment]]

Within the camp - there is a lockbox containing 30 gold, and a plan of [[the Temple of Resplendence]], and [[The College of Arcane History|Pinapota Library]].

In the centre of the encampment is a large cage, with a [chimera](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#chimera_mm) in it. It is trapped to release the creature based on magical glyphs on the ground (investigation required to spot them).

There is also a post in the ground with open shackles attached.

###  The Temple of Resplendence 
[[Bishop Fearson]] is dead, and [[Throzzak Dawnfall]] is barely still alive in the main part of the temple. The altar has been forced aside, and a staircase descends into the crypt below.

In the crypt, there are 2 gargoyles, 1 with a wing missing. They will awake and attack as soon as they party enter the room. If they utter the word 'Slumber' (or if they bring Throzzak), the gargoyles will return to their resting position.

The crypt is almost like a museum, with 3 pedestals with a plaque under each one. They read:
* [[Relic of Undeath]]
* [[Chalice of Temptation]]
* [[Shield of Kallor]]
