#  WHAT ACTUALLY HAPPENED 
Players were at site of corrupted Blath, long resting next to the machine. They called Danus, who said he would be over in a couple of hours to meet them. Meanwhile, they were attacked by Bandits, when a platinum dragon (Bahamut) swooped in a attacked the bandidts. He thanked Father T, before saying he was weak, and dissapeared leaving a note for where to find his shrine/blessing. [[Finding Bahamut's Shrine]]

The party rested again, waiting for Danus to show up. He turns up and gives them some info about his research. There are multiple planes, and at some points, aligning with the movement of the moons, the boundaries between the planes are weak. He's not sure why the moons are related. Some cases of 'leaks' between the planes can occurr naturally. This experiment on Blath seemed to have been an intentional use of this, pulling some kind of energy from the 'Astral Plane' in particular, where the undead are known to reside. When asked if this was a power the party should consider embracing, he advised that necromancy and revival magics are not innately bad, but this is not a good way to approach it, as it is dangerous and wrong.

He said he would keep researching using the samples from Blath, and the wolf saliva from Theodore. He would also see if he could get some of these weak points in the area guarded to prevent any innocents getting hurt.

The party headed to find Bahamut's shrine (Theodore was not aware of any temple/shrine to Bahamut despite living in the city for all his life, but Father T had heard rumours that such a shrine might exist).
