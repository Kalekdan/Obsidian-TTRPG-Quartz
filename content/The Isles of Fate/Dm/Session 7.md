###  Forest Journey 
Encampment is ~2 days travel - If they know where it is precisely, DCs are lowered (they can find it using Felrans compass, or if they speak with Erik).

For each day, roll a D20, and if less than 7, they face an encounter (roll a d6).
| 1 | See a unicorn, with a [[Golden Unicorn Horn | golden horn]] drinking from corrupted water - [Sea spawn](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#sea%20spawn_mpmm) crawl out |
| 2 | Thunder storm while sleeping, starts a forest fire - group skill check to see if it delays travel |
| 3 | A tree falls in front of the party - they are approached by a newly sentient pair of bears who have turned to thievery (1 has a sword, 1 has a bow) |
| 4 | See an overturned cart with several chests - there are 3 chests, 2 are [Spitting mimics](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#spitting%20mimic_idrotf), a final chest contains some empty vials, 1 containing poison, and a [[Quick Flask]]. Seems to be an alchemists experiment that went wrong.|

###  Interrogating Blath 
[[The Taxmen Headquarters]]

If they manage to get to Blath - they can interrogate him about the attack on the village, and the history of Bron/Bramble/Echo.
* The Taxmen attacked the village, as they refused to pay the protection money
* Bron was found in a forest, drenched in water, with a large burnt out area around him - he had nothing to his name, except the amulets, and a map, so the Taxmen lent him some money, however it was impossible to pay back and he has been in their debt since

Blath will have the following on him:
* 430g
* 3 gold rings
* Bag of holding:
    * Another 300g
    * A ledger listing most of the businesses in Pinapota, and the money claimed
    * A diary, including the day he found Bron
