The party arrived in Bres with the skyport burning, and encountered the captain of the other ship who helped them at the Matron's request.

They saw his drawings of the Matron, the motherly figure, and the massive tentacled monster.
####  Captains Peril 
As they descend from the skyport, the seemingly mad captain drops at their feet, having apparently killed himself. On his body are more scrawlings from the Matron, and 2 maps/diagrams.
* One shows a stately home with a path leading into the forest behind it.
* Another shows a large machine (Surzaks boundary breaker), with annotations, written in Primordial, stating that the gem in the centre if destroyed will disable the machine.
