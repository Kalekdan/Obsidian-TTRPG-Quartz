The Lover's Rose is one of the flowers tended by the [[Flower Druids]] at [[Wildflower Grove]].

It has pink petals, with red edges. It smells different to everyone, with the scent of what they desire most. Most people consider it one of the most beautiful things they have ever seen, and therefore to receive such a flower as a gift, can go a considerable way in obtaining favour.
