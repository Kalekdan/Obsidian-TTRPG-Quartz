The Relic of Undeath is an extremely rare artefact that contains the instructions for becomming a lich, as well as the incantation required.

> A small stone pyramid sits before you, no larger than your hand. Inscribed on each side is a peculiar script. The presence of this relic fills you with a strong sense of unease. It is cold to the touch, yet you find your hand recoiling as though burned.

It is a pyramid shaped stone, with infernal runes on each side. If someone is able to speak infernal, they can translate it. For each side they wish to translate, they must make a DC 15 wisdom save. If they fail, they take d20 psychic damage, and get 1 level of exhaustion. If they pass they take half as much damage and do not take the exhaustion.

Those in close proximity to the relic, especially if touching it with bare skin, find themselves having evil and unpleasant thoughts as it corrupts them.

- I jywwyd oar zmy wafd pfwz ky wafqmz
- I eazual ao zrilwoarpizual vorv vytih, upkfyv suzm kdaav ao il ullatylz
- Dyizm pfwz erytyvy flvyizm
- Tmy wafd rypiulw
