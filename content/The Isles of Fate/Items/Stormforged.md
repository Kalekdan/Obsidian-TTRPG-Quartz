>Stormforged is a fearsome greatsword that crackles with raw power. Made of a dark, swirling metal that seems to shift and move like storm clouds, the blade is etched with ancient runes that pulse with electric energy. When held, the sword thrums with power, sending shivers down the spine of any who dare to wield it.
>
>With each swing of Stormforged, lightning arcs and crackles along the length of the blade, striking down foes with a fury unmatched by any other weapon. The blade hums with power as it cuts through the air, leaving a trail of sparking energy in its wake. Those who stand in the path of the Stormforged can feel the hairs on their arms and the back of their neck stand up as the sword unleashes its full power.
>
>In the hands of a skilled warrior, Stormforged is a deadly weapon, capable of striking down even the most fearsome opponents with a single blow. The magic imbued within the sword is said to be drawn from the very heart of thunderstorms, making it a weapon of immense power and destruction. Stormforged is not for the faint of heart, but for those brave enough to wield it, it is a tool of unimaginable power and a true force to be reckoned with.

Stormforged is a greatsword, dealing 2d6 slashing damage. On a hit, it deals an additional 2d6 lightning damage. The target is unable to make opportunity attacks until the end of the wielder's turn.
