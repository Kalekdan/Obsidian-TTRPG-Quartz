Requires attunement.

To an untuned user, this blade appears an ordinary curved dagger with an uneven blade, and a wood and gold handle. Once attuned however, if this weapon deals damage to the person who is attuned to it, that damage is reduced to 0. The wound can still be visible to others for intimidation purposes.

Performing this action in front of someone gives advantage on any subsequent intimidation rolls (as long as that person does not know about the daggers ability)
