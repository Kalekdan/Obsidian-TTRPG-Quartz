Requires attunement

Properties:
* Arcane Insight: The wearer gains advantage on Arcana checks.
* Detect Magic: Once per day, the wearer can cast Detect Magic as a free action.
* Legendary Lore: The Eye is said to contain a fragment of the ancient knowledge, allowing the wearer to, once per week, ask one question and receive a cryptic but truthful answer, as per the Divination spell.
