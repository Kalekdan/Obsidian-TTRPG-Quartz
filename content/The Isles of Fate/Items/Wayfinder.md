The Wayfinder is a magnificent, golden compass that radiates with an otherworldly glow. Inlaid with two precious gems, this magical tool is both a work of art and a powerful navigational aid. The first gem is a vibrant blue sapphire set in the center of the compass, radiating with a deep, pulsing energy that seems to guide the needle with ease. The second gem is a deep ruby, set on the lid of the compass, and shimmers in the light with a soft, gentle glow.

As the holder of the Wayfinder gazes at the compass, the needle slowly spins and settles on a direction, pointing towards their desired destination with unerring accuracy. The golden metal of the compass is warm to the touch and imbued with powerful magic, making it a prized possession of any adventurer. The gems inlaid into the Wayfinder are said to hold great power and knowledge, and those who possess this magical compass are believed to have an edge over their rivals in any quest for treasure or discovery.

####  Using Wayfinder 
Requires attunement.

With both gems missing/broken, Wayfinder acts as a normal compass. 
With 1 present, it allows the user to picture a location in their mind for 1 hour, and will point to that location. If both are present, it allows the user to picture an individual, and it will point to their location.

Once within a few miles of the target, the compass will spin randomly.
