##  Story 
###  Overarching Premise 
[[Surzak]] is a Lich. His child became ill, so he started studying the arcane to save him, but was growing old himself. His elf wife was still 'young' and had a long time to live. He decided to become a lich to live with her, and try and save their son. She was appalled by what he did, and in a fight is killed. He is now searching how to reverse his lichdom, and once done, will attempt to bring her back.

Since she does not wish to be revived, he is weakening the boundary to the astral plane, resulting in breaches where deities are able to interact more with the material plane (often through speaking through others).

This is in fact all overseen by the demon from the astral plane who is using Surzak and the party to break down the borders so that he and his army can invade the material plane.
