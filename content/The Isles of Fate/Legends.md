[[Hircia]] has a rich history of myths and legends, some possibly based on truth, some not so much. A lot of the stories surround the rumoured lands beyond the endless sea which surrounds the known world.

###  The Sunken City 
Buried under the Stricken Sea, just of the NE coast of the Kingdom of Errun, there is rumoured to be a whole city. It is said that the island was pulled under the sea by a monster the size of a city. It was a city of great beauty and prosperity, a hub of trade and commerce that drew people from all corners of the world. But its fate was sealed when a giant sea monster emerged from the depths, drawn by the sound of the city's bustling harbor.

The beast was enormous, with tentacles that could wrap around a ship and drag it down to the ocean floor. It attacked the city with ferocity, pulling buildings and streets under the waves with its tentacles and devouring anyone who tried to escape.

The people of the city fought bravely against the monster, but they were no match for its might. In the end, the city was pulled under the waves, lost to the deeps of the ocean forever.

But the legend of the sunken city lives on, passed down through the ages by those who still remember its glory. Some say that the city is still out there, hidden beneath the waves and waiting for the day when it can rise again. Others claim that the monster still lurks in the deeps, guarding the city and its secrets from those who would seek to uncover them.

###  The Lost Land of Jotunheimr 
The legend of [[The Lost Land of Jotunheimr|the lost land of jotunheimr]] has been passed down through the ages, whispered in hushed tones around campfires and told as bedtime stories to wide-eyed children. It is said that in a time long ago, there existed a land unlike any other, where a race of giant beings lived in harmony with the earth and its magic.

These giants were said to possess an advanced understanding of magic, using it to shape the land and create wonders beyond imagination. They built towering cities of marble and gold, with spires that reached up to the very heavens themselves. The fields were filled with crops that grew with the aid of magic, and the air was filled with the sound of music and laughter.

But as with all things, the time of the giants eventually came to an end. It is said that the giants simply vanished from the land, leaving their cities and wonders behind. The location of their lost land is now unknown, and it is said that only the giants themselves know where it lies.

Despite their disappearance, many believe that the giants still exist, hiding among the mortal races and waiting for the day when they can reclaim their lost land. Some say that they can still be found in the far corners of the world, guarding the secrets of their ancient civilization and waiting for the day when they can return home.

##  Creation Stories 
###  The Tools of Creation 
Some believe that [[Hircia]] was created by a group of 3 deities each with a powerful tool. The combination of them all resulted in the known world. The 3 moons over [[Hircia]] are often attributed to each of them.
- Plough (The Farmer) - Carved the land
- Flask (The Matron) - Flooded the seas
- Sword (The Warrior) - Created life and death
These artefacts are rumoured to still exist, scattered around the land. Many temples claim to hold one or multiple of these prestigious tools.
