'Haven' is a settlement in [[The Northern Wilds]] which is built on a series of interconnected platforms that are suspended high above the jungle canopy. These platforms are connected by a network of rope bridges, making the city feel like a giant treehouse. The platforms are all made of wood, and they are surrounded by thick vines that have grown up the sides of the structures over time.

Haven is home to a diverse population of humans, elves, dwarves, and other fantastical creatures who all coexist in harmony. The city is known for its vibrant culture and its abundant resources, which are all sourced from the surrounding jungle. The citizens of Haven are skilled at harvesting exotic fruits, nuts, and herbs from the jungle, and they are also skilled at hunting and fishing in the surrounding waterways.

The city is ruled by a council of wise elders who are chosen from among the city's most respected citizens. They make all major decisions for the city, and they are responsible for maintaining order and ensuring that the city's resources are used wisely.

One of the city's most unique features is its "jungle library," which is a massive treehouse structure filled with books and scrolls that have been collected from all over the jungle. The library is tended by a group of scholarly elves who are responsible for cataloguing and preserving the city's vast collection of knowledge.

Overall, Haven is a bustling, vibrant city that is rich in culture and resources, and it is a safe haven for those seeking adventure in the midst of the wild jungle.
