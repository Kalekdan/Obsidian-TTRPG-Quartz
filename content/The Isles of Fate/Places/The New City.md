The New City is the name given to the wealthy and well developed central portions of the city of [[Pinapota]]. It is home to the [[The College of Arcane History|College of Arcane History]], known for it's immense library, as well as the [[Pinapota Bardic Academy]].

It is full of large white stone buildings.

Amongst the houses in the New City, you can find the ever popular market, with the temple opposite. The head of the clergy in there is [[Bishop Fearson]].
