In [[Pinapota]] industrial district, [[The Taxmen]] take up residence in an abandoned warehouse.

#### Ground Floor:

* Entrance: The main entrance is guarded by two burly enforcers. Visitors are thoroughly searched and questioned before being allowed inside.
* Reception Area: A small office space with a receptionist desk and waiting area. A secretary manages appointments and screens visitors.
* Meeting Room: A spacious room where the mob boss conducts business meetings with lieutenants and other high-ranking members.
* Training Area: A section of the warehouse converted into a makeshift training ground for the mob's recruits. Various weapons and combat equipment are scattered around
* Storage: A large room filled with crates and boxes, containing supplies, stolen goods, and illicit substances.
* Guards' Quarters: A room where the guards take breaks and rest between shifts.

#### Second Floor:

* Boss's Office: A lavishly furnished office where the mob boss conducts private meetings, manages operations, and keeps sensitive documents.
* Lieutenants' Quarters: Private rooms for the mob boss's most trusted lieutenants, where they plan operations and discuss strategy.
* Surveillance Room: A space equipped with monitors and surveillance equipment, allowing the mob boss to monitor the warehouse and its surroundings.
* Interrogation Room: A dimly lit room with various tools and restraints, where captured enemies and traitors are questioned.
* Hideout: A secret room concealed behind a bookshelf or a false wall, serving as a secure hideout for the mob boss during emergencies.
* Private Lounge: A luxurious lounge area with a well-stocked bar, reserved for the mob boss and important guests.

####  Basement: 

* Armory: A heavily guarded room housing an assortment of weapons, ammunition, and armor for the mob's operations.
* Treasure Vault: A secure chamber where the mob boss stores their most valuable possessions, including stolen artifacts, gems, and large amounts of money.
* Alchemy Lab: A hidden laboratory where the mob boss's alchemist conducts experiments and creates potions or illicit substances.
* Prison Cells: Small, cramped cells for holding captured individuals or enemies of the mob boss. Guards are stationed here at all times.
* Secret Exit: A concealed passage or tunnel leading to an escape route or a nearby location.

####  Occupants: 

* Mob Boss (Blath): The head of the organization, powerful and cunning.
* Lieutenants: Trusted and skilled individuals who manage different aspects of the mob's operations.
* Enforcers: Well-trained fighters responsible for protecting the headquarters and enforcing the mob boss's orders.
* Thugs: Lower-ranked members of the mob, involved in various criminal activities.
* Secretary/Receptionist: Handles administrative tasks and screens visitors.
* Guards: Patrol the premises, ensuring security and handling potential threats.
* Alchemist: Responsible for creating potions, poisons, or illegal substances.
* Prison Guards: In charge of maintaining and guarding the prison cells.
