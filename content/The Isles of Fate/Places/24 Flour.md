The smell is intoxicating, but the amount of flour in the air makes it difficult not to cough. Behind one of the tables, stands a large half-orc wearing a read shirt and pristine white apron.

24 Flour is a bakery in [[Pinapota]] run by [[Krig Krumbs]] and his twin [[Krissy Krumbs]]. It is open 24 hours a day, and the shifts are alternated between them.
