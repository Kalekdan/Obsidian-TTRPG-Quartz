Pinapota is the capital of [[The Kingdom of Errun| Errun]], and home to the primary residence of the [[The King of Errun|King of Errun]] in [[Pinapota Keep]].

The centre of the city is heavily developed, with winding stone streets and many tall building, but beyond this is a huge area of slums and poverty. There is a strong resentment both ways between those that live in the smart [[The New City|'New City']], and those living in the surrounding properties.

In the New City are several wealthy establishments, including [[The College of Arcane History]], as well as the widely recognised [[Pinapota Bardic Academy]].

[[Pinapota Surrounding Area| Surrounding Pinapota]] is a large farming district, which is also where a large portion the the less affluent population of Pinapota reside. Near the river, one can find the [[Pinapota Metalworks]], one of two blacksmiths in Pinapota, and the tavern, '[[The Rusty Anvil]]' opposite.
