The College of Arcane History, situated in central [[Pinapota]], is one of the larger estabilshments on the Isles of Fate where one can commit to a life of study of the arcane. It has one of the largest libraries on the continents, with a vast collection of historical tomes.

They also sell a collection of spell tomes for each school of magic. At the end of the library is a small circular room with 8 doors coming off it, for each of the schools of magic. In the middle of the room is a [[Faana Shelt | librarian]]. The 8 doors are as follows:
* [[Conjuration Study]]
    * [[Danus Jusco]] works here, is currently on an [[Owlbear Hoard | expedition researching boundaries between planes]].
* [[Necromancy Study]]
    * Door is locked with a purple glowing padlock
* [[Evocation Study]]
* [[Abjuration Study]]
* [[Transmutation Study]]
* [[Divination Study]]
* [[Enchantment Study]]
* [[Illusion Study]]
