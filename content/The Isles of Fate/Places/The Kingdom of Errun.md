The Kingdom of Errun is the central island in the Isles of Fate, and is occupied by a very traditional monarchy, who lay claim to the rest of the continents. It is an incredibly wealthy state, as due to it's central location, it is a hive of trade.

Though Errun is techincally one of the most powerful states within the Isles of Fate, most other groups do not consider them a threat, as the monarchy are quite complacent, and live in luxury, living off the vast riches that their interconnected trade routes provide them. The other states would not neccesarily benefit from the removal of Errun either, since most trade of any significance also goes via the Kingdom of Errun.

The capital of Errun is [[Pinapota]], and is where the heavily fortified [[Pinapota Keep]] exists. The keep is the primary residence of the [[The King of Errun|King of Errun]], though he is rarely seen.
On the northern coast sits one of the busiest shipping ports, [[Roseport]], which is the primary location through which trade with [[The Northern Wilds]] is conducted.

Just to the north east of Errun is the infamous [[The Stricken Sea|Stricken Sea]], a particularly dangerous stretch of water that makes trade with the [[Republic of Teshi]] particularly difficult.
