[[Pinapota]] Market is situated in the centre of the '[[The New City|Pinapota New City]]'. Almost every day there is a few stalls there, and additional stalls pop up once a week for the larger market.

Some of the regulars include:
* [[Adriana Truethorne]] - Hunter/Butcher
* [[Krig Krumbs]] - Baker ([[24 Flour]])
* [[Starnley Higgs]] - Trinkets (Pawn) ([[The Tin Box]])
* [[Willican O'Reilly]] - Merchant ([[Willican's Wares]])
