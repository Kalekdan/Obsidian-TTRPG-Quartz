Pinapota Metalworks is one of two blacksmiths in [[Pinapota]], the other being [[Sacrificing Steel]] in the [[The New City|Pinapota New City]]. It prides itself on being able to produce weapons and armour at the same or higher quality than the more expensive smiths in the centre.

It is worked by [[Saffron Brownlock]], a halfing woman, who was raised in settlements surrounding the city.
