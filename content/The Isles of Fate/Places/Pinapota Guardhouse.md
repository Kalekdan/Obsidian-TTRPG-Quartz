The Pinapota guardhouse is located just south of the [[The College of Arcane History|college of arcane history]], and is where the guards report to at the start and end of their shift.

There is a strong security presence of guards in [[Pinapota]] as the King attempts to exert his power, and enforce strict laws.

The guards often patrol the streets of Pinapota, and also perform the role of tax collection. They have been known to raid the old town to enforce laws, though it is often just a show of power.

The higher ranked guards have armoured big cats which patrol with them, making an escape very challenging.
