> Roseport is a bustling coastal city located on the shores of a calm, crystal-clear ocean. The city is built on a series of small, rocky islands connected by a network of bridges and causeways. The main island is home to the city's bustling harbor, where a constant stream of ships can be seen coming and going, bringing goods from all over the world.
>
>The city is built on a series of hills, with narrow, winding streets leading up to the top of the highest peak, where a grand castle can be seen overlooking the city. The castle is surrounded by lush gardens and rolling green fields, a stark contrast to the busy, crowded streets of the city below.
>
>The city is a hub of trade and commerce, with merchants and traders from all over the world coming to do business. >The streets are lined with stalls and shops selling all manner of goods, from fresh seafood and produce to exotic spices and rare artifacts. The air is filled with the sounds of haggling and the smells of cooking food and saltwater.
>
>Despite the hustle and bustle of the city, there is a sense of community and belonging among the people of Roseport. They take pride in their city and its history, and are always ready to welcome visitors with open arms.

Roseport is the busy port on the north coast of [[The Kingdom of Errun]]. It is where most of the trade is conducted between Errun and [[The Northern Wilds]]. A lot of smuggling also goes on around Roseport, via the less regulated entrances a few miles down the coast, nicknamed [[The Thorny Coves]].

One of the larger coves ([[Scuttle Cove]]), only accessible from the sea hosts the a stilted fishing community.
