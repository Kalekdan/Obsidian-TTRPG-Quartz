Heirloom Apparel is a very upmarket clothing shop in the [[The New City|Pinapota New City]] run by [[Risha Highvale]]. It is well known by the nobles of the city, and is one of the favoured locations by the elites.

###  DM Notes 
++ Show (Warning Spoilers!)|
Recently her shop has been cursed by a fiend causing most of the clothes to curse those wearing them. The fiend told [[Risha Highvale |Risha]] that if she is able to curse enough people, the remaining curses will be lifted (this is not true). See quest *[[A Fair Price]]*.
