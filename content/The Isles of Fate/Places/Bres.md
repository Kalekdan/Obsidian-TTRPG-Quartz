Bres is an extravagant city, renowned for its opulent architecture and lavish lifestyle. Located in a prime location, the city holds a great deal of power and influence over nearby states. The city is home to a large number of aristocrats and wealthy individuals, who flaunt their wealth and status at every opportunity.

However, the wealth disparity in Bres is stark, with a large portion of the population living in poverty. The streets of Bres are lined with grand mansions and palaces, each more ornate and grandiose than the last, while just a few blocks away, impoverished neighborhoods struggle to make ends meet.

Most notably about Bres is the vast artificial lake which sits at centre which the city is built around. This lake is often used for the wealthy who enjoy recreational sports on it. There is also a huge lighthouse in the centre which towers over the rest of the city.

Despite its extravagance, Bres is also a city of culture and refinement. The arts thrive in Bres, with theaters, opera houses, and galleries dotting the city. The city is also home to a number of world-class museums, showcasing the rich history and cultural heritage of the region. However, these cultural offerings are often out of reach for the poor and marginalized communities in Bres.

Overall, Bres is a city of great wealth and refinement, where the elite of society come to live, work, and play. But for many, the city is a harsh and unforgiving place, where the gap between the rich and the poor is vast and deeply entrenched.
