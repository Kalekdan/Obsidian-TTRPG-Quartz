Sacrificing Steel is a large blacksmiths in the [[The New City | 'New City']] in [[Pinapota]]. It is responsible for smithing swords and armor for the entire [[The Kingdom of Errun | Errun]] army. It is the largest of the two blacksmiths in [[Pinapota]], the other being [[Pinapota Metalworks]].

The lead blacksmith is [[Castien Kelmys]].
