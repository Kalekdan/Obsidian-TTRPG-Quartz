# Fauna
###  Ocean & Lakes 
Due to the large proportion of seas and lakes around the Isles of Fate, it will come as no surprise that a large number of creatures call these places home. They are not all friendly however, and can provide a challenge for any intrepid adventurer looking to claim the seas:
* [[Mer-people]]
* [[Algrex]] - "The pack hunting squid"
###  Forest & Jungle 
There are a few dense jungles on the islands, which are largely uninhabited and untouched. This has allowed for a wide variety of creatures to thrive:
* [[Nitari]] - "The glowing birds"
* [[Carpet Leech]] - "The carnivorous quicksand"
* [[Glitor Frog]] - "The winged frog"
