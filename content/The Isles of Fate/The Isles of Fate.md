#### Introduction 
The Isles of Fate is a fantasy setting for a D&D campaign. The goal of the wiki is to track the status of the world to ensure the campaign remains consistent. It will also be used to log campaign sessions, to understand what the players have learned and who they've met etc.

#### Setting 
The Isles of Fate is set on a world called [[Hircia]]. The known world is made up of a collection of 3 large islands, with a smaller island at the centre. This is surrounded by an archipelago and reef reef leading on to rough sees. Very little exploration has been done beyond the boundaries of the reef, so nobody is certain how far the seas extend.

The 4 main landmasses at the centre of the habited world the are known as:
* [[The Northern Wilds]]
* [[The Gem of the West]]
* [[The Eastern Isle]]
* [[The Kingdom of Errun]]
The surrounding islands, are mostly inhabited by smaller settlements, or have no colonies at all. The majority of those with permanent outposts, are considered part of the [[The Singualian Territories|Singualian Territories]], a group of independent states, who do not conform to any of the powers in the central landmasses.

The [[Flora & Fauna|fauna and flora]] on and around the Isles of Fate is very varied due to the huge assortment of climates and terrains between the islands.

There are 3 [[Moons of Hircia |moons]] in the skies over [[Hircia]].

Magic is present all over [[Hircia]], but the extent to which it is used can vary - from the very utilitarian study of magic as a science in [[Pinapota]], to the much more expressive and less restrictive use in locations such as [[Roseport]].
