Orlanthus is a half-giant arcane engineer [[The Lost Land of Jotunheimr | Jotunheimr]] and father of [[Bramble]].

He was working on strengthening the boundaries between the planes when [[The Lost Land of Jotunheimr | Jotunheimr]] was flooded by a huge wave. In the chaos, Orlanthus managed to send Bramble, who was an infant, away with a magical spell.
