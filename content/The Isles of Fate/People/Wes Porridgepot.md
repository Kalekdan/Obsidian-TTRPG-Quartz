Wes Porridgepot is the tavernkeep at [[The Swift Steed]] in [[Pinapota]]. It is a much more formal establishment than [[the Rusty Anvil]] in the old town.

Wes is a very personable barman as long as you fit the criteria for the kinds of people he wants in his bar. He is very aware of the image he wishes to portray with his clientele, so has very little time for rowdy or drunk patrons.
