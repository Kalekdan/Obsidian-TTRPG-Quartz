Saffron Brownlock is a hafling woman, who works as a blacksmith in [[Pinapota Metalworks]] in the outskirts of [[Pinapota]]. She has been working at the forge since being a small girl, after being orphaned at a young age, and raised by the previous blacksmith.

She is married to [[Otto Brownlock]], who owns [[the Rusty Anvil]], the tavern opposite [[Pinapota Metalworks]].
