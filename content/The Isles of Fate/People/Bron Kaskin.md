Bron Kaskin is a member of [[The Taxmen]], and distant descendant of the giants from [[The Lost Land of Jotunheimr|the lost land of the giants]].

He borrowed some money, and when he couldn't pay back the huge interest, they decided to employ him as muscle due to his large stature - he has been working for them since, and is no closer to paying of the debt..
