Elword is the son of [[Surzak]] and [[Fayeth Valrel]]. He passed away of an unknown disease, and became the catalyst for [[Surzak]]'s transformation into a lich.

++++ DM Notes |
The disease was actually caused by a devil from the astral plane who is using [[Surzak]] as a pawn to implement his plan to return to the material plane.
