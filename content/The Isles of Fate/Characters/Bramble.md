++++ Tom Original Raw notes |
ramble is a quarter goliath and 3 quarters human, this has resulted in him being very large of stature. He was found abandoned as a baby with a single locket with a specific inscription. He was found by a halfling rambler and his wife, they took him in and raised him as their own. Over the years he grew and his lack of intelligence found him in trouble on multiple occasions, his strength also found him as the village work horse. When he was a young man a band of raiders attacked his village and the leader of the raiders killed Ram's adoptive father but in the heat of the raid the chief raider dropped a locket with the same inscription. Ram assumed it was his brother and decided to set out to find out more about his parentage. He travelled and had many adventures, he often found himself being taken advantage of because of his low intelligence. His incredible strength and fighting ability earned him a reputation.
