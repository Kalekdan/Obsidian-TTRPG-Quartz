## Introduction
The Price of Progress is a fantasy D&D campaign. The goal of the wiki is to track the status of the world to ensure the campaign remains consistent. It will also be used to log campaign sessions, to understand what the players have learned and who they've met etc.

The campaign begins in the capital city of [[Aetheria]], a large magically advanced locale, in the midst of a golden age, and the celebration of it's bi-annual [[Radiance Festival]].
## Setting
The Price of Progress is set in the world of [[Hircia]], a setting initially designed for [[The Isles of Fate]]. The campaign is primarily focused in [[Jotunheimr]] before the events of [[The Isles of Fate]], in the year 375, and as such, before it's downfall in the year 492 ([[Hircia|see the timeline of Hircia here]]).

[[Jotunheimr]] is a reasonably small island continent, far beyond the main landmasses of [[The Isles of Fate]]. It consists of a large landmass, with a few towns and cities. By far the most populous, is the capital city of [[Aetheria]], which sits at the literal and figurative centre of the civilisation.
