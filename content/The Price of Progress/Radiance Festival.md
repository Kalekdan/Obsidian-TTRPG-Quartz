The Radiance Festival is a celebration in [[Aetheria]] every few years, to celebrate the achievements and innovations of the people of [[Jotunheimr]]. It is a 'World Fair' of sorts, and many unique creations are unveiled every year. This year, amongst other, there is a lot of expectation for the reveal of a so called '[[Celestial Nexus]]', which has promised to change how [[Aetheria|Aetherian's]] live forever.

## What is on display?

There are a number of large and significant displays of innovation, as well as some smaller displays which the residents and visitors can enjoy.

These will be left up for the duration of the festival (could be a fun location for some combat with changing weather) |
Smaller displays include:
- **Floating lanterns** - The city is lit with glowing glass balls which float around the streets and between the islands.
- **Elemental constructs** - Small, animated constructs representing the four elements (earth, air, fire, water) that perform simple tasks or tricks, delighting festival-goers
- **Potion brewing demonstrations**
- **[[The Sky Bazaar]]'s Curiosities**
- **Illusionary Art Gallery** - A gallery featuring magical paintings and sculptures that change appearance based on the viewer's perspective, showcasing the talents of local artists.
- **Elemental Cuisine Tasting** - A culinary showcase featuring dishes inspired by the four elements, such as fire-roasted meats, water-infused desserts, and earth-based vegetarian options.
### Rumours of subterfuge
As the ceremony approaches, rumours of sabotage and political manipulation arise. The players can investigate these rumours, uncovering a conspiracy that threatens to disrupt the festival and seize control of the Nexus.
