The Veils are a secretive crime syndicate based out of [[The Whispering Exchange]] beneath lake [[Aethermere]]. They seem to have a large amount of power over the politics of [[Aetheria]] though their members are mostly unknown, even to themselves due to their decision to always wear veils.
