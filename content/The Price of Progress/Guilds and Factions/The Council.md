The Council are the ruling power in [[Aetheria]]. They are made up of 20 powerful individuals from around the city, mostly nobles. They are self elected, and the majority of their members are made up of [[The Arcanist Consortium]]. The Council are responsible for the running of the city including maintaining [[the Heart of Aetheria]] and other magical infrastructure. They also make any key decisions in the city.

They are based out of [[The Council Spire]], one of the buildings in the central district.

Some of their members include:
- [[Councillor Darian Halcyon]] - nobleman and councillor
- [[Captain Therrik Stonehand]] - Head of [[the Watch]] (the guards of [[Aetheria]])
