Jotunheimr is a reasonably small island continent, far beyond the main landmasses of [[The Isles of Fate]]. It consists of a large landmass, with a few towns and cities. By far the most populous, is the capital city of [[Aetheria]], which sits at the literal and figurative centre of the civilisation.

At the north of the continent, is a large forest, called the [[Mistwood]], and a settlement in there known as [[Mistwood Haven]].

The south of the island features a large bay, known as the [[Hollow Basin]], and a mountain range making exploration of the southern portion of the continent not for the faint of heart.

It's population is about 50% giant, or giant-like, but most of the cities are populated with individuals who are a mix of races and species. The more remote settlements are where you might encounter more traditional 20ft 'giants'.
