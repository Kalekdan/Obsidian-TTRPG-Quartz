Aetheria is the capital city of [[Jotunheimr]], and the most populous settlement on the continent.

Aetheria is a city of floating buildings and landmasses, held aloft by ancient magic crystals that harness the power of the elements. The city is a marvel of engineering and magic, with intricate bridges connecting the floating structures. A series of massive, floating crystals orbit the city, casting colourful light and creating a mesmerizing display in the sky, dispersing magic to all the different [[Districts of Aetheria|districts]] (Central, North, South, East and West). The magic is powered by a huge [[The Heart of Aetheria|elemental fire]] in the central island. The city hovers in the sky above the great lake [[Aethermere]] which bubbles at random intervals, taking on a milky appearance from above when doing so.

**Merchants:**
* [[The Sky Bazaar]] - A sprawling, multi-levelled marketplace featuring floating stalls held aloft by minor levitation crystals. Merchants sell rare magical items, enchanted artifacts, and exotic goods from across [[Jotunheimr]].
* [[Gilded Promenade]] - A luxurious shopping district for the wealthy, lined with opulent boutiques offering custom spellbound jewellery, magical clothing, and rare tomes.
* [[The Whispering Exchange]] - A hidden black-market hub located in a series of caves beneath the lake. Traders deal in illicit goods, forbidden tomes, and unregistered magical relics.

**Entertainment:**
* [[The Shifting Amphitheatre]] - A grand venue where performers showcase magical plays, illusions, and concerts. The shapeshifting stage shifts in form, adapting to each performance.

**Inns/Taverns/Brothels:**
* [[The Emberglass Tavern]] - A renowned establishment where bards weave tales of old, accompanied by enchanted instruments that create dazzling light shows.
* [[Silken Shadows]] - A discreet, high-class brothel where employees are renowned for their charm and use of subtle glamour magic.

**Craftsmen:**
* [[The Aetherforge]] - A legendary smithy where master craftsmen work alongside enchanters to create powerful weapons, armour, and tools.
* [[Prism Glassworks]] - Specializing in creating magically imbued glassware and mirrors that double as magical scrying devices.
* [[Runes & Relics]] - A workshop where artisans craft runestones and repair ancient artifacts brought in by scholars and adventurers. Slightly more 'bric-a-brac' than some of the more established shops.
.
**Government Buildings:**
* [[The Council Spire]] - The seat of Aetheria’s ruling council, a towering structure with floating chambers used for diplomatic meetings and legal proceedings.
* [[The Archives]] - A vast library and record hall preserving treaties, laws, and historical records, guarded by enchanted constructs.
* [[The Ministry of Magitech]] - An administrative building overseeing the regulation and development of magical technology across the city.

**Public Services:**
* [[The Luminary Post]] - A magical postal service delivering messages via enchanted scrolls and small flying constructs.

**Security:**
* [[The Sentinel Towers]] - Watchtowers positioned around the city with wards that detect unauthorized magic use and intrusions.
* [[The Aegis Vault]] - A fortified building used to store confiscated magical artifacts and high-security prisoners.
* [[The Watch]] - A highly trained city guard tasked with maintaining order, often equipped with magically enhanced weapons and armour.

**Religion:**
* [[The Grove of the Old Ones]] - A sacred area in the jungle outskirts, honouring ancient, primal forces through naturalistic rituals.

### Guilds & Factions
- [[The Council]] - The primary ruling power in [[Aetheria]], based out of [[the Council Spire]]. They appoint their own members and successors, a large number of whom are in [[The Arcanist Consortium]].
- [[The Arcanist Consortium]] - A powerful faction of mages focused on pushing the boundaries of magical knowledge and innovation. They have many members in the council, and therefore have a lot of influence in [[Aetheria]], especially for a private organisation.
- [[The Gilded Compass]] - A guild of merchants and explorers who control trade routes and seek new lands to expand Aetheria’s influence.
- [[The Stone Aegis]] - A militant group devoted to protecting the city from external threats, funded and controlled by [[The Arcanist Consortium]]. They ride flying constructs as they patrol the outskirts of [[Aetheria]].
- [[The Veils]] - A secretive criminal syndicate, based out of [[The Whispering Exchange]] beneath lake [[Aethermere]].

### Notable Individuals
- [[Archmage Lyssandra Vale]] - The head of [[The Arcanist Consortium]], known for her ground-breaking research in elemental magic.    
- [[Captain Therrik Stonehand]] - Leader of [[The Watch]], a pragmatic and respected figure with a deep sense of justice.
- [[Eira Solwyn]] - A famous bard and performer at [[The Emberglass Tavern]], rumoured to have ties to [[The Whispering Exchange]].
- [[Councillor Darian Halcyon]] - A charismatic diplomat and key figure in [[The Council]], often navigating political intrigue.
