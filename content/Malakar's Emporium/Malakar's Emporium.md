#### **Puzzle Setup**

The vault door is covered in **five Infernal names**, each etched into the stone. Players must **speak the three correct names** to open the door. If they speak the wrong names, the vault **lashes out with magical backlash** (a pulse of necrotic energy).

#### **The Names on the Door:**

1. **Azarith, The Wise**
2. **Baelor, The Deceiver**
3. **Crivash, The Mighty**
4. **Dravok, The Gambler**
5. **Ezeroth, The Martyr**

#### **How to Solve It:**

- The players must **deduce the three right names** based on previous clues.
    
- Throughout the vault, they find **Infernal texts** referencing these figures:    
    - _Azarith unlocked knowledge beyond the gods._ (Confirms **Knowledge** is correct.)
    - _Crivash crushed mountains with his strength._ (Confirms **Dominion** is correct.)
    - _Ezeroth’s sacrifice spared his people._ (Confirms **Sacrifice** is correct.)
    - _Baelor whispered lies that burned an empire._ (Hints **Deception is BAD**.)
    - _Dravok let fate decide his path, and fate failed him._ (Hints **Chaos is BAD**.)

#### **Failure Penalty:**

- If a player speaks a wrong name, a **lash of infernal energy** (3d6 necrotic damage) erupts from the door, and they must wait **1 minute** before trying again.
    
- If they speak **all five names at once**, the door expels a **shockwave**, knocking the party backward and dealing **5d6 damage to all**.
