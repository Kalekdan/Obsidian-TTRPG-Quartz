## Characters: 
### Elara 
Elara is a skilled and cunning gentlewoman thief with a reputation for her audacious heists and meticulous planning. Raised in the streets, she honed her agility, stealth, and lock-picking abilities, making her an expert in acquiring valuable artefacts and outmanoeuvring her adversaries. With her quick wit, resourcefulness, and unwavering determination, Elara navigates the criminal underworld with finesse, always staying one step ahead of those who seek to capture her.

### Kaelen  

## Plot Summary 
Kaelen is a bard on a pirate ship, but when it is attacked by assassins, he flees. He meets Elara amidst a failed heist, pursued by a group of thugs - Someone had tipped them off. He ‘accidentally’ saves her, and in return, asks her assistance in lying low.

When the assassins locate their hideout, Elara demands Kaelen explain the situation.

Elara, a skilled gentlewoman thief with a penchant for acquiring rare artefacts, crosses paths with Kaelen, a bard who harbours a hidden power rooted in Soulweaving. Unbeknownst to Kaelen, he possesses a fragment of an ancient artefact sought by an enigmatic organisation known as the Whisperbound, masters of Whispershroud magic.

As Elara and Kaelen form an unlikely alliance, they discover that the artefact fragment Kaelen possesses has the potential to unlock great power when combined with the essence threads woven by Soulweavers. This revelation sets them on a perilous journey to safeguard the fragment and unravel its true purpose.

In their quest, Elara and Kaelen must navigate a treacherous world filled with political intrigue, where the Whisperbound relentlessly pursue them to reclaim the artefact. Elara's skills as a gentlewoman thief prove invaluable in acquiring information and outwitting their pursuers, while Kaelen gradually taps into his latent Soulweaving abilities, weaving essence threads to aid their cause.

Together, Elara and Kaelen encounter various factions that seek to control the artefact's power. They must make alliances, decipher cryptic prophecies, and confront their own doubts and insecurities. As the stakes escalate, they uncover a deeper connection between Soulweaving and Whispershroud, realising that the two magic systems hold the key to a greater cosmic balance.

In the climactic showdown, Elara and Kaelen must harness their combined skills and magic to prevent the artefact from falling into the wrong hands. As they confront the leaders of the Whisperbound, they unleash a potent fusion of Soulweaving and Whispershroud, unleashing a wave of harmony and silence that disrupts the enemy's plans and reveals the truth behind their intentions.

Ultimately, Elara and Kaelen's journey not only shapes their destinies but also unveils a profound connection between the magic systems and their place in the world. Their partnership serves as a catalyst for understanding and uniting the diverse realms of Soulweaving and Whispershroud, bringing balance to a fractured land and paving the way for a new era of magic and harmony.

## Magic System 
### Soulweaving 
Essence Threads are invisible, ethereal strands that flow through the world, connecting all living beings together. Each individual possesses their unique set of threads, representing their essence, personality traits, and potential.
Soulweavers are individuals who possess the innate ability to perceive and manipulate Essence Threads.
* **Threadseers:** They can see and read the threads, deciphering the emotions, memories, and intentions of others.
* **Threadshapers:** They can manipulate and mould threads, altering emotions, influencing actions, and even mending or severing connections.
* **Threadspinners:** They can create new threads, weaving them into intricate patterns to enhance physical abilities or grant temporary powers.
#### Limits and Consequences 
Soulweaving requires a deep understanding of the interconnectedness of souls and considerable mental and emotional discipline. Overuse or misuse of Soulweaving can lead to severe consequences, such as physical and mental exhaustion, loss of empathy, or even the unravelling of one's own threads, resulting in the loss of identity or insanity.

### Whisperbound
The whisperbound is an magical ability which can be taught. It is a language which when spoken enables the speaker to control sound:
- Prevent someone from making sound
- Place a zone of silence
- Make someone temporarily deaf/silent
