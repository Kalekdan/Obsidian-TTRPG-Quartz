Inspired by https://youtu.be/CIeug7Uz3Dc?t=397 - a list of possible terrain changes that can happen during combat to make it more interesting. Effectively adding flavour to the following changes:
- Difficult terrain
- Cover
- Sight
- Inaccessible terrain
- Knockback
- Ensnaring
- Moveable obstruction
- Immoveable obstruction

| Name                    | Category               | Environment   | Description                                                                                                             |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------- | ------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Swarm of insects        | Sight                  | Jungle        | A buzzing swarm suddenly emerges from disturbed undergrowth, partially obscuring vision and making targeting difficult. |
| Fallen tree             | Inaccessible terrain   | Forest/Jungle | A tree crashes down mid-battle, blocking a previously usable path or separating combatants.                             |
| Sudden downpour         | Sight                  | Plains        | A storm rolls in rapidly, reducing visibility and turning terrain slick.                                                |
| Collapsing scaffold     | Knockback              | City          | A nearby structure gives way, sending debris flying and knocking back anyone nearby.                                    |
| Swinging boom           | Knockback              | At Sea        | A strong gust of wind swings the sail boom, potentially hitting combatants and knocking them prone.                     |
| Rolling boulder         | Moveable obstruction   | Mountain      | A boulder dislodges and rolls through the battlefield, altering movement paths and crushing anything in its path.       |
| Rising fog              | Sight                  | Coast         | Thick fog rolls in from the ocean, obscuring vision for both ranged and melee combatants.                               |
| Cave-in                 | Immoveable obstruction | Underground   | Part of the ceiling collapses, permanently blocking part of the cavern and creating new cover.                          |
| Flash flood             | Difficult terrain      | Desert/Canyon | A sudden wall of water rushes through, making terrain difficult and washing unsecured combatants away.                  |
| Animated vines          | Ensnaring              | Forest        | Vines suddenly animate and attempt to entangle creatures in a specified area.                                           |
| Bursting steam vent     | Knockback              | Underground   | A natural vent bursts open, knocking back anyone in the blast radius and creating scalding terrain.                     |
| Sudden dust storm       | Sight                  | Desert        | Wind kicks up a violent dust storm, obscuring all vision beyond a few feet.                                             |
| Ship tilts violently    | Difficult terrain      | At Sea        | The ship heels over due to rough waves, causing everyone to struggle for footing.                                       |
| Collapsing balcony      | Inaccessible terrain   | City          | A balcony crumbles during combat, stranding or injuring those on it.                                                    |
| Exploding barrel        | Moveable obstruction   | City/Ship     | A barrel explodes (perhaps from fire), damaging nearby creatures and scattering debris.                                 |
| Slippery algae          | Difficult terrain      | Coast         | Algae is exposed or disturbed, making the area extremely slick.                                                         |
| Lightning strike        | Immoveable obstruction | Plains        | Lightning hits a tree or structure, setting it ablaze and creating new cover or hazards.                                |

| Fleeing animal stampede | Knockback              | Plains/Forest | A herd of frightened animals charges through, trampling and shoving combatants.                                         |
| Exploding spores        | Sight                  | Jungle        | A puffball fungus is disturbed, filling the air with blinding spores.                                                   |
| Rising tide             | Inaccessible terrain   | Coast/Beach   | The tide rises suddenly, submerging lower areas and cutting off escape routes.                                          |

| Erupting geyser         | Knockback              | Volcano       | A sudden burst of scalding water blasts upward, throwing creatures into the air.                                        |
| Falling lava rock       | Immoveable obstruction | Volcano       | Chunks of molten rock fall and harden, creating new obstacles on the battlefield.                                       |
| Lava flow surge         | Inaccessible terrain   | Volcano       | A flow of lava changes course and floods part of the field, cutting off access.                                         |
| Tremor cracks ground    | Moveable obstruction   | Tundra        | A quake splits the frozen ground, pushing ice blocks around.                                                            |
| Fluttering ash cloud    | Sight                  | Volcano       | Winds shift, carrying a dense cloud of ash across the battlefield.                                                      |
| Blinding reflection     | Sight                  | Snowfield     | Sunlight reflects off ice or snow, dazzling combatants and making it hard to aim.                                       |
| Slippery slope          | Difficult terrain      | Mountain      | A rockslide creates a slick slope that is hard to climb or cross.                                                       |
| Dislodged icicles       | Knockback              | Tundra        | Icicles fall from above with force, knocking combatants down if struck.                                                 |
| Crumbling ruins         | Moveable obstruction   | Ruins         | As combat rages, pieces of ancient stone architecture fall unpredictably.                                               |

| Mana surge              | Difficult terrain      | Magical Nexus | Wild energy floods the area, distorting reality and making movement unpredictable.                                      |
