When travelling by ocean, many scenarios can occur. This roll table can trigger some unexpected ones. Possibly roll 1 each day, but can adjust for a specific campaign. These have been taken from all over, so I can't claim them all as original.

| Dice Roll | Encounter | Details |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Boat being destroyed by Kraken in the distance | In the distance, a galleon can be seen with a giant tentacle coiling around it, slowly pulling it under the waves. Some people appear to be jumping from it into the water and grabbing whatever is left from the boat that can float. The gargantuan creature is a Kraken, and the players can decide to try to help the survivors or flee. |
| 2 | Castaway | Someone is spotted in the ocean, clinging to a piece of driftwood. |
| 3 | Coral Reef | The boat’s keel (its lower part) impacts with a coral reef. Unless someone goes see underwater to fully indicate a safe trajectory the boat is prone to keep crashing against more corals. It doesn’t do much damage to it but can become dangerous if the ship starts impacting several times. |
| 4 | Seagull Post | A seagull lands on the bow with a message tied to it's leg. |
| 5 | Leviathan Encounter | A slow moving but non hostile leviathan that is just swimming along just below the surface. Can add a check to see if the ship is destabilised by the wake.|
| 6 | Whirlpool | A whirlpool is spotted. Possible for the boat to be dragged in if not spotted in time.|
| 7 | Abandoned Ship | A merchant ship is spotted floating without crew, and no signs of a struggle |
| 8 | Desert Island | An island is spotted, with no visible people living on it. |
| 9 | Flying fish | An awe-inspiring school of glittering flying fish burst from the ocean around you, chittering with delight like a beautiful birdsong.|
| 10 | Slave Ship | A slave ship transporting people to be sold. |
| 11 | Living Island | An island on the back of an ancient creature (giant turtle?|
| 12 | Murder Mystery | One of the crew turns up dead, and nobody is admitting to it. |
| 13 | Monster Hunters | A boat of monster hunters is encountered, who are looking for a Kraken/Leviathan/Merfolk etc |
| 14 | Pirates! | The crew spot a pirate ship on the horizon that appears to be turning towards you! |
| 15 | Smugglers | A small smuggling raft with lots of cargo is encountered.|
| 16 | Ship fight | A pair of ships is spotted in the middle of a violent attack. They are distracted so will not necessarily notice the party unless they decide to make themselves known.|
| 17 | Flotilla Town | A floating town of fishermen is spotted with ports, bars, etc. |
| 18 | Freebies! | A floating chest/barrel hits against the ship, possibly from a shipwreck|
| 19 | Sirens | A swarm of Sirens are encoutered, and have entranced half the crew. |
| 20 | Bounty hunters | A group of thugs board the ship and begin fighting. They have been paid to kill/kidnap someone. |
| 21 | Stowaway | You find a stowaway on the ship. |
| 22 | Mysterious Fog | Strange creatures can be heard, shapes can be seen moving in fog, you hear wood breaking and screams followed by silence. |
| 23 | Storm | A huge storm rocks the ship, and all hands on deck to stabilise! |
| 24 | Gunpowder accident | A crewmember accidentally ignited some of the gunpowder supply and blew a hole in the side of the ship! |
| 25 | Message in a bottle | A bottle with a message in is spotted floating in the water. |
| 26 | Ship in a bottle | A glass bottle with what appears to be a model ship, but it's no model! Ship has been cursed and trapped in a bottle. |
| 27 | Ritual at Sea | Some small boats are gathered in a circle. Dressed in black robes, their crew chant in the deep speech.|
| 28 | Storm Sorcerer | A storm sorcerer in a manta cloak climbs aboard. The mage offers swift winds in exchange for safe passage, good company, and a black pearl |
| 29 | Is it a boat? | An abandoned boat that when explored, is infested with mimics! Possibly boat was transporting them and they overpowered the crew. Or the boat is a mimic! |
| 30 | Broken steering | As the crew tries to correct there course, they find the rudder is not responding.|
| 31 | Dead whale | A huge and bloated dead whale floats past the ship. |
| 32 | Were-sharks | Several were-sharks attack and board the boat. |
