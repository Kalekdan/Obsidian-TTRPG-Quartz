When travelling through forest, many scenarios can occur. This roll table can trigger some unexpected ones. Possibly roll 1 each day, but can adjust for a specific campaign. These have been taken from all over, so I can't claim them all as original.

| Dice Roll | Encounter | Details |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hunting Contest | The party see a hunter apparently looking for a rare creature. There is a reward for the hunter who is able to kill it. Reward can be collected at the nearest town. |
| 2 | Chatty Bard | A chatty bard runs across the party. Time for an exposition dump! |
| 3 | Fleeing Noble | A noble is fleeing and asks the party to escort him safely, and not tell anyone about seeing him |
| 4 | Naked Bard | A naked bard asks for directions to the nearest inn where he left his clothes and money. |
| 5 | Arrow Mail | An arrow strikes a tree trunk just above you, a message wrapped around the shaft. |
| 6 | Mother Owlbear | An owlbear makes its nest in the brambles. It’s only trying to protect its eggs. |
| 7 | Timber! | A tree falls in front of you blocking the trail ahead, just of the track is ... |
