Is a halfling barbarian, played by [[Joe]]

Baba worked as a stable hand for most of his life, but had a tendency to drink. As a result of this, he practically bankrupt himself and lost his job. He would often find himself in bar fights and soon alienated people close to him, including his family.

He now seeks to redeem himself, by proving he is a capable person, and can function independently, but many of his vices have not escaped him.
