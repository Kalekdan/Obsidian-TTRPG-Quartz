## **Mariah, the Diva of Frost (Final Boss)**

_Medium Undead (or Fey), Chaotic Evil_  
**Armor Class**: 15 (natural armor)  
**Hit Points**: 85 (10d8 + 30)  
**Speed**: 30 ft, hover 10 ft

**STR** 12 (+1) | **DEX** 14 (+2) | **CON** 16 (+3) | **INT** 12 (+1) | **WIS** 14 (+2) | **CHA** 20 (+5)

**Saving Throws**: Con +5, Cha +7  
**Skills**: Performance +9, Persuasion +7  
**Damage Resistances**: Cold, Psychic  
**Condition Immunities**: Charmed, Frightened, Paralyzed  
**Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft, Passive Perception 12  
**Languages**: Common, Celestial

---

### **Abilities**

**1. Legendary Performer (Recharge 5-6)**  
Mariah sings a hypnotic melody. All creatures within 30 feet must make a **DC 14 Wisdom saving throw** or become **charmed** for 1 minute. While charmed, they spend their turns humming along and take **5 (1d10)** psychic damage at the start of each of their turns. They may repeat the save at the end of their turn.

**2. Frost Aura**  
At the start of her turn, creatures within 10 feet of Mariah take **4 (1d8)** cold damage unless they succeed on a **DC 13 Constitution saving throw**.

**3. Spellcasting** _(Spell Save DC 14)_  
Mariah is a 5th-level spellcaster. She uses Charisma as her spellcasting ability and knows the following spells:

- **At Will**: _Frostbite, Minor Illusion_
- **2/Day Each**: _Hold Person, Calm Emotions, Ice Knife_
- **1/Day**: _Cone of Cold_ (this should be her **signature move** to threaten the party if they group up).

---

### **Actions**

**1. Frosted Note (Ranged Spell Attack)**  
_+6 to hit, range 60 ft, one target._  
Hit: **12 (2d8 + 3)** cold damage. The target must succeed on a **DC 14 Dexterity saving throw** or become **slowed** (Speed halved) until the end of their next turn.

**2. Diva's Shove** _(Bonus Action)_  
Mariah magically shoves a creature within 30 feet. The target must succeed on a **DC 13 Strength saving throw** or be pushed 10 feet and knocked prone.

---

### **Legendary Actions** _(3 per round)_

- **Screeching High Note** _(1 Action)_: One creature within 30 feet takes **6 (1d12)** thunder damage (DC 13 Con save for half).
- **Glide Away** _(1 Action)_: Mariah moves up to 15 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.
- **Command the Knights** _(1 Action)_: Silent Knight or Holy Knight can make an attack as a reaction.
