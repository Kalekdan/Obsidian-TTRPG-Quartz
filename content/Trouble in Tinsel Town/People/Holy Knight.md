## **Holy Knight (Support Caster and Melee)**

_Medium Humanoid, Lawful Evil_  
**Armor Class**: 16 (Chainmail)  
**Hit Points**: 45 (6d8 + 12)  
**Speed**: 30 ft

**STR** 16 (+3) | **DEX** 10 (+0) | **CON** 14 (+2) | **INT** 11 (+0) | **WIS** 12 (+1) | **CHA** 15 (+2)

**Saving Throws**: Wis +3, Cha +4  
**Skills**: Religion +4, Intimidation +4  
**Languages**: Common, Celestial

---

### **Abilities**

**1. Aura of Devotion** _(10 ft radius)_  
All allies in the aura cannot be **charmed**.

**2. Spellcasting** _(Spell Save DC 12)_  
The Holy Knight uses Charisma to cast spells:

- **At Will**: _Guidance, Sacred Flame_
- **2/Day**: _Bless, Healing Word_

---

### **Actions**

**1. Greatsword (Melee Weapon Attack)**  
_+5 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target._  
Hit: **10 (2d6 + 3)** slashing damage.

**2. Holy Command (Recharge 5-6)**  
The Holy Knight commands nearby enemies. All creatures within 10 feet must make a **DC 12 Wisdom saving throw** or be forced to use their reaction to move 10 feet closer to him.
