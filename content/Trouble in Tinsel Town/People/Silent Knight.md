## **Silent Knight (Stealthy Skirmisher)**

_Medium Humanoid, Neutral Evil_  
**Armor Class**: 15 (studded leather)  
**Hit Points**: 38 (7d8 + 7)  
**Speed**: 30 ft

**STR** 12 (+1) | **DEX** 18 (+4) | **CON** 13 (+1) | **INT** 11 (+0) | **WIS** 12 (+1) | **CHA** 10 (+0)

**Skills**: Stealth +6, Acrobatics +6  
**Languages**: Common

---

### **Abilities**

**1. Sneak Attack (1/Turn)**  
Silent Knight deals an extra **7 (2d6)** damage when he hits a target with advantage.

**2. Shadow Step** _(Bonus Action)_  
Silent Knight teleports up to 20 feet to an unoccupied space in dim light or darkness.

---

### **Actions**

**1. Shortsword (Melee Weapon Attack)**  
_+6 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target._  
Hit: **8 (1d6 + 4)** piercing damage.

**2. Icy Throwing Knife (Ranged Weapon Attack)**  
_+6 to hit, range 20/60 ft, one target._  
Hit: **7 (1d4 + 4)** piercing damage + **3 (1d6)** cold damage.

**3. Silence the Weak** _(Recharge 5-6)_  
Silent Knight targets one creature within 10 feet. The target must succeed on a **DC 13 Constitution saving throw** or be **silenced** until the end of its next turn.
