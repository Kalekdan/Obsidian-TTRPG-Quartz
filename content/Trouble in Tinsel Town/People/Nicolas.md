## **Nicolas, the Wooden Guardian**

_Nicolas has been turned into a **Paralyzed Statue**—but when freed, he is a sturdy woodworker turned ally._

_Medium Humanoid, Neutral Good_  
**Armor Class**: 14 (leather apron, natural toughness)  
**Hit Points**: 30 (5d8 + 5)  
**Speed**: 25 ft

**STR** 14 (+2) | **DEX** 12 (+1) | **CON** 14 (+2) | **INT** 11 (+0) | **WIS** 12 (+1) | **CHA** 10 (+0)

**Abilities:**

- **Rebuild the Bell** _(Action)_: Nicolas uses his action to repair or empower the magical Jingle Bell when it is nearby. He can restore it fully over 3 rounds.

**Actions:**

- **Hammer Smash** _(Melee Weapon Attack)_: +4 to hit, 1d8 + 2 bludgeoning.
