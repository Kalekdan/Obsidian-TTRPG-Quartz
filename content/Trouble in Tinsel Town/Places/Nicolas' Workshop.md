Now run by his elves, Nicolas' workshop is a magical blacksmith and carpentry workshop. They make protective items for the party. Nicolas has been missing for several years after facing and locking up an evil bard. They assume he was successful as the magic stopped, but they haven't seen him since. When searching for him, the only thing they recovered was a frostbitten toe ([[Missile Toe]]). They have it to an intern to study, but he turned it into the Missile Toe.
