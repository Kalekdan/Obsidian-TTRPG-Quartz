####  Introduction 
Trouble in Tinsel Town is a comedy campaign, with Mariah Carey as the BBEG. She was a powerful bard, who could control people with music and was locked in a frozen jail in a cave - she was locked up by Santa, who she managed to petrify into a statue in her cave before she was locked away. Her magic is stronger when people recite her spells (songs) so gains power at christmas. She is thawing out for Christmas with the help of her 2 henchmen, the Silent Knight, and the Holy Knight who are trying to get everyone to sing.

#####  Act 1 
Party are approached by the mayor, a large drunk man with a red nose and long brown hair ([[Mayor Rudie]]), Town bell (the jingle bell) has gone missing. Everyone seems overly concerned. Some carol singers have gone missing too. The party may discover it is in fact a magic bell, that keeps the town under protection, and drowns out any magical influences over them. When people start singing christmas songs, they fall under Mariah's Influence. It was stolen by the Silent Knight.

In the town is a workshop belonging to a powerful wizard Nicholas (Santa) who designed the bell. The elves who work there will tell of Santa who hasn't been seen for years, but left his workshop to them. The party can find the schematics of the jingle bell as well as other magic items here.

#####  Act 2 
The missing people are located in a church nearby at a service run by a mysterious figure (the Holy Knight) - he is rounding up lots of people in one go to sing songs to cast a spell to free mariah. Combat encounter in the church with Silent Knight casting spells to slow down the party, and Holy Knight (more like a palladin statblock) trying to get the congregation to finish a song.

The lyrics in the song will tie into things in the combat.
* **I don't care about the presents, underneath the Christmas tree**
    * If they hide in the presents they will be invisible to her and her henchmen

    * The statue of santa clause is actually a petrified santa, and he will help them in combat if they can free him
