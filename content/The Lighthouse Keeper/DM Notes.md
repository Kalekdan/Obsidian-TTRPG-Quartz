## Synopsis 
Ships are frequently crashing into the rocks beneath the lighthouse despite the lighthouse shining. Crew are sent to investigate a wreckage. They encounter some people trying to steal from the shipwreck (combat possible)
They explore a cave up to the lighthouse - includes a large lake with a sea monster in
In the lighthouse, they find a dwarven keeper, who is alive. He is under the influence of a harpy, who is causing the ships to crash

## Intro 
The party meet in a tavern (The Tasty Battleaxe). Theres no ale (replace with a drink ordered by the party) due to shipments crashing. (party can go and recover the shipment from the wreck). Barman points out [[Preston Carter]] who is drinking and looks sad. He has lost his brother in a shipwreck.

The party can go and look for him and the shipment at the wrecks. Or go straight to the lighthouse.
> It's a bloody dangerous route by land, so the keeper usually goes by boat, and those boats are crashing an' all. Ships are crashing there daily. In fact one crashed there just this morn.

## The Wreck 
The party go to the wreck and spot some bandits looting. Combat ensues. Investigating the wreck they notice the ship was heading direct to the lighthouse, no sign of trying to correct the course. A high perception may notice one of the bodies still breathing, [[Donovan Carter]] (Preston's brother). They can try and help him, and he will give them some info about why they crashed, but will remain vague for fear of putting 'his love' (the harpy) at risk. High enough persuasion or intimidation can get more detail. They can continue to the lighthouse via a cave system from the beach through the cliffs.

## The Bridge 
The party can go direct to the lighthouse by going over the bridge. When they get there, the bridge has collapsed. They can attempt to find a way to cross the bridge (if they succeed add some bandit thralls defending the matriarch). In most cases they fail and fall down into the lake in the cave below.

Alternatively, they may see a note on the bridge posts;
> Bridge under repair, please use alternative route via beach.
They can follow the path to the right down to the beach.

## The Cave 
If **arriving from the wreck** side, they encounter the bandits camp, and can loot it finding, some health potions, a +1 weapon, and some of the missing ales from the inn. They can also find a [[Bandits note| note]] with instructions to disarm a trap. A high enough perception will notice a [[Repulsive Necklace| necklace]] (advantage against charmed - can adjust to +1 if there are lots of party members with 'Fey Ancestry') under the pile of notes on the desk. There is also a [[Necklace note| note]] loosely explaining the effect it has.

As they head through, there is a pressure plate which triggers arrows to fire from the wall. Without the note, DC 16 Dex check (bad failure triggers the trap). With the note, a DC 5 Dex to disable the trap.

Alternatively, they can go through the nothern path, and attempt to move the blockade out of the way (acrobatics to climb over DC 15, or athletics DC 15 to move it).

They then get to the lake, which they must pass.

If **arriving from the broken bridge**, they fall straight into the lake.

In the lake is a giant octopus, which they must fight. Beyond the lake they can leave the cave.

## The Lighthouse 
On arriving at the lighthouse, they see the light shining. They head inside and meet a dwarf ([[Numnet Deepbeard]]). He is the lighthouse keeper, but seems not to be fussed by the party, or the fact that ships are crashing. He is very friendly initially, seemingly oblivious to anything wrong. If they ask too many questions, or get too accusational, he will get very defensive and try to attack. If killed, the party can loot his body to find a [[Love Letter]] to the Harpy Matriarch, promising to do whatever he can for her.
