####  House Rules 
These aren't neccesarily to be used in the campaign, but I thought they were quite good so putting them here to track.
* **Session Recap**
    * Before every session, all players roll a d20. Whoever rolls the lowest has to recap last session, and if they do it well enough they get an inspiration
* **Unlimited Throwing**
    * Thrown weapons don't consume any action to 'draw'. So can throw multiple per action
* **Spell Customization**
    * When a character wants to use a spell in new and interesting way, but it doesn't work with the way the way the spell is worded at all, I let them roll a relatively high Arcana check (depending on how big the change) while casting it to rework the spell on the fly. Succeed, and get the desired effect. And if they fail the roll by 10 or more, the spell is changed... just not in the way they want.
* **Bonus Potions**
    * Only costs a bonus action to use a potion on oneself, but still a full action to use it on someone else
* **Bonus Potions Alt**
    * Magic Item, '[[Quick Flask]]' can hold one potions worth and must be filled before combat (or as an action). The consumption can then be a bonus action.
* **Finish Him!**
    * Cleave: Deal left over damage to another target within 5ft of original enemy when melee attacking (make new attack roll but deal remaining damage)
    * Pierce: Deal left over damage to another target in a straight line behind original enemy when ranged attacking (make new attack roll but deal remaining damage -1 for every 5ft beyond the first)
