###  Meeting Ireena 
The party leave the [[Blood of the Vine Tavern]] with [[Ismark the Lesser | Ismark]] to head to the [[Burgomaster Mansion]] where his sister, [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]], is staying with their father. They walk past [[Bildraths Mercentile]], and the house with the screaming...

When they arrive at the house, they notice lots of heavily trodden tracks leading up to the property, and all the windows are boarded up and covered in religious iconography. [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]] opens the door on a latch, and lets them in once she sees their brother. She appears distraught, and tells them that their father passed a couple of days ago, from the stress of the relentless attacks on their house during the night. Apparently werewolves and other of [[Strahd]]'s thralls have been trying to get to her every night, but they held out, in part thanks to the symbols which are symbols of the Morning Lord.

The party decide to interrogate [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]] to see what she can tell them. They ask about [[Madame Ava]], who she is not familiar with, but says it sounds like a [[The Vistani]] name. [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]] also confirms the house with the screaming as being Mary's house.

They ask for the party's help in burying their fathers body, and helping her escape Borovia. The party agree and spend the night at the house planning to leave the next day.

###  The Funeral 
The party, joined by [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]] and [[Ismark the Lesser | Ismark]] make there way to the church. [[Suutha]] has the father's body slung over his shoulder.

Inside the church, they meet [[Donovich]] the preist, who will perform the ceremony. They can also hear screaming from below the church, and [[Donovich]] tells them that is his son, who once tried to kill [[Strahd]], but returned here cursed as his vampire thrall. He is screaming that he is hungry, and [[Donovich]] has apparently locked him underground a year ago whilst trying to find a cure, but has so far failed.

The group all step outside for the ceremony. After they body is lowered into the grave, [[Malachi]] performs a brief ritual to ensure the body will not reanimate. Whilst discussing next plants, [[Donovich]] reiterates that they ought to help [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]] escape [[Borovia]], and should get her to [[Vallaki]], or even better, to the [[Abbey of St Markovia]] in [[Krezk]]. The party decide they will see if they can help [[Donovich]] and his son [[Doru]], before they leave.

[[Suutha]] is able to break open the door leading to the cellar where [[Doru]] is being kept. They descend into a dark basement, and can hear him muttering and begging them to leave, commenting that he can 'smell their blood'. [[Chunk]] attempts to cover up the smell with a 'Prestidigitation' spell, but the vampire thrall see's through it. Whilst the party manouver around, [[Doru]] gets more and more agitated, and they also notice a chest in the corner near where he was seated. Eventually it becomes too much, and [[Doru]] attacks [[Suutha]], and the whole party turn on him to defend themselves.

A brutal extended fight ensues, with [[Jeff]] and [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] trying to escape, before having to return as [[Malachi]] gets restrained when he tries to follow. Eventually, the party manage to defeat [[Doru]], with [[Chunk]] delivering a brutal final blow, as [[Donovich]] comes down and witnesses his sons death. He is understandably distraught, having intended to attempt to cure [[Doru]].

Before leaving the basement and the grieving father, [[Jeff]] has a look through the chest at the rear, and finds a +2 silvered dagger.

###  Meeting Strahd 
The party decide to head back to the [[Burgomaster Mansion]], not before stopping off at [[Bildraths Mercentile]] for supplies. There is a heavy premium on holy water when they try to buy some, so they instead settle for a few smaller utilities.

They decide they will spend the night at the mansion, before deciding what to do the next day. Just before sleeping, they hear a loud solitary ring of the church bell where they had been earlier. [[Jeff]] decides to keeps watch whilst the others rest. In the middle of the night, there is a knock at the door, and [[Jeff]] answers it to see [[Strahd]]. He wakes [[Chunk]] to help. [[Strahd]] asks them if they have given the letter to [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]] yet, and he reiterates that she now has 1 day left, and that she would know what that means.

In the morning, they fill in the rest of the party who agree not to tell [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]] that they had been keeping it from her, but just to tell her that the letter arrived late and she has 1 day left. She is very disturbed, and wants to leave. They agree to leave with them first thing tomorrow, with the silver weapons in the mansion. For today, they wish to explore the rest of [[Borovia]].

###  Donovich's End 
Their first stop is to revisit the church, and see what the cause of the bell in the evening prior was. As they enter the church, they immediately see [[Donovich]] hanging, having killed himself. [[Suutha]] and [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] cut him down, and [[Chunk]] finds a couple of coins on his body.
