###  Mallachi is Caught Up 
Mallachi suddenly comes to, and finds himself following the tracks towards Wizards of Wines. He sees Davian, and goes with him to catch up with the party at Yesterhill after they successfully prevented the ritual and retrieved the gem. As they arrive, a few of the druids and barbarians who were running from the rest of the party are picked off.

###  Stabilising Wizzle 
As Mallachi and Davian arrive at Yesterhill, they find Erlanor and Ismark standing with 2 bodies on the floor, the unconcious form of Ellion, and the decapitated form of Wizzle. Davian asks how things went and congratulates Erlanor on surviving. Meanwhile, Malachi manages to bring Ellion around, and casts a spell on Wizzle that stops his remains from decaying for a few days, this has the side effect of Wizzle's eyes opening and looking at whoever is closest to his body. The party ask Davian if there is any way to bring Wizzle back, and he informs them of the abbot of Kresk who has been said to bring beings back from beyond death before.

###  Helping the Winery 
The party take Wizzle's body back to the Wizard of Wines, to rest and make a firm plan. Erlanor returns the gem to Davian as the party pass the rotting vines, and Davian thanks him profusely. Inside, the party ask Davian if he'll travel with them to Kresk to help battle Strahd and in the shorter term try to revive Wizzle. Davian agrees leaving his nephew Elvir in charge. Elvir asks the party if they would help deliver some shipments of wine to Barovia, Vallaki and Kresk. The party agree despite no promise of payment, and make a plan to take the first shipment to Vallaki after they've visited Kresk (Vallaki pays more so is entitled to the first shipment). 

###  Learning of the Order of the Silver Dragon 
As the party agrees to help load the cart with the wine for the shipment, Ellion sits down with Davian and asks again if he knows anything of the order of the Silver dragon, he prompts him with the location he'd heard previously *The Argynvost manor*. At the mention of the manor, Davian opens up.
He explains that he's heard of the Argynvost manor/vault before. Long ago there used to be a dragon called Argynvost who lived there in an amber temple. Before he was a Vampire Strahd fought Argynvost and killed him. On hearing this, Vladamir Hornguard, the dragonknight, returned to the manor to form a bastion to kill Strahd.

###  Australian Wine merchants 
After resting and recovering from the battle of Yesterhill overnight, the party heads towards Kresk. The turn away from Vallaki at the crossroads, and as they get closer they feel the air getting colder. They arrive at the gates to Kresk to find a stone walled city, with a road up to the Abbey of Saint Markovia in the background. The guards at the gate question the party and why they're at Kresk. They see the Wine in the back and ask if it's for them. A surprisingly Australian Ismark argues that it's none of their business. After a lot of back and forth Erlanor eventually concedes that this is their shipment, and they open the gate without further delay. Inside the gates they meet Baron Dimytri Krezkov and Baroness Krezkova. They insist the wine is delivered to their house, and subsequently direct the party to the Abbot on the top of the large hill in town. Screams are often heard from up there, but most of the town seem surprisingly unperturbed. The Abbot apparently doesn't seem to age either, and just stays in his abbey most of the time, except to occasionally go to the shrine on the N of town. 
###  The Abbey of Saint Markovia 
After unloading the wine in the burgomasters house, the party continue up the winding path to the abbey. As they climb the weather seems to get even colder. Once they reach the top, there are a couple of small stone guarding the large iron gate entrance. A 15ft wall surrounds the main building. They push through the gate, and hear a pig like snorting, and a voice asking who they might be. Otto Belview comes down to see the party, and allow them through, whilst Zygfrek remains at his post calling down.

Otto has the appearance of a beardless dwarf, but with many of his humanoid limbs replaced with a random assortment of animal parts. He begins to guide the party in. At the main entrance, there are two guards atop the gate, and there is a tarnished copper plaque on the wall with the name of the abbey, and 'may her light cure all illness'. Ismark knows it to be an abbey to St Marcovia.

Once into the courtyard, there is a thick fog, with a 15ft high curtain wall surrounding them, with guards with their backs to the party. Upon closer inspections, all the guards are in fact scarecrows. In the middle of the courtyard is a well, with a small humanoid tied to it, with bat wings and spider mandibles. They are flapping around madly as though trying to escape. The sheds along the edges of the courtyard have screams coming from within. Inside the sheds, they can see remains of chicken coops and individuals with many deformities hiding in the darkness at the rear.

At the bottom of the well they hear a scurrying. Ellion lights up the well and sees a 5ft tall wiry spindly creature look up and hiss before scurrying up the well to slash at Ellion. Is has 3 red spider eyes on one side, and a humanoid face on the other. It also has a frog hand instead of a hand. When Ellion screams, the creature reproduces his scream back to him exactly. Ismark manages to throw him back down the well where they hear the impact then silence.

###  Meeting The Abbot 

They venture inside the building, with Ellion going first. There is gentle music playing on the large ground floor. They find themselves in a 60ftx60ft room with a large cauldron over a fire hearth. There is a large suspended golden disc engraved with a sigil of the sun. There are some stairs ascending and descending to one side.

Stood behind the large table in the centre, is a young handsome man in a monks robe. There is also a young woman stood at the window. The man looks up as they arrive. The woman turns around, and they see that her left hand is missing, and she has a few bones sticking out. She also has her face missing entirely. The young man introduces himself as the Abbot, and the woman as a 'bride' who he has been making for Strahd. He believes Strahd must find true love in order to heal the land. He's taken appendages from people who didn't need them and stitched a flesh golem. She still needs more parts, namely a hand and an attractive face. He looks through the party and decides that Mallachi's hand would be perfect. Erlanor creates an illusionary hand on the floor which would be a better fit.

The Abbot begins to walk towards it, and Ismark steps between the hand and the Abbot to stop him realising the illusion. The Abbot stwikes Ismark vewy vewy hard. When he tries to pick up the hand the illusion fades. He also strikes Erlanor nearly knocking him unconscious in one hit.

They are forced to agree to leave Wizzle with him, and get the hand and face for him. In their heads, they hear the Abbot say 'you have no choice'. They leave back to the courtyard, and notice all the creatures appear to be part of the same family. Davian tells them that this place used to be a shrine to the morning lord, but it fell to Strahd years ago. They venture through one of the other doors in the abbey. From downstairs they can hear unnatural whispering sounds, so they decide to go upstairs instead. At the top of the stairs they see a huge shape lumbering down the hall towards them. It's a 7ft tall flesh golem like the bride. They leave immediately.

They head back down to town to join the burgomaster for a banquet. When they arrive the food is already laid, and looks exquisite. The burgomasters son has already started eating.
