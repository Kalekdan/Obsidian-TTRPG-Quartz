Borovia is a town the party encounter early in the campaign and is relatively deserted. It is located through the misty [[Svalich Woods]]. There are a few residents who are still alive, but most appear to be dead or undead.

It is home to the Durst Manor, a house in which a family cult had been experimenting with immortality. The party explore here in the [[The Curse of Strahd - Session 1 | first]] and [[The Curse of Strahd - Session 2 | second]] sessions of the campaign.

Also in Borovia is the [[Blood of the Vine Tavern]], and [[Bildraths Mercentile]].

In the north east of the town is the [[Burgomaster Mansion]]

Overlooking the town on a large cliff, is [[Strahd | Strahd's]] castle.
