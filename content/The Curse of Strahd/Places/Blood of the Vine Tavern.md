The Blood of the Vine Tavern is a tavern in [[Borovia]]. The party visit in the [[The Curse of Strahd - Session 2 | second session]] where they encounter [[Ismark the Lesser]] who he asks for assistance in helping his sister [[Ireena Kolyana]] to escape [[Borovia]] and [[Strahd | Strahd's]] clutches.

The party also see a group of [[The Vistani]] enjoying a drink in here.
