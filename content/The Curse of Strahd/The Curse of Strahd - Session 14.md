###  Answers from the Burgomaster 
Back in the Burgomaster's house, the mob outside are still being a nuisance. [[Ellion]] speaks honestly to Luca, and tells him that he doesn't know where the cows are. Luca runs off back to his house. The party speak with the Burgomaster to get some answers. He tells them that Ilya, their son, had dies of the flu, and a few days later they took him to the Abbot where he was resurrected, but apparently as a demon.

He also told the party a bit about the history of Krezk. He tells how there used to be another abbot, who tried to rival [[Strahd]]. He gathered a large group of troops but got massacred. He thought the morning lord would assist him, but he ended up dying. Not much later, the new abbot came along and has lived in the abbey since.

They asked some questions to Davian about the order of the silver dragon. He tells them that during [[Strahd]]'s final conquest to take Barovia, they tried to fight back, but were ultimately unsuccessful.

###  Sharing a Drink with Friends 
In an effort to calm the mob, the party bring up the wine they had delivered to Krezk and share it around. A short while later, a raven flies through the door, looking barely alive. It morphs into Adrian (one of the members of the wizards of wine, and Davian's son (nephew?). He tells them the wine was poisoned. The party remembers collecting samples from some vials left by the Yesterhill folk which they now confirm to be the poison. Apparently it causes everyone to die at midnight.

They are told that the Abbot might be able to help, but the party are hesitant to go to him again. There is also mention of a vampire hunter who lives in a tower in the middle of a nearby lake (Lake Baratok). They decide to head there immediately first. They head to the tower, through the svalich woods. The come to a sign, N Krezk, E Vallaki, SW Wizards of Wine.

###  Busty the Vampire Slayer 
At the lake, they notice a few footprints leading up to the tower, one set of large human prints, and one set with one of the legs putting a lot of weight through the heel. Outside the decrepit tower is a locked cart with a keep out sign hanging. There is a puzzle lock on the door, with the word Exethanther over it. They complete the puzzle and head into the tower. On the ground floor there is a lift with chains going up. There is also a chest which they find some silvered bolts and a large crossbow, as well as some garlic and stakes. There are 4 clay statues in the room as well.

On the second floor, there are books scattered everywhere, and a mahogany bust mounted on one wall. When they approach the bust, it springs to life. It is called Buster, and tells them that it is in love with its owner, Esmerelda. In order to win her over he wants to gift her, a poem, some wine and some flowers. They are unable to help but carry him upstairs to allow him to explore the house with them.

On the third floor, there is not a lot, other than a seemingly magical ceiling with stars and the night sky on it.

The top floor is the bedroom, with a large locked chest, which smells of lavendar, they try but are unable to unlock it. They head back outside to the cart and try to break in after seeing what appears to be antidotes to something on the side. After successfully unlocking the door, they push it open, but it triggers a trap and the whole thing blows up, knocking [[Robyn]] and Davian unconscious.

###  And now we wait 
The entire party are quite injured so do not wish to travel back through the Svalich Woods. They decide they will wait for Esmerelda to return, and ask for her help. After about 30mins, they hear her come through the entrance. They tell her they are in the house and need her help. The give her the sample they have of the poison. She confirms it to be 'Midnight Tears'. The antidote was apparently in her cart. The only other person who may be able to help is the Abbot in Krezk. She says if they provide him with a staff he had been looking for then he might help. The staff is currently under the position of a group of werewolves nearby.

She gives the party a few supplies for the journey, including some silvered arrows, and health potions, most of which they consume on the spot.
