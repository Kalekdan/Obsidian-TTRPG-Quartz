###  A New Ally 
After losing Ireena, the party decide to head to to the amorment to pick up Grogs rolling pins. They see the aftermath of the festival and the guards clearing up. Erlanor asks about the festival and is told it is put on by the burgomaster to increase morale.

After picking up the now silvered rolling pins, they head back to the tavern. They see a man at the bar with a big top hat. They grab some drinks and the man heads upstairs. Chunk follows. He puts his ear to his door and hears him writing in a journal and talking to himself.
> '...and that's the tale of the giant man eating plant with the most remarkable singing voice...'

Chunk returns at night and attemps to steal the journal. He unlocks the door, but the man (Rictavio, a half elf bard) sees him and finds it quite suspicious.

In the morning, Rictavio is writing in a different journal at the bar. Chunk apologises. Rictavio leads Chunk and Erlanor upstairs. Thinks the people at the tavern are were-ravens. If we can get him some food for his horse he might help. He suspects the vistani camp to the south are in league with strahd, if we can prove it he will talk more. We tell him what we know about it. He wants us to help eradicate the vistani camp. He has a powerful friend called Tiger who could help vs Strahd.

Chunk and erlanor discuss the options. They want to help the ravens at the tavern first, then will help him. They will meet him tomorrow.

Chunk goes to see if Ismarch will join them too. They replay the prophecies to him. He tells them about:
* Mad mage of Mt Baratok (N of lake zaravich):
    * Some people have seen lightning bolts on the water N side of lake Zarovich.
* There are rumourch of the Vallakovich's son married someones daughter who went crazy.
* 'True Evil' probably refers to Strahd.

Yevgeni and Szoldar will accompany them. It's 10/12 iles to Krezk then onwards. Its about a 2 day mission. They pay them to join, and buy some rations.

###  Towards Krezk 
They head towards the western gate (the sunset gate). The guards nod at them as they leave; 'All will be well'.

There are a few abandoned houses on the outskirts of Vallaki, where the metalworkers used to live. As they keep walking it gets mistier. They come accross a bridge and just over it a crossroads with a broken sign. They can see a lake to the north. Yevgeni and Szoldar know which way to go. Could go to the towere if we want, but the party decide not to.

The churning of the mists seems to increase and the see 3 beserkers. They fight them and level up.
