###  The Funeral of a Friend 
When heading back to the church to bury Suutha, the party bump into a large purple man with a claw for his arm. He is putting up signs around town for the 'Wolfs Head Jamboree'. He asks if the party had seen any wolves, to which they respond that they are not in the mood to chat, but know that there are some creatures in the house they just came from.

They carry on to the church and speak with the priest. He tells them that St Andral was the first overseer of Borovia, and he outdated Strahd. They return his bones to the location under the floorboards at the church much to the priests relief.

As they are burying Suutha and saying their goodbyes, they see a high elf seemingly following directions coming towards the church. He speaks with Lucian (the preist). He is looking for a group, he has an amulet of 'the order of the silver dragon'. Lucian tells him of a haunted abandoned mansion, called Argynvostholt. The legend is that a great silver dragon died there. He has also heard of a 'amber temple' which he believes could be related.

After burying Suutha, Erlanor gets the sense that someone has been watching them. He keeps glimpsing someone in the shadows. He spots a short chubby guy wearing rags and trying to blend in. Once noticed he tries to walk away.

He is called Ernst, and is from Vallaki. Lady Fiona (his boss) sent him to spy on them. She lives in north east of Vallaki.

###  The Wake 
They head to the tavern to have a drink and whilst in there they see a man with pointed ears with a top hat looking merry.

They also notice Ismarch sat in the corner looking very stressed chatting to a man with blue eyes and dark hair. The same man that the party saw running towards Vallaki looking for Ireena a few days prior. (who they thought they saw dissapear into a puff of smoke)

The group go to the bar for a drink, and Erlanor and Chunk head to Ismarch to warn him about the man. The man charms Chunk. The man (called Vassili) tells them that Issech took Ireena, and he is trying to help get her back. They try and draw Ismarch away.

Ellion is looking for artifacts and pulls out a silver 'donut'; 'order of silver dragon'.

According to Vassili, Issech probably took Ireena to the burgomasters mansion. They spend the night at the tavern, with Erlanor staying in the room with Ismarch, and tries to warn him about Vassili but is ignored. During the night they see purple flashes out the window.

###  The Burgomasters Mansion 
In the morning, they head south towards the burgomasters mansion. At the crossroads, there is a street reader reading a sign about the festival of the blazing sun. As they continue to the mansion, which appears to be a somewhat neglected castle, they see guards dragging people inside.
> 'I didn't mean what I said' etc.
They also see people being dragged out looking shaken

There are people bringing 'offerings' in the forms of bundles of sticks. Mallachi asks why they are laying sticks;
> It's for the festival of the blazing sun
>
> They have weekly ceremonies, 'all will be well'

Vassili knocks on the door. They party tell the doorman they are friends of the burgomaster and ask if they can have a meeting.

Inside, there are framed portraits on the wall down a long carpeted hall. Bundles of twigs lay heaped against the wall. The portraits depic a powerful looking man and his family. Some of the portrayed people look very alike. The maid opens a door down the main corridor for the group to wait in for the burgomaster (Varghas Valakovich). The room smells of cigar smoke and has a bear head on the wall. After 2-3 hours of waiting, the burgomaster comes in. He denies knowing Ireena. He says Essech does what he wants. After the burgomaster leaves, they speak with Essech. Chunk casts zone of truth on him.

'Is Ireena your sister?'
> Yes
'If I promised not to take her, would you tell me where she is?'
> He leads chunk to his bedroom. There are dolls everywhere. They all look like Ireena. Ireena is cowering in the corner. Issech keeps dreaming about her. Feels like he was separated a long time ago. Blinsky made the dolls. The dolls get more and more similar to Ireena. Issech locks the door behind him when they leave.

Ismarch confirms that Ireena is adopted. They ask Essech about the purple flashes they saw. He tells them he's been 'practicing', and can summon fire from tendrilly hands. He doesn't remeber losing his arm, but sees it get torn off by wolves in dreams.

###  Festival of the Blazing Sun 
Before leaving, Ellion detects that Vassili is undead. Malachi opens a window for them to return later to rescure Ireena. They decide to head to Blinsky for now, and return for Ireena in the evening during the festival.

They go past the crossroads, and take a right, led by Vassili. There is a woodedn sign shaped like a rocking horse. Through the dirty glass they see toys. 'Is no fun, is no Blinsky'. Malachi casts detect magic but sees nothing. Chunk dresses as a kid ('Robbie') and goes in with Malachi.

The shop owner, Gadalf Blinksy, has lots of dolls. There is one that looks like Strahd, and one that looks like Ireena (that is not for sale). Erlanor goes in and mentions Gertruda, but Blinksy is not familiar with her. Chunk buys the Strahd doll.

As they head out, there is lots more bustle from the square. They see people in stocks wearing crude donkey heads. There is a parade of unhappy children dressed as flowers walking by followed by unhappy adults. There is a large wicker sun in the centre of the square that is being splashed with oil. It starts to rain. The burgomaster tries to light it, but his torch goes out. A guard laughs. The burgomaster is shocked and furious, and has the guard handcuffed and dragged towards the burgomasters house. Erlanor casts firebolt and lights the wicker ball as a distraction. The burgomaster smiles and walks back and throws the guard on the fire.

The party sneak away to the mansion, where Vasilli charms the party again, and successfully charms all except Erlanor. Vasilli and Ellion wait outside whilst the rest sneak to find Ireena.

They manage to sneak her out, and as they turn the corner with her Vassili has a hunger in his eyes. He suddenly ages 15 years, and turns into Strahd. They try and convince him to let her go to no avail. He dissapears.

Chunk stomps on the Strahd doll.
