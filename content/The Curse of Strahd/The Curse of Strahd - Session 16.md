###  Retreat 
After losing [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] to the werewolves, the party including Esmerelda and Davian, retreated back to Esmerelda's house, with the staff. They stopped off to take Buster the talking statue with them.

On the way, they encountered [[Rowan Thorne]], a mysterious old elf, who had apparently been watching them and the werewolves. Together, they all return to Krezk with the staff, to see if the Abbot will make them the antidote for the poison the town is infected with.
