Donovich is the preist at the church in [[Borovia]], and he helps the party bury the body of [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]] and [[Ismark the Lesser | Ismark]]'s father. The party also attempt to help him cure his son [[Doru]] from his vampiric curse in [[The Curse of Strahd - Session 2]], but end up killing him. Later Donovich takes his own life as a result.
