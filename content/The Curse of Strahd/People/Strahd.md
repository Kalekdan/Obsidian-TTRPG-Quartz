Strahd is a vampire who cursed the land of [[Borovia]] in order to achieve immortality. The [[The Vistani]] appear to revere him. He treats them well, as they helped him before he became a vampire. [[Madame Ava]] also tells the party that he killed his brother as part of achieving immortality.

He has an obsession with [[Ireena Kolyana]] who he has bitten twice.
