Ireena Kolyana is the daughter of [[Kolyan Indirovich]] and sister of [[Ismark the Lesser]]. She lives in the [[Burgomaster Mansion]] in [[Borovia]].

[[Strahd]] is apparently obsessed with her, and has bitten her several times. [[Strahd]] delivers her a letter via the party, warning her she has 3 days, but the party do not immediately know what that means.
