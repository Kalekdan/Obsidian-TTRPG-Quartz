###  A New Acquaintance 
Having defeated the owner of Gulthias Staff, Erlanor puts it in his jacket, and feels it throbbing. It appears to be linked to the blights which are hammering at the door. The door is just about to break, so the party run through the only location they've yet to explore, and end up ion the wine cellar. There is thick moss on the walls, and a thin mist on the floor. The eastern side of the cellar has a rack filled with wine bottles, and the western rack is empty.

Behind one of the racks of wine, is a gnomish man. At this point the blights above break through the door and they begin fighting. During the fight, Jeff goes to check the other entrance, and hears a few more blights coming that way. As Erlanor is quite damaged, he breaks the staff, and it sprays black ooze everywhere. All the blights stop.

They speak with the gnomish man, [[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]], and he tells them he has been hiding out and living in the basement for some time. He came to work here, then the people with the blights turned up. As they all head upstairs, they see 2 huge humanoid figures wrapped in vines dead on the floor. [[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]] finds 2 empty vials on one of them, which had some kind of liquid in at some point. As they step outside, the vines look very withered, more so than when they arrived. They speak to Davian to let him know they've freed the vineyard. He tells them the cause of the rot is the gem which has been stolen from them. 

###  The History of Borovia 
The gem had been in the family for generations, and is needed to grow plants in Borovia. There had once been 3 of them; one was lost ages ago, thought to have been taken by the forestfolk in Yesterhill. The second was lost a couple of weeks ago. One of Davian's employees, Elvir, had tracked the thieves to Berez, but couldn't find them. The 3rd was lost 3 days or so ago.

The party suggest going to Berez to find the gem, but are informed it is too dangerous. Yesterhill would be the best opportunity to recover a gem. The Huntress had a shrine there, but the forest folk overtook it. Yesterhill is about an hour to the south.

Before Strahd took over the lands, the 'ladies three' had control over the lands; The Huntress, The Weaver, and The Seer. Each had a shrine (the windmill they had visited previously was one of the shrines). Each of these 3 sisters had 1 gem. The forestfolk who stole the gem in Yesterhill are completing a ritual tomorrow.

Erlanor enquires about where he might be able to acquire some holy water, and is suggested The Abbot in Krezk may be able to help. As they head to bed, Erlanor finds another vial on the other vine creature.

###  Spies in the Night 
As they are sleeping, Erlanor and Jeff hear some people entering the building through the broken door. Erlanor goes out of his room to alert the others. He see's the intruders and recognises them to the people from yesterday (forestfolk). When they notice him they run away. [[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]] wakes up and goes invisible to try and chase them, but loses sight of them.

Whilst they're sleeping, [[Suutha]] becomes aware that he is losing touch with his god.

In the morning, they head downstairs for breakfast and talk with Davian about the plan for the day. Davian thinks the people in the night came for something, but is unsure what. On further prying, he admits he thinks they are looking for a sacrifice to allow them to complete their ritual later today.

He tells the party:
> Long ago, Strahd took over the lands, and desecrated the 3 shrines. Half of the forestfolk rejected Strahd, but the others took him as their new god, and worship him. They took the gem.
He thinks the forestfolk might be trying to animate a form of Strahd, to make a second coming.

###  Ritual Shmitual 
They decide to head to Yesterhill to attempt to recover the gem and disrupt the ritual. The path ascends up a hill, south of the vineyard. They see dark ominous clouds, and lightning repeatedly striking the same spot somewhere at the top of the hill. Once on the hill, they see several concentric circles of paths, lined with cairns of dark rocks. After a few minutes observing, they see a patrol of a couple of people walking the inner circle. After waiting a while longer, they also see patrols on the middle ring, and outer ring. 

[[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]] goes invisible and runs up the hill to the cairns in the centre. Atop the hill is a ring of black rocks. He sees the lightning strike a bit further down the hill. It does illuminate a large effigy next to him, made of twigs. It is a large cloaked figure, with fangs, and a hollow chamber in the chest, about the size of a body. The statue looks to be representing Strahd. In the head of the statue, about 20ft up, is a glowing green gem.

[[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]] runs back to rejoin the group, and tells them what he saw. [[Suutha]] had been observing the patrol timings deduces the optimal time to avoid them all. They all dash to the top of the hill, avoiding detection. Once at the top, Erlanor casts Gaseous Form on [[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]], and allows him to float up to the top of the statue. Once at the top, he turns him back. [[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]] attempts to break into the statue's head to retrieve the gem.

As he begins doing this, it alerts the patrols, and the party soon find themselves surrounded, with [[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]] still at the top of the statue. 1 of the forestfolk surrounding them appears to be wearing slightly more regal clothes. They are chanting, as they point to [[Suutha]] and shout, 'the final component' and begin to tie him up and raise him into the cavity in the statue. The chants are in a foreign language to them all, but they pick up the use of the word Strahd in there.

Erlanor turns to the regal looking forestfolk and uses Suggestion, the 'make them stop'. In response the forestfolk turns and shouts 'Please Lord, Make Them Stop!'. The dark crackling clouds above begin to swirl lower, and a dark booming voice from that tells everyone to stop what theyre doing. [[Suutha]] and [[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]] fail to resist this, so [[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]] must refrain from breaking the gem out of the statue. This also affects most of the forestfolk, with only 2 druids still chanting.

[[Suutha]] manages to break free from the ropes, and [[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]] jumps down to help. [[Suutha]] commands one of the chanting druids to flee, and they run away. With only 1 druid still chanting, combat begins.

During combat, the clouds seem to come lower, and there are lots of bats going back and forth. Erlanor sits on the outside of the inner ring, and launches spells in, whilst [[Suutha]] and [[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]] are fighting their way out. [[Suutha]] manages to run up to the statue, and decapitate it, resulting in the gem falling free onto the ground. He shortly falls unconscious in the middle of the group of forestfolk. In an attempt to draw them away, Erlanor catapults the gem further down the hill, which successfully draws a few of the druids. 

[[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]] is able to get to [[Suutha]] to help him regain consciousness but, soon falls unconsious himself. They both fight for consciousness for a while, before [[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]] is decapitated. As he dies, the clouds lower even further, and Strahd apparates in front of everyone. He shouts to 'Stop breaking my playthings' and casts a fireball into the midst of everyone. The beserkers all run.

Strahd turns into a bat and fucks off.

Erlanor grabs the gem and runs over to stabilise [[Suutha]], who is still unconscious. Erlanor speaks with Ismark to see what they can do for [[Wizzle Nimbledigit | Wizzle]]. He suggests The Abbot in Krezk may be able to help bring him back, if they're not too late...
