###  Dinner with the Burgomaster 
[[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] and [[Ellion]] were invited to have dinner with the Burgomaster and his family. During the meal, they notice the son, Ilya is looking agitated, and very gaunt.

Early the following morning, [[Griz]] is wondering through the town, and sees Luca, the somewhat slow farmer, who's had several cows go missing. In an attempt to console him, several other members of Krezk turn up, and before long a mob forms, blaming Luca for killing his cows.

[[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] and [[Ellion]] hear the noise and investigate and are also dragged into the mob. [[Robyn]] also happens to be passing by at the same time. They agree to investigate the missing cows as long as the party can leave the next day. They leave Luca in the kitchen of the Burgomasters house in the meantime.

Whilst investigating, they see a few bodies, and Ilya hunched over one. When they approach, he turns into a large demon, and a fight ensues. They kill him, and report back to the Burgomaster, but his wife Anna is missing. He is upset, but do not seem entirely surprised that their son was a demon.

On the way back, [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] notices [[Griz]] get called into an alleyway by an anonymous figure, and is given a note.
