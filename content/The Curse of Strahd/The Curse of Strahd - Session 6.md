###  The Bluewater Inn 
The party return to [[Vallaki]] via the morning gate. The plan to rest up at [[Bluewater Inn]] and help find somewhere for the kids to stay. Outside the tavern, they notice a group of ravens nesting in a hole in the roof. They realise they've seen ravens around quite a bit and are suspicious that they may be spies for [[Strahd]].

They head into the inn, and see it is quite busy - there are a group of 5 people on the left, sitting quietly with hooded black coats. On the right is a pair of quite drunk looking men who the group decide to approach. They are introduced as [[Szoldar Szoldarovich]] and [[Yevgeni Krushkin]]. [[Yevgeni Krushkin | Yevgeni]] in particular seems very drunk. The party chat to them for a bit, to ask about the area. They find out the toymaker Blinsky is in the south of [[Vallaki]]. They also ask about somewhere for the kids to stay. [[Szoldar Szoldarovich | Szoldar]] mentions there is an orphanage, which he will guide them to if they want.

The barmaid comes over, and says her husband will take a look at the kids for them if they want.

Speaking more to [[Szoldar Szoldarovich | Szoldar]], they establish more about the area. He was born in [[Vallaki]], and is something of an explorer in the area. He reccomends visiting [[Krezk]]. When they ask about adventuring outside of [[Borovia]], he tells them that it is not possible. After leaving the gates, you will walk for about a mile before finding yourself back where you started. The [[The Vistani]] seem to be able to come and go as they please however, likely due to their allegiance with [[Strahd]]. The party also ask about the ravens they've seen, and if they can do anything about it. This makes [[Yevgeni Krushkin | Yevgeni]] uncomfortable.

The barmaid's husband comes over, and takes a look at the kids. He confirms they are in a magical trance of sorts, and he offers to put the children up for the night, or advises the party take them to the orphanage, to see [[Claudia Belasco | Mrs Belasco]].

They also gather from [[Yevgeni Krushkin | Yevgeni]] that there is somewhere in town to buy armor, [[Thindal's Armorment]] down the road to the west.

The party speak to the barmaids husband about helping him with their 'raven infestation', but he is very defensive, and insists they do not touch them.

###  The First Innocent Death 

The party head outside, and [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] sends [[Kio]] up the building to take a look at where the ravens are. They attack him and kill him. Half his body remains at the top of the building, and the other half falls down to a very distraught [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]].

They solemnly head back inside, and take a seat, and get a large drink. [[Suutha]] notices the barmaid, and the rest of the family have dark clothes and raven feathers sticking out of their hair. She angrily approaches the barmaid and grabs her accusing her of having some part in [[Kio]]'s death. As this happens, she draws a blade, and the 5 hooded figures sat at the edge of the pub start towards [[Suutha]].

Meanwhile, [[Jeff]] uses the distraction to explore one of the two upstairs landings in the tavern, but finds nothing of interest, aside from lot's of raven motifs on the walls and as decoration.

The rest of the group manage to diffuse the situation downstairs and leave with the kids and [[Szoldar Szoldarovich | Szoldar]] and [[Yevgeni Krushkin | Yevgeni]], to head to the orphanage.

###  The Orphanage 
The orphanage is next to an old church, in an old groundskeepers house, which is in much disrepair. They knock on the door, and [[Claudia Belasco]] answers the door. She invites them in and they ask a few questions about the establishment. They have had some issues with a child called [[Felix]] who she says is troubled. [[Milivoj]] is the oldest child, and is currently in a coma. [[Claudia Belasco | Mrs Belasco]] believes this to be due to [[Felix]] attacking him. Another child, [[Cedric]] also died, thought to be at the hands of [[Felix]]. [[Felix]] is young, less than 8 years old.

They enter the property, and are walked through the living room area, where there are several children playing. A group of 3 girls are looking at a doills house, whilst another boy looks after some of the younger kids. There are a lot of children, and it is quite chaotic and noisy. They speak with [[Claudia Belasco | Mrs Belasco]] who believes the problem with [[Felix]] is the locket which he turned up with, she believes it to be cursed. They decide to go and see [[Milivoj]], the boy in a coma. In his room there is dirt around, and a shovel on the floor. [[Malachi]] examines him and see's blood on his lips, and he looks quite ill and feverish. He confirms it is however a magical condition, and not contagious. [[Claudia Belasco | Mrs Belasco]] suggests that maybe the priest next door [[Lucian Petrovich]] might know what happened.

They head back through the children's play area, and notice one of the girls rubbing her back as if wounded, whilst another has bruises on her arm. [[Jeff]] speaks to one of the injured girls, and sees an 'inhuman' bite mark on her back, which happened a few days ago, but the girl doesn't remember, thinking that perhaps it happened when she was asleep. The bruised girl similarly isn't aware of receiving the bruises. She does mention that she misses [[Cedric]], and disliked [[Felix]] who was close to him.

They ask [[Claudia Belasco | Mrs Belasco]] if they can rest in her office for a short while. They sit in there and [[Suutha]] and [[Jeff]] notice a lockbox on the shelf with an x carved into it. Inside the box is a locket. There is an engraving on it, written in Celestial that reads: *"Let darkness hide from the gaze of the light."* They put the locket back.

There are also a few books around about 'demonic curses' and other rituals. They head upstairs with [[Claudia Belasco | Mrs Belasco]]'s permission to go and see [[Felix]]. They don't tell her about finding the locket.

They head upstairs and explore, noticing lots of rooms. There are 5 open doors, and 1 locked. There is also a boarded up broken window, and a trap door on the ceiling. Most of the rooms are full of empty beds, but [[Suutha]] notices some runes on the bedposts in Infernal. [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] translates it to mean *"Dark dreams"*. In one of the rooms lie the two kids that the party rescued on beds. [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] casts a protective spell on them both, and uses his dagger to attempt to scratch out the runes on the beds they are sleeping on.

In one of the other rooms they see a girl crying in the corner. She says she misses her friends who ran away, as they didn't want to share a room with [[Felix]]. Another room has the same perfectly made beds with the runes, however a single bed in the corner looks very used, and does not have the runes. The final locked room contains 3 cribs, with babies asleep in 2 of them. They also see a girl holding a baby and singing a lullaby and another girl sat on the floor. They had locked the door to hide from [[Felix]]. [[Cedric]] had stolen his locket, so pushed him out the window. The locket apparently looks like the one the party described having found downstairs. They confirm that [[Felix]] is in the attic.

They head up to the attic and it is mostly barren and dusty. There is a lone boy sat in a chair, who confirms he is [[Felix]]. He says he is not ashamed of killing [[Cedric]].

###  Questions at the Church 
Determined to find out more before tackling [[Felix]], the party head to the church of St. Andral next door to speak to [[Lucian Petrovich]]. There is a sermon ongoing when they arrive at what appears to be a church to the 'Morning Lord'. They speak with [[Lucian Petrovich | Lucian]], who tells them the Burgomaster claims that all is well, but that is not the case. The church used to hold the bones of St. Andral, that protected it from [[Strahd]], but these were stolen a few weeks ago. Only the priest knew about them. 

They spend the night there - [[Malachi]] has some dream??? TBC

In the morning, they speak more with the preist. He asks if they will help find the bones for him. He only told [[Milivoj]] who worked at the church as a grave digger.

###  Return to the Orphanage 
The party return to the orphanage and sneak into [[Claudia Belasco | Mrs Belasco]]'s office to take the locket and head upstairs to confront [[Felix]]. They head into the attic and [[Felix]] is still there. When they confront him, a large demon rises out of his body and starts fighting the group. In an attempt to destroy the demon, [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] takes out the locket and stabs it, but it doesn't appear to do anything other than enrage the demon. The locket is thrown between them as they continue to fight. At one point they manage to dispel the demon by placing the locket on [[Felix]] and it goes back into him. However he still appears to be possessed, so they remove it again, and the demon returns, looking healthier than before. As the party is getting quite battered, [[Suutha]] attempts to destroy it by attacking [[Felix]] whilst he is unconscious on the floor - this unfortunately kills [[Felix]] but does nothing to dispel the demon. They eventually manage to destroy it, but are all very shaken after seeing [[Suutha]] kill the child. [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] takes the locket from [[Felix]]'s body and pockets it.
