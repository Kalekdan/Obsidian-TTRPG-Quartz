###  The Durst House Basement 
Upon entering the basement of the house, the party hears loud voices and screams appearing to come from everywhere, with no direction in particular. Nobody is able to make out what the voices are saying. The emblazoned sword and sheild that [[Suutha]] had picked up previously, also turned to dust shortly after they made it to the basement.

The party decide to explore to find the source of the noises. They walk through a few eerie rooms in the dungeonlike basement. The first room they encounter has several beds, seemingly incredibly old, and covered in mould. Continuing through, they reach a large square room with a well in the centre, and a bucket, about 30ft deep. [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] drops a gold coin into the well but nothing happens. Around this room are 5 smaller rooms, each with a bed, and a small storage container - these appear to be rudimentary living quarters. The party splits up and takes a room each, and attempts to open their respective crates. [[Jeff]] successfully picks the lock on his, and inside finds a leather pouch, with a few silver and gold coins inside. He soon realises the pouch was made of human leather...

[[Suutha]] manages to smash the lid off her box, and inside is a moss agate. [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] asks [[Suutha]] to attempt the same for his box, and they successfully break in, and find an eye patch, with a carnelian stone embedded in it. [[Malachi]] fails to open his chest but [[Suutha]] is also able to brute force it open. Inside it is a shortsword, coated in a layer of silver.

The party are unable to find anything else of interest in this strange room. They experiment with dropping their items in the bucket and lowering it down the well, but they quickly wheel it back up when nothing happens.

###  The Crypts 
Continuing through the basement, the party encounter a room with a large table, and what appear to be childrens bones on the floor, with blade markings in them. [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] pockets one to investigate later. They also stumble upon what appears to be some crypts. [[Suutha]] walks into the first one, and encounters what appears to be a ghost of [[Rose and Thorn | Rose]]. The sarcophagus in the room has the lid pushed aside and is empty. The ghost of Rose proceeds to scream and grapple with [[Suutha]] and possesses her. From this point, [[Suutha]] finds herself incredibly averse to being given instructions, and will contest any. She heads straight into the second crypt which also has an open and empty sarcophagus. Also in the room is a ghost which appears to be [[Rose and Thorn | Thorn]], but seems uninsterested in [[Suutha]]. Before realising what [[Suutha]] had encountered [[Jeff]] heads in after her. The ghost of [[Rose and Thorn | Thorn]] possesses [[Jeff]], making him incredibly emotional and depressed, causing him to break down and cry.

Having observed these interactions, [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] casts 'Protection from Evil and Good' on himself, making him immune to possession and other undead or similar creatures. With this protection, he heads into one of the 3 remaining crypts, and sees a sarcophagus with the name Walter Durst engraved on it. He manages to remove the lid and inside is the skeleton of a baby. The party investigate the other two rooms which have sarcophagus's with the names Gustav Durst and Elizabeth Durst, and the skeletons of adults inside.

###  A Grotesque Collection 
The party continue to explore the basement, and begin to head down a long narrow corridor. [[Jeff]] leads the way, but as he takes a step, a floor tile gives out beneath him, and he falls 10ft into a pit of spikes, and badly injures himself. The party manage to get him out of the hole, and proceed much more cautiously, with [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] sending out his familiar [[Kio]], to scout the rest of the corridor. They are able to tell that at the end of the corridor it descends deeper, but they can hear that is where the background screaming/chanting appears to be coming from. [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] is able to distinguish that they are chanting "Let the Durst family rest". They proceed further down, and walk into a large square room with many alcoves with grotesque items in each, including; a preserved goblins hand, an aspergillum made from bone, a frog lashed to a stick and many other oddities. On the southern wall of the room are some steps descending into a room with shallow water covering the floor. The entrance is blocked by a portcullis. [[Suutha]] and [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] are able to lift the gate open and head into the room inside.

Inside are two ghasts in the form of Elizabeth and Gustav Durst, who become enraged at the sight of [[Suutha]] and [[Jeff]]. The party manage to fight them off, and soon after they defeat them, a blinding white light stuns and causes the party to blackout. Shortly before blacking out, [[Malachi]] and [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] catch a glimpse of a bat flying through a crack in the wall and breifly take the form of a man before they too pass out.

###  Borovia 
Upon waking, the party find themselves outside the Durst house, which once pristine, is now incredibly aged and worn much like the surrounding properties. The fog has also lifted slightly, allowing them to see other houses around them, and a sign signifying that they are indeed in [[Borovia]].

They also notice that they have each been bestowed a unique item:
* [[Jeff]]: A wolf’s head wrought in silver that is also a whistle
* [[Malachi]]: A walking cane with an iron ferrule that strikes sparks on stone
* [[Suutha]]: Pallid leather gloves crafted with ivory fingernails
* [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]]: A lantern with a black candle that never runs out and that burns with green flame

In addition to these items is a [[Thank you letter from Strahd | letter from Strahd]], thanking the party for ridding him of the Durst family. It also contains a message for 
[[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena Kolyana of Barovia]], warning her she has 3 days...

The party level up to level 3.

The party hear a faint crying from deeper in the town, so decide to explore to try and find [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]]. The first door which [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] confidently knocks on, swings open and reveals 5 zombies approaching. He quickly shuts the door again and they move on. In the second property, a timid man comes to the door, and when [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] tells him they are friends of [[Strahd]], the door is slammed in their face. [[Jeff]] manages to convince him to open the door and speak with them. He tells them that this is a dangerous place, and that [[Strahd]] had caused all this decay. The party find out that the place they are likely to find people nearby is the [[Blood of the Vine Tavern]]. He also tells them [[Bildraths Mercentile]] is where he gets his food from.

They head to the tavern.

###  Blood of the Vine tavern 
Inside the tavern, they see a group of 3 [[The Vistani]] gathered around a table, but they dont seem particularly interested in conversing with the party. When they ask if they know where [[Ireena Kolyana]] might be found, they point to a man sat on his own, [[Ismark the Lesser]] who they say is her brother. The party head over to talk to him and tell him they are looking for his sister to help her. He buys them all a drink and gives them information about her current situation.

She is currently staying at the [[Burgomaster Mansion]] in [[Borovia]] with their father, who is the Burgomaster. [[Strahd]] is apparently obsessed with [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]], having bitten her twice, so [[Ismark the Lesser | Ismark]] asks for their assistance in getting her away from [[Borovia]] and to [[Vallaki]].

[[Ismark the Lesser | Ismark]] tells the party about [[Strahd]] and his castle which is situated on top of a 1000ft cliff, overlooking [[Borovia]]. [[Strahd]] is a vampire, who cursed the land to achieve immortality.

The party discuss and agree to go and find [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]], so they can either deliver [[Strahd | Strahd's]] threat/letter, or they can help her escape [[Borovia]].

Before leaving, the party head back to the group of [[The Vistani]] and [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] casts 'Suggestion' on the nearest to attempt to get him to hand over a silver coated dagger that [[Malachi]] had noticed. It fails, and the group become quite aggressive, so [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] leaves with [[Malachi]]. [[Suutha]] heads to the toilet, and on leaving, attempts to sneak up the the group by crawling on the floor. This obviously doesn't work, and they notice the 6ft10 half-orc in the bright tavern and shoot her puzzled looks. Meanwhile, [[Jeff]] attempts to use this distraction to steal the dagger from under their noses, but is noticed. They are both chased out of the bar.

Outside the bar, the party reconvene with [[Ismark the Lesser | Ismark]] and agree to travel with him to the [[Burgomaster Mansion]] to find [[Ireena Kolyana]].

As the party are getting ready to leave, they make note of a couple of places they may wish to investigate in [[Borovia]]; in particular, [[Bildraths Mercentile]], and a smaller property further along, from which the sound of crying is still emanating....
