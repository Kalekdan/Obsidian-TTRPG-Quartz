###  Convincing Ms Belasco 
Having fought the demon in the orphanage, Suutha takes a lock of Felix's hair. The party head downstairs to see Ms Belasco. They convince her that Felix died at the hand of the demon during the fight. Erlanor checks on the children who they'd left, who seem ok.

Millivoj has woken and is walking around aimlessly. They are speaking to him when Lucian arrives, so they go with Lucian to bury Felix. They dig the grave and Lucian hesitates when he looks down and sees the head caved in. Chunk is able to convince him that it's all ok. They continue the ceremony, and Suutha places the hair locket she took on her necklace which has several other body parts on it.

As they wrap up, there is a crack of lightning to the east, and they notice the silhouette of a man stood on the orphanage with red eyes wearing a cape.

The party agree to help Lucian in restoring the bones of St Andral to the church, which apparently offered protection to the area. They wish to restore them to protect the kids they left at the orphanage. They find out the bones had been kept under the floorboards at the back of the church, but these had been pried up and stolen. They speak to milivoj, the only other person who knew about the bones, and he admits he told Henrik Van Der Voort, the coffin maker about their existence. Erlanor also takes this time to take a look at the locket from Felix, and realises it isn't magic.

###  The Armoury 
They head across town to the coffin maker in the SE, near the junkyard. They plan on stopping off at the armoury first, to see if they can pick up any bits. As they're walking, they see an old poster stapled to the wall for a 'Wolfs Head Jamboree'. They do not take much notice and head on to the armoury.

Once there, they begin bargaining to get some stuff made. Suutha hands him the broken chainmail she has, but he doesn't have the skill to fix it up. He suggests the metalcasters might be better, located on the island in the middle of the lake. They had once tried to overthrow the town, as they were done with the barons 'all will be well' sentiment. They can bend metal at will. There used to be 50+ of them, now only ~5.

Suutha gives him a silvered swoerd, Erlanor gives 10g, and Grogg another 10G, for him to make silver nails to put through Grogg's rolling pin. It will take 1 or 2 days to complete.

After this they stop off at the tavern, and ask more about the ravens. He is hesitent to tell much after their previous altercations. He tells them if they can recover his shipment for them, he may tell them more. The vineyard is a few miles W.

###  The Coffin Maker 
On the way to the coffin maker, they encounter a deformed purple man leading guards around. He points to one of the signs hanging, and they rip it down and put another one up in its place.

Once they get to the coffin makers, Henrik Van Der Voort tells them the bones are upstairs. He was told by a young mand to take the bones (not Milivoj). He won't go with the party to get them as upstairs is now a monsters lair.

Chunk goes invisible and sneaks upstairs to collect the bones. The rest of the party explore downstairs, in the room full of coffins.  Upstairs, Chunk sees several crates, which likely have monsters in. One doesn't have markings on it, so he tries to look inside that one. Inside is a 'Doru-like' creature (vampire thrall). Grogg rushes upstairs to see if he can help, and the others go with him.

A fight ensues whilst Chunk manages to get all the bones and climb out the window. The others are cornered, and Erlanor and Suutha are knocked unconscious. Whilst trying to escape, Suutha is killed. They manage to block the door behind them. Whilst speaking to Henrik Van Der Voort, he tells them that Vassili Von Holtz told him to take the bones (a young man with dark hair).

Chunk slaps Henrik Van Der Voort, angry at Suutha's death. Grogg swings for him as well and kills him.
