Suutha is a half-orc barbarian, played by [[James N]].

She is 6 foot 10, but despite her size has a tendency to try to be light fingered.
Physically, she is covered in furs across her entire body from neck to toe, but at the back of her neck you can see that her hair continues down her spine in a sort of mane. Not that you'd be able to tell but it also spreads across her shoulders.

She is most commonly seen with her maul in hand, but she also carries 2 hand-axes by her side, but would be more than capable of wrestling most foes with her bare hands.

She is quick to anger, and showed little sympathy for the priest, nor empathy for how it might affect [[Ismark the Lesser | Ismark]] and [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]] when she let slip what happened. This being said she also has a caring side, and helped carry out one of the two children at significant personal risk [[The Curse of Strahd - Session 5]].

Thus far she has been overwhelmingly disappointed by the lack of Ale in their travels.
