Rowan Thorne is a half-elf Druid/Warlock played by [[Joe]].

##  Background 
Rowan Thorne was a young druid when he joined the army that was sent to destroy Strahd von Zarovich. He was a devout follower of the Fane of the Forest, and believed deeply in the righteousness of the mission. The Fane herself led the charge, her power imbuing the soldiers with a fierce determination to succeed.

But as they journeyed deeper into Barovia, things began to go wrong. Strahd's minions seemed to anticipate their every move, and soon the army was caught in a deadly trap. In the chaos that followed, many of Rowan's fellow soldiers were killed, and he himself was badly wounded.

As he lay on the battlefield, certain that he was about to die, Rowan felt the Fane's power flowing into him. She had one last gift to bestow upon him: slowed aging. With this power, Rowan would age at a much slower rate than normal, allowing him to live for centuries, if not longer, and continue the fight against Strahd until he was finally defeated.

The Fane also imparted a final request to Rowan: that he avenge her death by defeating Strahd once and for all. She charged him with the task of finding allies, mastering his warlock powers, and using them to overcome the dark lord and his minions. Rowan accepted this charge with a solemn vow, and has spent the centuries since then preparing himself for the ultimate showdown.

Now, he has returned to Barovia, where he hopes to gather allies and unravel the mysteries of the land. He knows that the fight ahead will be long and difficult, but he is determined to see it through to the end. With the Fane's power at his fingertips, and his own formidable skills as a druid and warlock, he may just have a chance to succeed where so many others have failed.
