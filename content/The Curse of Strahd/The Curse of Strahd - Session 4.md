###  Preparing to Leave 
After leaving the church where the party found [[Donovich]], they decide to head to the [[Blood of the Vine Tavern]] again with [[Ismark the Lesser | Ismark]] and [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]] to drink to their fathers life. On their way, they notice a hunched figure in rags, pushing a cart, seemingly filled with pastries, around the streets of [[Borovia]], who seems to hurry on when she notices them.

They head into the tavern, and buy a round of drinks. They notice a new group of 3 [[The Vistani]] women (Alenka, Mirabel, and Sorvia), and decide to approach them to ask for directions for where they are heading next. The party decide they will go with [[Ismark the Lesser | Ismark]] and [[Ireena Kolyana | Ireena]] towards the [[Abbey of St Markovia]] in [[Krezk]], and stop off at the [[Tser Pool Encampment]] to see [[Madame Ava]], since that is in the same direction.

###  Mad Mary 
Before leaving Borovia, they decide they will investigate the house that has been the source of the crying for the past few days. They find the doors and windows boarded up like the [[Burgomaster Mansion]], but there is no evidence of tracks etc signifying any kind of attack. [[Jeff]] manages to pick the lock and they head in and find a largely empty house. Upstairs, where the noise is coming from, the party encounter [[Mad Mary]], clutching a doll and sobbing. After attempting to console her, she tells them that she lost her daughter [[Gertruda]] to [[Strahd]]. On the doll, their is a tag saying 'is no fun, is no Blinksy', referencing the toymaker 'Blinksy' from [[Vallaki]]. They also find that she is very keen to have one of the pastries that the old lady was selling so they agree to get her one.

[[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] sends up [[Kio]] to find the old woman, and they spot her a few streets away, and [[Suutha]] runs after her. She is going door to door selling pastries, and offers to sell some to the party for 1 gold each. They buy a few, and [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] and [[Suutha]] eat theirs and find them incredible. [[Malachi]] also eats one, and finds themselves in a blissful trance, unable to move.

They head back to [[Mad Mary]]'s house, and take her a pastry. Upon seeing [[Malachi]] however, she does not want one, but [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] gaslights her into thinking she's already taken it and owes them money. Feeling bad, he leaves the pastry for her regardless.

###  Journey to the Tser Pool Encampment 
To prepare to leave, and give [[Malachi]] a chance to recover, they head back to the [[Burgomaster Mansion]] to spend the night and collect silvered weaponry. [[Malachi]] takes a silvered hand-axe, and [[Erlanor Valhorn | Erlanor]] takes a silvered dagger (for [[Chunk]]).

The party all level up to level 4.

They head out of Borovia to the west, where the road bends SW and the trees close in. They see an arched stone bridge in the distance, where there are 2 direwolves and 2 wolves drinking from the river. They managed to defeat them in combat. As they continue, a thick fog forms, and the track narrows with the forest closing in on either side. They continue for ~1 mile, and come to a crossroads where they see a very old looking gallows next to a decrepit graveyard. The gallows are initially empty, but they all suddenly notice a body hanging from it, which [[Suutha]] see's as herself. She cuts down the body, but it soon disappears again. The sign at the crossroads details [[Borovia]] to the east (where they came from), [[Tser Pool Encampment | Tser Pool]] to the northwest, and [[Ravenloft]] and [[Vallaki]] to the south west, up the hill.

###  Tser Pool Encampment 

They continue with their initial plan to head to the [[Tser Pool Encampment]], and follow the northwest path as it slopes downwards into the trees. They continue to walk until the road dissapears and is replaced by muddy wagon tracks. They see a clearing next to a river that widens into a lake, with several large colourful tents, and a group of [[The Vistani]] around a fire singing and drinking. They also have several horses.

The party are invited to join the [[The Vistani]] for a drink, and one of them tells them a story around the fire. He tells of a wizard who came to the land many years ago, and tried to start a revolution against a vampire (presumably [[Strahd]]. There was a huge battle of spells, and the wizard's body was flung off the cliff and washed away in the river. The [[The Vistani]] are grateful for [[Strahd]] as he treats them well since they helped him before he became a vampire.

###  Madame Ava 
They ask about [[Madame Ava]], and are instructed to go to a large tent at the back, lit from within. Inside, they see an old crone, looking into a crystal ball. The party discuss with her, and ask about [[Strahd]]. She says that he needs freeing from his curse - he struck a pact with evil and murdered his brother for eternal life. According to her, "[[Strahd]] has always been [[Strahd]], not the man I recognise from his youth", and she promises to read the groups fortunes:
* I see a lonely mill on a precipice. The treasure lies within
* You will find what you seek in the castle, amid the ruins of a place of supplication.
    * I see a symbol in this castle
* The weapon lies in a dragon’s house, in hands once clean and now corrupted.
* Your greatest ally will be a wizard, his mind is broken but his spells are strong
* Find the leader of the feathered ones who live among the vines. Though old, he has one more fight left in him.
* Find a girl driven to insanity, locked in the heart of her dead father’s house. Curing her madness is key to your success.
* I see true evil lurking, look to the mothers tomb.

She is unable to give more details than the contents of the fortunes. They leave and try and bargain for a horse, but to no avail. They return to the crossroads, and intend to head to [[Vallaki]]. Just beyond the crossroads, they notice a humanoid figure, which turns out to be a [[Revenant]]. They manage to defeat it, and retrieve a broken chainmail, which [[Suutha]] takes.
