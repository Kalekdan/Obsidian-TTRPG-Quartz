**Consumable - single use (3 seeds)**

As an action, plant it in soil or any natural surface. Within **10 ft**, all **plants grow rapidly** (covers an area with 5 ft difficult terrain for 1 minute). Anyone in the area when planted (excluding the planter) must make a DC 15 saving throw or be restrained.

They can make a DC 10 saving throw on the start of their turn to break free.
