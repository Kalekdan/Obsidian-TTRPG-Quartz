_(Base: Necklace of Adaptation)_
- **Appearance:** A silver locket containing a drop of seawater that never evaporates. The inside glows faintly under moonlight.
- **Lore:** Once worn by a sailor’s wife who prayed nightly for his return. It is blessed by a spirit of the drowned.
- **Feature:** Grants the ability to breathe underwater and advantage on saves against necrotic effects from undead.
- **Discovery:** Found on a beach/coast near [[Virellis]]
