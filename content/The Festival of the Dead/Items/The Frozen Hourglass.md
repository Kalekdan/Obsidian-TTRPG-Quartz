_(Base: uncommon wondrous item, requires attunement)_
- **Appearance:** The symbol of the Shrine made manifest — a literal broken hourglass. Sand leaks endlessly but never runs out.
- **Lore:** Said to have belonged to the founder of the Shrine. The sand is said to be drawn from both sides of the veil.
- **Feature:**
    - Once per long rest, you can reverse a single death saving throw failure.
    - Each use risks a whisper from beyond (50% chance the dead “notice” you).
- **Discovery:** Hidden in the [[Elaris the Dying Monk|Elaris']] room in [[House of Final Rest]]; the monks fear it’s blasphemous to use.
