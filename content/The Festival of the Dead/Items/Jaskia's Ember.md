_(Base: Weapon, +1 longsword)_
- **Appearance:** The blade is blackened steel, faintly warm to the touch. When it strikes undead, faint orange runes pulse along the fuller like smouldering coals.
- **Lore:** Forged by Jaskia Boneheart herself before her selection as Hero. Meant to “bear the warmth of the living” into the dark.
- **Feature:** Once per day, you can ignite the blade for 1 minute — it sheds bright light and deals an extra 1d4 fire damage to undead.
- **Discovery:** Found on her person, in the [[Shrine of the Unquiet Souls]] crypt
