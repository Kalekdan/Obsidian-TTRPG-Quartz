**Species:** Rock Gnome (believes she’s a Halfling)  
**Class:** Druid (Order of Obad-Hai)  
**Player:** [[Alicia]]

### Core Concept
A devout yet unorthodox druid who thinks she’s a large halfling and not, in fact, a gnome. Cricket is a curious mix of pious discipline and chaotic curiosity — callous toward life and death, but deeply dedicated to the natural order. She wants to do good, but mostly in the way _she_ defines it: through adherence to the strict laws of Obad-Hai, rather than compassion or sentiment.

### Primary Motivation
To prove her worth to the Order of Obad-Hai and validate her calling as a druid. She dreams of someday visiting Obad-Hai’s Great Library and being remembered as a scholar of the natural world — not just a failed farmhand with fluffy ears.

### Immediate Goal (in Virellis)
To complete her assigned mission from the Order swiftly and effectively — ideally without being stung, distracted, or emotionally compromised. She’s determined to uphold the will of Obad-Hai and show her superiors that her discipline and devotion outweigh her past mistakes.

### Key Backstory
Cricket (born **Clementine Fiddlesticks**) was the daughter of two gnomish inventors, **Bobbins and Jingle Fiddlesticks**, engineers from a goblin metropolis. Their final invention — a supposedly bear-repelling spray — failed catastrophically. The bears won.

Cricket survived the attack and was later found by **Judith and Jasper Abernathy**, two kindly halflings who adopted her as their own. Raised in **Harefoot Hollow**, Cricket grew up believing she was simply a tall, youthful halfling. While she helped on the family farm, her curiosity for the wilderness often led her astray — to the frustration of her community.

When a group of travelling druids from the **Order of Obad-Hai** passed through, Cricket saw her escape from a stifling village life. She joined eagerly, seeking purpose and belonging. The Order’s austere lifestyle proved far harsher than she imagined, and her attempts at _Wild Shape_ were disastrous — one accident left her with permanent fluffy ears, a reminder of her imperfect mastery.

Despite her struggles, Cricket’s long lifespan and relentless persistence have earned her a deep (if slightly warped) understanding of nature’s balance. She believes wholeheartedly in the natural cycle — life, death, decay, renewal — and sees emotion as an obstacle to that truth.

### Personality & Beliefs
- **Traits:** Callous, Goal-Oriented, Ruthless, Curious, Impatient
- **Ideals:** Devotion to Obad-Hai and the Natural Order; Escape from the dull life of Harefoot Hollow
- **Bonds:** Dolly the Duck (her companion), Obad-Hai, and her halfling parents Judith and Jasper
- **Flaws:** Emotionally detached regarding death; unflinchingly devout; secretly terrified of bees
### Allies & Connections
**Obad-Hai** – Cricket’s god and guiding principle. She dreams of visiting His Library and treats His teachings as absolute law — morality is secondary to balance.

**Judith & Jasper Abernathy** – Her adoptive halfling parents, now elderly. They adore her and share embellished tales of her “heroic adventures” with their neighbors. Cricket finds them embarrassing but loves them deeply.

**Bobbins & Jingle Fiddlesticks** – Her biological gnome parents, long deceased. She is unaware of her true lineage or their fate, believing herself to be a halfling.

### Quirks & Details
- Thinks she’s a halfling. No one has told her otherwise.
- Afraid of bees.
- Fond of ducks — especially her companion, Dolly.
- Loves cider but is an absolute lightweight.
- Keeps a journal of every plant, animal, and fungus she meets — rated by usefulness and taste.
