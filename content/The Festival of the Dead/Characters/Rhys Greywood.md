Rhys is a human fighter/cleric played by [[James N|James]]
![[Pasted image 20250707101036.png| 300]]

- **Name:** Rhys Greywood
- **Class:** Cleric of Helm (The Watcher)
- **Core Concept:** A guilt-ridden ex-soldier (late 30s/early 40s) seeking atonement for past atrocities, driven by his oath to protect the innocent and find a "just path."
- **Primary Motivation:** To atone for his role in "The Silent Harvest," a massacre of innocent villagers under a corrupt lord. He seeks to reconcile his gentle, penitent side with his cold, efficient killer side.
- **Immediate Goal (in Virellis):** To attend the Festival of the Dead as a personal pilgrimage, specifically to bear witness to the spirits of the villagers he helped kill.
- **Key Backstory (The Silent Harvest):** As a soldier, Rhys was ordered by [[Lord Mordain Ironwright]] to secure "strategic resources" from Greenhollow village, believing he was fighting "bandits." In truth, they were innocent villagers protecting a sacred grove. Rhys participated, found a "Family Knot" talisman on a slain villager, and later overheard Mordain mocking the dead, revealing the lie. This led him to desert.
- **Secrets/Vulnerabilities:** He is a deserter, a killer of innocents (which he keeps secret), and deeply fears his "cold side" taking over. His former leader, [[Annelise Ironwright]] (now suffering from dementia), is a major emotional weak point.
- **Oath of Helm:** His oath emphasizes shielding the powerless, seeking truth, prioritizing justice over efficiency, and acting as an agent of his own will rather than a weapon for others.
### Key NPCs & Factions
- **Lord Mordain Ironwright** (Primary Antagonist): Rhys’s former commander. A vain, cruel, and deeply insecure tyrant who rules the lands Rhys came from. Mordain hides his weakness behind a mask of brutality and delights in mocking the loyal and the earnest. He would view Rhys as a traitorous dog and would likely want him dead or captured to make an example of him.
- **Annelise Ironwright** (The Faded Matriarch): Mordain's mother and Rhys’s former, respected leader. A once-brilliant, pragmatic ruler, she now suffers from dementia. She willingly abdicated and lives on a quiet farm near the city, cared for by a loyal friend. She is a source of profound grief for Rhys and represents the "just path" that has been lost. Her condition is genuine, but Mordain could use her as leverage against Rhys.
- **Elara** (The Caretaker): Annelise’s fiercely loyal former handmaiden and current caretaker. She is pragmatic and wary of outsiders but respects Rhys for his continued devotion to her mistress. She could be a future ally or a source of information about the Ironwrights' territory.
The Survivors of Greenhollow (Potential Hook): There may be survivors or refugees from the village Rhys helped destroy. They would recognize the "Family Knot" talisman he carries. Their appearance would be a source of immense conflict and a powerful roleplaying opportunity.**
