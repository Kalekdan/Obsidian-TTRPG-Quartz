**Class:** Sorcerer

### Core Concept
A sea witch caught between worlds — living and dead, shore and sea. Once a devoted wife, now a custodian of the drowned, Nyxsandra guides lost souls beneath the tide even as she searches for one she cannot find. Her power is inseparable from her grief, her magic flowing with the rhythm of waves and the silence that follows them.

### Primary Motivation
To find her husband’s lost soul, trapped between life and death, and uncover the truth of the power that binds him there. Though she has long accepted her own loneliness, this sliver of hope compels her to leave her coastal exile for the first time in years.

### Immediate Goal (in Virellis)
To seek the source of her husband’s curse — following whispers of spirits, omens, and old sailors’ tales that may point toward his fate. Her purpose draws her inland, away from the comfort of the sea, in search of forbidden knowledge and allies willing to brave the unseen.

### Key Backstory
Once, Nyxsandra Thalasson lived a simple, joyful life with her husband by the sea. But when his ship was lost to a storm, she stood on the shore for days, praying for him to return. On the seventh night, the tide came in black and cold — and with it came voices.

The grief that followed awakened her sorcery. She became the **Keeper of the Low Tide**, able to commune with the drowned and guide them toward rest. But her gift came with isolation: whispers called her a witch, a consort of devils, a herald of storms.

She withdrew to a weather-beaten cottage overlooking the coast, its walls lined with salt-stained charms and driftwood relics. For years, she served the lost — lighting candles for the dead, speaking to spirits trapped in the undertow — yet her husband’s voice never came.

That changed when a sign reached her shore:  
a sailor’s journal, a priest’s warning, or a ghost’s confession — each bearing the same truth. Her husband’s soul had not passed on. It lingered, bound by something ancient and cruel.

Now, with sorrow turned to resolve, Nyxsandra steps beyond the breakers and into the mortal world once more, seeking the means to set him free.

### Personality & Beliefs
- **Traits:** Poised, Melancholic, Compassionate, Secretive, Resolute
- **Ideals:** Redemption, Love enduring beyond death, Balance between sea and soul
- **Bonds:** Her lost husband; the spirits of the drowned who still whisper to her
- **Flaws:** Haunted by grief; drawn to sorrow; fears that saving him will mean losing herself
### Allies & Connections
**The Drowned Souls** – The countless spirits who linger at the shore. Some guide her; others tempt her toward despair.
**Her Husband** – Once a sailor, now a lost soul caught between realms. His absence defines her purpose.
**The Coastal Folk** – Fear and respect her in equal measure.

### Quirks & Details
- Keeps seashells filled with the ashes of drowned sailors.
- Smells faintly of brine and candle wax.
- Refuses to turn her back on the ocean, even when far inland.
- Hears whispers on still water that others cannot.
- Carries a piece of driftwood shaped like a heart — all that remains of her husband’s ship.
