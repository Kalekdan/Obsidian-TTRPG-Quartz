**Class:** Artificer  
**Player:** [[Ali]]
### Core Concept
A smallfolk “scientist” who has spent his life convinced that he survives through intellect and innovation, when in truth his endurance comes from sheer constitution and stubborn luck. Stuart’s mind may not be as sharp as he believes, but his spirit is indomitable—and his experiments, though often catastrophic, somehow never quite kill him.

### Primary Motivation
To continue his family’s “scientific legacy” and prove his theories right—whatever they happen to be this week. Whether testing alchemical mixtures, launching fireworks, or declaring new natural laws, Stuart is always in pursuit of the next great breakthrough (and the next explosion).

### Immediate Goal (in Virellis)
To represent his family’s work and uphold their reputation within the three experimental societies he belongs to. He seeks to gain recognition as a legitimate scientist among smallfolk and to show that his parents’ efforts were not in vain.

### Key Backstory
Stuart was born into a family of experimental pioneers.
- **His father**, leader of the _Aeronautical Society of Smallfolk_, dedicated his life to proving that smallfolk deserve to fly. He died in a tragic (and somewhat predictable) firework accident.
- **His mother**, head of the _Society for Planetary Reclamation_, believed the earth had been corrupted by its curves and sought to restore it to its perfect flat state. She died while attempting to level the landscape with explosives.

Now orphaned but driven by inherited purpose, Stuart continues both their causes—and for good measure, he’s also joined a third group, _The Circle of Flat Earthers_, mostly because he found the name amusing.

### Skills & Proficiencies
- **Acrobatics** – To dodge flying debris and get out of danger’s way.
- **Deception** – To persuade others of the validity of his “scientific” conclusions.
- **Medicine** – To treat the frequent burns, bruises, and chemical mishaps of experimentation.
- **Perception** – To spot hazards before they explode.
- **Tools:** Smith’s Tools (for crafting armor), Tinker’s Tools, and Alchemist’s Supplies (for conducting experiments).
### Factions & Affiliations
**The Aeronautical Society of Smallfolk** – Founded by his father. Advocates for smallfolk as legitimate aviators and experimenters.

**The Society for Planetary Reclamation** – Founded by his mother. Dedicated to returning the world to its “perfectly flat” state.

**The Circle of Flat Earthers** – A group Stuart joined for the name alone.
