Traditionally, [[Anhural]] is not killed, he retreats/is sent back to his domain to recover and returns next year.
There is a portal at the back of the cave, to the realm of death where Anhural is from.

**at 50% HP [[Anhural]] begins to retreat, but gains +1 dmg on all attacks**

**+2 legendary resistances**
**+2 legendary actions:**
- **Shadow Slip (1 Action)**  
	- Anhural moves up to half his speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
- **Grasping Gloom (1 Action)**  
	- One creature within 10 ft must succeed on a STR save or be pulled 5 ft.
- **Veil Claw (2 Actions)**  
	- Make one claw attack.
**lair actions (initiative 20)**
- **Deepening Shadows**  
    Dim light becomes darkness in a 15 ft area until next round.
- **Whispering Regret**  
    One creature makes a WIS save or has disadvantage on its next attack.
- **Shifting Stone**  
    A 10 ft area becomes difficult terrain.
