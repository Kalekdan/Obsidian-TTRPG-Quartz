**At the gate or near the shrine**  
_"Hold there. You’re new to Virellis, I take it? Don’t mind the crowds — everyone wants their prayers heard before the Festival. Keep your wits about you, and you’ll manage fine."_

**Sizing up the party**  
_"You look like folk who’ve seen more than a market stall or two. Virellis welcomes all in the days before the dead walk — but don’t mistake welcome for safety."_

**Hinting at the strain without giving the quest outright**  
_"The Festival draws pilgrims by the hundreds, and the guard by the dozens. We’re stretched thin — too thin. If you see a watchman looking sharp with their orders, don’t take it personal. We’ve no room for mistakes this week."_

**If they ask directly about unrest or danger**  
_"A town this busy always has its troubles. Petty theft, frayed tempers, travelers who don’t know their place. Nothing that should concern you… unless you go looking for it."_

**If he wants to be welcoming but firm**  
_"Enjoy the Festival. Spend coin, light lanterns, speak your prayers. But remember — when the veil thins, this is not a place for fools. Mind yourselves, and you’ll live to see dawn after."_
