The Hero is decided each year by the monks in the [[Shrine of the Unquiet Souls]]. They are expected to challenge and best the demon at the end of [[the festival]] to send the undead back.

This year, the hero is [[Jaskia Boneheart]], a blacksmith from the town.

Previous year's heroes include:
- [[Finley Jugg]] - The barman from [[Last Orders]] (has [[Tankard of Booming Toast]])
- [[Parson Smelt]] - A local farmer (has [[Seed of Entanglement]])
most previous years heroes are deceased, either from natural causes or from adventuring (it's common for ex-heroes to take up adventuring after being nominated)
