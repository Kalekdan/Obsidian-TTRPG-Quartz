## 🎭 **Campaign Primer: The Festival of the Dead**

**System:** Dungeons & Dragons 5e  
**DM:** Joe  
**Starting Level:** 3
**Tone:** Mystery, folklore, undead, moral ambiguity  
**Setting:** The coastal town of **Virellis**  
**Character Fit:** Outsiders with secrets, pilgrims of the dead, mercenaries seeking work, or skeptics pulled into something ancient - or anything else that strikes your fancy

---

### 🏙️ Welcome to Virellis

High on the cliffs above a stormy sea lies **Virellis**, a sacred coastal town surrounded by vast, ancient graveyards. It is quiet for most of the year—until the eve of the **Festival of the Dead**.

For one night each year, the veil between life and death **grows thin**, and the dead walk once more.

Pilgrims flock from every corner of the realm. Families gather to see lost loved ones. Lanterns are lit. Names are spoken. It is a time of **remembrance, reverence, and joy**.

But at the end of the night, **something dark stirs.**  
It always has.

---

### 🕯️ A Sacred Tradition

As the final bell tolls at midnight, a **demon emerges** from the veil. It calls to the dead, asking them to **stay.** If unopposed, it raises them as mindless servants, threatening the town and all who remain.

To stop it, the monks of the **Shrine of the Unquiet Souls** select a **Hero by prophecy**—a mortal champion who descends into the cave beneath the cliffs to **defeat the demon** and **seal the veil** once again.

This tradition has lasted **hundreds of years.**  
It has never failed.

---

### ✨ This Year’s Festival

This year, the Hero is **Jaskia Boneheart**, a blacksmith born and raised in Virellis. Strong, brave, and beloved by many, she has already begun her sacred preparations.

The town is bustling with excitement and pride. Banners fly. Lanterns are hung. Priests give blessings. Markets are thriving.

And you are arriving just in time.

---

### 🧭 Your Role

You’ve come to Virellis just days before the Festival begins. Your reasons may vary:

- Rumors of **bandits near the road**—and coin for those who clear them out.
    
- A chance to **speak with the dead**—to find closure, forgiveness, or forgotten knowledge.
    
- Curiosity about the **Festival’s power**—or fear of what it might bring.

- Or maybe you lived and grew up in Virellis all along?

You’re outsiders, walking into a town at the edge of the world, in the days before the dead rise. **Everything seems normal. For now.**

---

### 🧠 Character Prompts

- **Why are you coming to Virellis?** Coin? Closure? Pilgrimage? Escape?
    
- **Who have you lost?** Would you want to see them again?
    
- **How do you feel about the undead?** Sacred? Profane? Unnatural?
    
- **Would you obey prophecy—or defy it?**
    
- **Are you haunted by your past—or ready to haunt someone else’s?**
    

---

### ⚠️ What You (the Player) Should Know

This is a **medium form campaign** that blends mystery, the undead, small-town politics, and prophecy. As the story unfolds, you’ll uncover **secrets about the town, the Festival, and yourself.**

Something **will go wrong.**  
The question is: **what will you do when it does?**
